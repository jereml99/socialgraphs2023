'''Roshon Bernard Fegan''' (born October 6, 1991), known  [[Mononymous person|mononymously]] as '''Roshon''' (stylized as '''ROSHON''', formerly stylized as '''RO SHON'''), is an American actor, rapper, and dancer. He is best known for his role as [[List of Shake It Up characters#Ty Blue|Ty Blue]] on the [[Disney Channel]] original series ''[[Shake It Up (American TV series)|Shake It Up]]'' and for his role as Sander Loyer in the Disney Channel movie franchise ''[[Camp Rock]]'' with the [[Jonas Brothers]] and [[Demi Lovato]]. He writes and produces his own music as well as working with the president of Lava/Universal Republic. In 2012, Roshon appeared on ''[[Dancing with the Stars]]''.

==Early life==
Roshon Bernard Fegan was born on October 6, 1991, in [[Los Angeles]], [[California]].<ref name="Medianet: Disney Channel">{{cite web|url=http://www.disneychannelmedianet.com/web/showpage/showpage.aspx?program_id=3129298&type=fegan|title=Roshon Fegan is "Ty Blue"|access-date=2010-11-12|work=Medianet: Disney Channel|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120614180853/http://www.disneychannelmedianet.com/web/showpage/showpage.aspx?program_id=3129298&type=fegan|archive-date=2012-06-14}}</ref><ref name="Philippine Daily Inquirer">{{cite news|url=http://showbizandstyle.inquirer.net/entertainment/entertainment/view/20100828-289286/Another-Fil-Am-cast-in-Camp-Rock-2|title=Filipino-American cast in 'Camp Rock 2'|access-date=2011-01-14|newspaper=Philippine Daily Inquirer|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100902050020/http://showbizandstyle.inquirer.net/entertainment/entertainment/view/20100828-289286/Another-Fil-Am-cast-in-Camp-Rock-2|archive-date=2010-09-02}}</ref><ref name="The Manilla Bulletin">{{cite web|url=http://www.mb.com.ph/articles/275612/two-filipinos-cast-camp-rock-2|title=Two Filipinos cast in 'Camp Rock 2'|access-date=2011-01-14|work= The Manilla Bulletin}}</ref><ref name="Amazon.com - Camp Rock">{{cite web|url=https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0017LFKMO|title='Camp Rock 1 and 2': Various Artists: Music|access-date=2011-01-14|work= Amazon.com - Camp Rock}}</ref> His father is [[African-American]] actor and producer [[Roy Fegan]], whose television credits include ''[[The Shield (TV series)|The Shield]]'', ''[[Married... with Children]]'', ''The Meteor Man'', and ''[[Will & Grace]]'', among others. His mother is [[Filipino people|Filipino]].<ref name="Medianet: Disney Channel" /><ref name="Philippine Daily Inquirer"/><ref name="The Manilla Bulletin"/><ref name="ABS-CBN News">{{cite web|url=http://www.abs-cbnnews.com/video/entertainment/09/07/10/jonas-brothers-amazed-pinay-camp-rock-2|title=Two Pinoy-Americans in "Camp Rock 2"|access-date=2011-01-14|work= ABS-CBN News}}</ref> Raised in [[Los Angeles]], Roshon Fegan had studied at the BK Acting Studio and the USC 32nd Street Performing Arts School as well as attending Tom Bradley Elementary then Hollywood High School, Before leaving to be [[home-schooled]] to accommodate his acting career.

==Career==
Fegan began his acting career at the age of 12 with a small role in the 2004 feature film ''[[Spider-Man 2]]'', followed by an appearance in 2006 on the TV series ''[[Monk (TV series)|Monk]]''.<ref name="Medianet: Disney Channel"/><ref name="Philippine Daily Inquirer"/> In 2008, he appeared in the comedy film ''[[Drillbit Taylor]]'' and the [[VC FilmFest|VC FilmFest Award]] winning film ''[[Baby (2008 film)|Baby]]''.<ref name="Medianet: Disney Channel"/> In June 2008, Fegan gained popular notoriety as Sander Loyer in the [[Disney Channel Original Movie|Disney Channel]] original movie ''[[Camp Rock]]'', which was the number one entertainment telecast on cable TV in 2008,<ref name="Medianet: Disney Channel"/><ref name="The New York Times">{{cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2008/08/21/arts/television/21disn.html?_r=1&partner=rssuserland&emc=rss&pagewanted=all|title=Youth TV Takes the Lead in Diversity|access-date=2011-01-14|work= The New York Times|first=Edward|last=Wyatt|date=2008-08-21}}</ref> with the Jonas Brothers. He also performed songs on the ''[[Camp Rock (soundtrack)|Camp Rock]]'' soundtrack  as well as the DVD bonus track ''Camp Rock''.<ref name="Amazon.com - Camp Rock"/> In 2010, Fegan reprised his role as Sander in ''[[Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam]]'', the sequel to ''Camp Rock''.<ref name="Medianet: Disney Channel"/><ref name="Philippine Daily Inquirer"/><ref name="The Manilla Bulletin"/><ref name="ABS-CBN News"/><ref name="Youtube.com - RadioDisney">{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8jORvGQt5i8 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110227014754/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8jORvGQt5i8 |archive-date=2011-02-27 |url-status=dead|title=Roshon Fegan – "Anything Is Possible" Music Video|access-date=2011-03-15|work= Youtube.com - RadioDisney}}</ref> Roshon also co-starred on the Disney Channel original series ''[[Shake It Up (U.S. TV series)|Shake It Up!]]'' as Ty Blue, Rocky's older brother.

Fegan was featured on the [[Dancing with the Stars (U.S. season 14)|14th season]] of the [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] show ''[[Dancing with the Stars (U.S. TV series)|Dancing with the Stars]]''.<ref>[http://abc.go.com/shows/dancing-with-the-stars/cast-announcement/ThemeGallery/928812 "Dancing with the Stars{{spaced ndash}}2012 Cast Announcement for Season 14"] abc.com. Retrieved 28 February 2012.</ref> [[Chelsie Hightower]] was announced as Roshon's ballroom dance partner. He stayed on for eight weeks and was placed in the top 6.

==Personal life==
Roshon is a songwriter, producer, actor, artist and freestyle dancer who learned his moves by watching [[Michael Jackson]] and other influential dancers. A drummer since the age of two, Roshon also plays the piano and guitar.<ref name="Medianet: Disney Channel"/>
He has released a number of singles on [[iTunes]] and has finished his first self-produced EP ''I AM RO SHON'' under his own label, "3inaRo Entertainment".<ref name="Medianet: Disney Channel"/><ref name="Philippine Daily Inquirer"/><ref name="iTunes - 3inaRo">{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/i-am-ro-shon-single/id502542896|title=3inaRo on iTunes|access-date=2011-01-14|work= iTunes - 3inaRo}}</ref><ref name="iTunes - ROSHON">{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/i-am-ro-shon-single/id502542896|title=ROSHON on iTunes|access-date=2011-01-14|work= iTunes - ROSHON}}</ref> The name ''3inaRo'' (pronounced "three-in-a-row") is a reference to being a triple-threat entertainer in his three passions: acting, freestyle dancing, and music.<ref name="Philippine Daily Inquirer"/> He currently lives in [[California]] with most of his family.
He was in a relationship with dancer Dytto.{{Citation needed|date=October 2019}}
