'''Benjamin Howard Laub''' (February 23, 1984), better known by his stage name '''Grieves''', is an American hip hop artist based in [[Seattle]], [[Washington (state)|Washington]].<ref name="hiphopdx2013"/> Born in [[Chicago]], [[Illinois]],<ref name="hiphopdx2010"/> he has lived in [[Colorado]], [[San Diego]], and [[New York City]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/interviews/id.1549/title.underground-report-sage-francis-grieves|title=Underground Report: Sage Francis & Grieves|publisher=HipHopDX|first=Andres|last=Vasquez|date=May 31, 2010}}</ref> He is signed to [[Rhymesayers Entertainment]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://consequenceofsound.net/2011/06/album-review-grieves-togetherapart/|title=Album Review: Grieves – Together/Apart|publisher=Consequence of Sound|first=Nick|last=Freed|date=June 27, 2011}}</ref>
