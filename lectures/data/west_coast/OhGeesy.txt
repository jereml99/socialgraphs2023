'''Alejandro Carranza''' (born November 27, 1993)<ref name="allmusic"/> known professionally as '''OhGeesy''', is an American rapper from [[Los Angeles]]. He is noted as a founding member of the hip hop group [[Shoreline Mafia]].<ref name="xxl">{{Cite web|title=THE BREAK PRESENTS: SHORELINE MAFIA|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/shoreline-mafia-interview-the-break/|website=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]|language=en}}</ref> In 2021, with the group's apparent disbandment, OhGeesy has shifted towards a solo career with the release of his debut solo album ''Geezyworld'' which peaked at number 102 on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]].<ref name="billboard">{{cite magazine | url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/billboard-200/2021-09-10/ | title=Billboard 200 | magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] }}</ref>

==Career==

===Shoreline Mafia===
OhGeesy started rapping alongside members of [[Shoreline Mafia]] in 2012. He met member Fenix Flexin while doing graffiti in [[Los Angeles]]. In 2016, Shoreline Mafia became a fully-formed rap group.<ref name="xxl"/>

===Solo career===
In February 2021, he appeared on American rapper [[Drakeo the Ruler]]'s single "For Real" and its subsequent music video alongside American rapper Ketchy the Great.<ref name="uproxx">{{Cite web|title=Drakeo The Ruler Proclaims His Authenticity With 'For Real' Featuring OhGeesy And Ketchy The Great|url=https://uproxx.com/music/drakeo-the-ruler-for-real-video-ohgeesy-ketchy-the-great/|website=[[Uproxx]]|date=11 February 2021 |language=en}}</ref> In May 2021, he released his single "Get Fly" with American rapper [[DaBaby]] alongside a music video directed by Austin Simkins.<ref name="hotnew2">{{Cite web|title=OhGeesy & DaBaby "Get Fly" On New Latin-Inspired Single|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/327041-ohgeesy-and-dababy-get-fly-on-new-latin-inspired-single-new-song|website=[[HotNewHipHop]]|date=28 May 2021 |language=en}}</ref> In July 2021, he released the music video for his track "Big Bad Wolf", a collaboration with American rapper [[YG (rapper)|YG]].<ref name="complex">{{Cite web|title=Watch OhGeesy' Video for New Track "Big Bad Wolf" f/ YG|url=https://www.complex.com/music/ohgeesy-yg-big-bad-wolf-video-geezyworld-album-announced|website=[[Complex Networks|Complex]]|language=en}}</ref> In August 2021, he released his debut solo album ''Geezyworld'' with appearances from [[A Boogie wit da Hoodie]], [[DaBaby]], [[YG (rapper)|YG]], [[Central Cee]], BlueBucksClan, and Moe Faygoo.<ref name="hotnew3">{{Cite web|title=OhGeesy Releases Debut Solo Project "GEEZYWORLD" Featuring YG, A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie, & More|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/350681-ohgeesy-releases-debut-solo-project-geezyworld-featuring-yg-a-boogie-wit-da-hoodie-and-more-new-mixtape|website=[[HotNewHipHop]]|date=27 August 2021 |language=en}}</ref>

==Personal life==
He became a father in March 2019.

In October 2021, during an interview with [[HipHopDX]], OhGeesy spoke about his struggle with drug addiction and how it almost caused him to retire from music.<ref name="hiphopdx">{{Cite web|title=OHGEESY ALMOST QUIT RAPPING AFTER AN ACID TRIP: 'I WENT FUCKING BANANAS'|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.65052/title.ohgeesy-almost-quit-rapping-acid-trip|website=[[HipHopDX]]|date=8 October 2021 |language=en}}</ref>

==Legal issues==
In August 2019, he was removed from [[Disneyland]] after making alleged gun threats.<ref name="complex2">{{Cite web|title=Shoreline Mafia's Ohgeesy Kicked Out of Disneyland Over Alleged Gun Threat|url=https://www.complex.com/music/2019/08/shoreline-mafia-ohgeesy-kicked-out-of-disneyland|website=[[HotNewHipHop]]|language=en}}</ref> In September 2022, he was arrested on charges of loaded gun and [[codeine]] possession.<ref name="thesource">{{Cite web|title=SHORELINE MAFIA'S OHGEESY BUSTED FOR LOADED GUN AND CODEINE POSSESSION|url=https://thesource.com/2022/09/08/shoreline-mafias-ohgeesy-busted-for-loaded-gun-and-codeine-possession/|website=[[The Source]]|date=8 September 2022 |language=en}}</ref>
