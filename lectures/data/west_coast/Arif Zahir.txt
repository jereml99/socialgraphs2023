'''Arif Zahir Lopes-Thrower''' (born April 15, 1994) is an American actor, musician, and internet personality. His YouTube channel, '''Azerrz''', has over 7 million subscribers<ref name=":0" /> and features voice impressions of a plethora of celebrities and cartoon characters. Since 2021, Zahir has voiced [[Cleveland Brown]] on the television series ''[[Family Guy]]'', succeeding [[Mike Henry (voice actor)|Mike Henry]].<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|last1=Schneider|first1=Michael|date=September 25, 2020|title='Family Guy' Casts YouTube Star Arif Zahir as the New Voice of Cleveland Brown|url=https://variety.com/2020/tv/news/family-guy-cleveland-arif-zahir-1234783294/|access-date=September 25, 2020|website=Variety|language=en}}</ref>

==Early life and education==
Zahir is of African-American and Cape Verdean descent and was born on April 15, 1994, in [[Berkeley, California]]. He was partially raised in [[New Bedford, Massachusetts|New Bedford]], [[Massachusetts]] where he attended Nativity Preparatory School and Keith Middle School before moving back to California in 2007.<ref>{{Cite web|date=October 28, 2020|first=Phil|last=Paleologos|title=New Bedford Native the New Voice of Cleveland Brown [Phil-osophy]|url=https://wbsm.com/new-bedford-native-the-new-voice-of-cleveland-brown-phil-osophy/|access-date=November 10, 2020|website=1420 WBSM|language=en}}</ref>

Zahir began doing voice impressions in middle school to impress his classmates. He originally made a YouTube channel in 2007 which focused on performing magic tricks.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Arif Lopes's YouTube Stats (Summary Profile) – Social Blade Stats|url=https://socialblade.com/youtube/user/magicmind0|access-date=December 16, 2020|website=socialblade.com}}</ref> After doing so and finding success he started attending [[Hussian School of Art|Hussian College's]] campus in Los Angeles. He later dropped out to spend more time focusing on his YouTube career.<ref name="variety.com">{{Cite web|last1=Schneider|first1=Michael|date=October 14, 2020|title=How 'Family Guy' Newcomer Arif Zahir Landed His Big Break as the New Cleveland Brown|url=https://variety.com/2020/tv/news/family-guy-cleveland-brown-arif-zahir-1234804840/|access-date=October 19, 2020|website=Variety|language=en}}</ref>

Zahir landed his first acting role playing a character named Arif in the short film ''No One But Lydia.'' Starting in 2018, he became a recurring cast member on ''[[How It Should Have Ended]]'' voicing [[Black Panther (character)|Black Panther / T'Challa]].<ref name="screenrant.com">{{Cite web|date=May 21, 2018|title=How Black Panther Should Have Ended|url=https://screenrant.com/black-panther-how-it-should-have-ended/|access-date=November 25, 2020|website=ScreenRant|language=en-US}}</ref> Through community challenges and his first viral hit with "Ted Plays Call of Duty" (which has over 20M+ views), Arif quickly grew his channel to over 6.5+ million subscribers and 650+ million views.<ref>{{Cite web|date=October 19, 2020|title=Spotlight on Arif Zahir: Unconventional Ways To Land An Acting Gig|url=https://forcreators.com/arif-zahir/|access-date=December 1, 2020|website=forCreators.com|language=en-US}}</ref>

He is also a rapper and singer and releases music under the moniker '''4rif'''.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web |title=4rif's YouTube Stats (Summary Profile) – Social Blade Stats |url=https://socialblade.com/youtube/channel/UCx4009c0ifgDZ7qoG01wLMQ |access-date=September 25, 2020 |website=socialblade.com}}</ref> He released his debut album ''At Last Sight'' on August 30, 2019.<ref name=":4" /> He followed it up with an [[Extended play|EP]] called ''Go Gadget/Blues Clues'' which was released on April 29, 2020, in collaboration with rapper Dlngqnt.<ref name=":10">{{Cite web|date=April 29, 2020|title=Dlnqnt and 4rif Drop "Go Gadget / Blues CLues" EP|url=https://www.showouthiphop.com/post/dlnqnt-and-4rif-drop-go-gadget--blues-clues-ep|access-date=December 1, 2020|website=Show Out Hip-Hop|language=en|archive-date=October 25, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211025230719/https://www.showouthiphop.com/post/dlnqnt-and-4rif-drop-go-gadget--blues-clues-ep|url-status=dead}}</ref>

On June 26, 2020, [[Mike Henry (voice actor)|Mike Henry]], who had voiced Cleveland Brown since 1999, announced he was stepping down and believed a person of color should voice the role.<ref>{{Cite web|author=Leah Asmelash|title=Mike Henry will no longer voice Cleveland on 'Family Guy'|url=https://www.cnn.com/2020/06/27/entertainment/mike-henry-cleveland-family-guy-trnd/index.html|access-date=September 26, 2020|website=CNN|date=June 27, 2020 }}</ref> On September 25, 2020, it was announced that Zahir would be voicing Cleveland starting in Season 20 of ''[[Family Guy]]''.<ref name=":2" />
