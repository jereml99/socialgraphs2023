'''Steven Thronson''' (October 21, 1975&nbsp;‚Äì October 13, 2020<ref name="auto">{{Cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/CGVogUplaXR/ |archive-url=https://ghostarchive.org/iarchive/instagram/saintdog.rip/2420018517837718993 |archive-date=2021-12-25 |url-access=registration|title=Saint Dog on Instagram: "Rest In Peace, Rest In Power to a true underground legend. üôèüèº Saint Dog aka Saint Vicious passed away yesterday in Southern California.‚Ä¶"|website=Instagram.com|accessdate=14 October 2020}}{{cbignore}}</ref>), better known by his stage name '''Saint Dog''', was an American rapper from [[Southern California]].

==Career==
He was an original founding member of [[Kottonmouth Kings]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.newstimes.com/news/article/Saint-Dog-takes-listeners-into-ghetto-61580.php|title=Saint Dog takes listeners into ghetto|date=2004-07-21|website=Newstimes.com|access-date=2020-03-26}}</ref> along with [[D-Loc]] and [[Johnny Richter]]. He contributed to their first three releases ''[[Stoners Reeking Havoc]]'', ''[[Royal Highness (album)|Royal Highness]]'', and ''[[Hidden Stash]]'', as well as the latest Kottonmouth album ''Kingdom Come''. On August 2, 2019, Saint Dog released his third and final solo album ''Bozo'' via [[Suburban Noize Records]] & Force 5 Records. On October 13, 2020, it was confirmed by his management that Saint Dog had died on that day, eight days short from his 45th birthday.
