'''Terrence Louis Henderson Jr.''',<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/Bxy5ltBHYQq/ |archive-url=https://ghostarchive.org/iarchive/instagram/iamstillpunch/2049954060015862826 |archive-date=2021-12-25 |url-access=registration|title=Punch on Instagram: "Michael Anthony Jones and Terrence Louis Henderson Jr. Or Mike Mike and Punchino. Circa 1994"|website=Instagram|access-date=May 25, 2019}}{{cbignore}}</ref> better known by his [[stage name]] '''Punch''', is an American rapper, songwriter, record producer and record executive from [[Carson, California]]. He is best known for being the [[President (corporate title)|president]] of independent record label [[Top Dawg Entertainment]] (TDE).<ref name="Tardio 2016">{{cite magazine | last=Tardio | first=Andres | title=TDE's Punch Discusses His Debut Album | magazine=Billboard | date=March 7, 2016 | url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/6898399/tde-punch-debut-album-interview | accessdate=April 25, 2017}}</ref><ref name="Ivey 2017">{{cite web | last=Ivey | first=Justin | title=Kendrick Lamar Is Upset Beyonce Didn't Win Album of the Year at 2017 Grammy Awards | website=XXL Mag | date=February 13, 2017 | url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2017/02/kendrick-lamar-is-upset-beyonce-didnt-win-album-of-the-year-at-2017-grammy-awards/ | accessdate=April 25, 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://radio.com/2015/10/27/tdes-punch-reveals-kendrick-lamars-tupac-connection-and-more/|title=TDE's Punch Reveals Kendrick Lamar's Tupac Connection and More|work=radio.com|accessdate=25 April 2017}}</ref> He has also executive produced several successful albums, including [[Kendrick Lamar]]'s ''[[Section.80]]''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://rateyourmusic.com/release/album/kendrick_lamar/section_80/ |title=Section.80 by Kendrick Lamar (Album, West Coast Hip Hop): Reviews, Ratings, Credits, Song list |publisher=[[Rate Your Music]] |date= |accessdate=2017-07-18}}</ref> (2011), [[Schoolboy Q]]'s ''[[Habits & Contradictions]]''<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/Schoolboy-Q-Habits-Contradictions/release/3630258 |title=Schoolboy Q - Habits & Contradictions (CDr, Album) |website=Discogs.com |date=2015-01-08 |access-date=July 18, 2017}}</ref> (2012), [[Terrace Martin]]'s ''[[3ChordFold]]'',<ref name="3ch">{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/3chordfold-mw0002558085/credits |title=3chordfold - Terrace Martin &#124; Credits |publisher=[[AllMusic]] |date=2013-08-13 |access-date=July 18, 2017}}</ref> (2013) and more. Aside from his solo musical career, Punch is a member of [[hip hop collective]] A Room Full of Mirrors, alongside fellow rappers Daylyt, Nick Grant, [[Willie B (producer)|The Ichiban Don]], Lyric Michelle, Hari, and more.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/punch-daylyt-and-more-spit-bars-on-a-room-full-of-mirrors-rawar-new-song.1991030.html|title=Punch, Daylyt & More Spit Bars on a Room Full of Mirrors' "RAWAR"|website=HNHH|date=21 April 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/rb-hip-hop/punch-a-room-full-of-mirrors-interview-1235003367/amp/|title = TDE's Punch Introduces New Collective a Room Full of Mirrors, Announces Visual EP Release Date|magazine = [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date = 29 November 2021}}</ref>

== Career ==
Terrence Henderson founded the Carson, California-based independent record label [[Top Dawg Entertainment]] (TDE) in 2004, with Anthony "Top Dawg" Tiffith and [[Dave Free|Dave "Miyatola" Free]]. He then began his musical career in 2007 under the [[moniker]] Punch. In March 2016, Punch revealed plans to release his debut [[studio album]].<ref name="DA">{{cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/tde-co-president-punch-plans-to-finally-release-an-album-news.20540.html|title=TDE Co-President Punch Plans To Finally Release An Album|website=HotNewHipHop|date=8 March 2016|access-date=May 25, 2019}}</ref> Punch told ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' that he plans on doing "a project for sure", even though his artistic process has usually come in spurts: "I think I’m going to finally finish it because I write a lot and I record every so often. I think I’m just gonna go ahead and knock it out at this point."<ref name="DA"/> On December 5, 2016, Punch released a single titled "Gold".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://hiphopdx.com/singles/id.35779/title.tde-co-president-punch-digs-deep-for-gold |title=TDE Co-President Punch Drops "Punch" Single |publisher=[[HipHopDX]] |date=2016-12-05 |access-date=July 18, 2017}}</ref>
