'''Skyler Austen Gordy''' (born August 23, 1986),<ref>{{citation news|work=Journal-Courier|agency=Associated Press|url=https://www.myjournalcourier.com/news/article/today-in-history-august-23-sacco-and-vanzetti-18294277.php|title=Today in History: August 23, Sacco and Vanzetti executed|date=2023-08-14|access-date=2023-08-20|url-status=live|archive-date=2023-08-21|archive-url=https://archive.today/20230821005007/https://www.myjournalcourier.com/news/article/today-in-history-august-23-sacco-and-vanzetti-18294277.php}}</ref> better known by his [[stage name]] '''Sky Blu''' (stylized as '''8ky 6lu'''),<ref>{{cite web|author=The Times of India |url=http://article.wn.com/view/2014/03/20/American_rapper_Sky_Blu_aka_LMFAO_performs/ |title=American rapper Sky Blu aka LMFAO performs - Worldnews.com |publisher=Article.wn.com |access-date=2014-04-30}}</ref> is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, DJ and dancer best known as one half of the musical duo [[LMFAO]], with the hit song "[[Party Rock Anthem]]". He formed the duo with his paternal half-uncle, [[Redfoo]], and released two albums together,<ref>{{cite news|last=Roberts|first=Randall|title=Critic's Notebook: LMFAO's brand of party rock uniquely American|url=http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/music_blog/2012/01/critics-notebook-lmfaos-brand-of-party-rock-uniquely-american-.html|access-date=25 August 2012|newspaper=The Los Angeles Times|date=13 January 2012}}</ref> 2009's ''[[Party Rock]]'' and 2011's ''[[Sorry for Party Rocking]]''.

== Career ==

In 2006, Gordy, under the stage name '''Sky Blu''' teamed up with his uncle [[Redfoo]] to form [[LMFAO]]. The band started in Miami, Florida and released rap songs over electronic beats.<ref name=mb>{{cite web | url=https://mrbaradi.com/post/155549558896/erman-la-interviews-sky-blu-on-life-after-lmfao | title=Erman LA interviews Sky Blu on life after LMFAO, new album }}</ref> They are known for their hit songs "[[Party Rock Anthem]]" and "[[Sexy and I Know It]]," which were released in 2011.<ref name=mb/> "Party Rock Anthem" became the most successful song of their career, peaking at number one in over ten countries, including France, the UK, the U.S., Canada, New Zealand, Germany, Ireland, and Australia. Redfoo announced that LMFAO would go on hiatus in 2012.<ref name=ds>{{cite web | url=https://www.digitalspy.com/music/a811685/whatever-happened-to-lmfao/ | title=Whatever happened to LMFAO? | website=[[Digital Spy]] | date=22 October 2016 }}</ref> Earlier that year, LMFAO performed alongside [[Madonna]] at the [[Super Bowl XLVI]] halftime.<ref name=mb/>  Gordy later claimed in a Facebook post in 2016 that Redfoo had tried to claim all of the band's royalties for himself and that the latter had announced LMFAO's hiatus a year after not speaking with Gordy, who had suffered serious back injury.<ref name=ds/><ref name=mb/> 

In October 2010, Gordy and his girlfriend Chelsea Korka created BIG BAD out of their Hollywood home which is an entertainment company consisting of musicians, artists, producers, DJ's, inventors, creators, and had successful tours in America, Dominican Republic, South America, Asia, and parts of Europe.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bigbad.com|title=Big Bad|website=Big Bad|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=perrosky87|title=Sky Blu by LMFAO BBU BIG BAD UNIVERSITY Conferencia de prensa en Panama|date=2012-04-20|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZZSiARAl7r0|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.bandsintown.com/BigBadUniversity/past_events?page=7|title=Big Bad University|website=www.bandsintown.com|language=en|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Cite press release|url=http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/all-access-hall-pass-live-and-big-bad-entertainment-are-proud-to-support-operation-smile-173137181.html|title=All-Access Hall Pass Live And Big Bad Entertainment Are Proud To Support Operation Smile|last=Live|first=All-Access Hall Pass|website=www.prnewswire.com|language=en|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=Luanny Christine|title=LMFAO - SKY BLU - Rihanna - You Da One - (BELÉM-PA) 26/04/12|date=2012-04-27|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=djL42UFnN5c|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=alcides netto|title=Sky Blu - LMFAO {{!}} Favela Chic - Manaus 2012|date=2012-05-06|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zNqyAd7VNnA|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref> Gordy also created Big Bad University ("the first university for dreamers"), a collective of artists, which was mainly composed of rapper [[Shwayze]], Korka, and producer/singer/songwriter Mark Rosas. 

In 2013, Sky released his two first singles "Pop Bottles" and "Salud".<ref name=ds/> That year he also released his 9-track first album ''Rebel Music'', and a second album ''We Evolve Everyday'' as free downloads.<ref name=ds/><ref name=mb/> In 2014, Gordy introduced to the world his alter ego '''The Party President''' and a new stylization of his name '''8ky 6lu''', follwowed by the release his single "Go On Girl". Gordy explained that the numbers 86 represented his birth year.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.flavourmag.co.uk/lmfaos-sky-blu-want-create-harvard-school-dreamers/ | title=LMFAO's Sky Blu: 'I want to create a Harvard school for Dreamers.' | date=19 May 2014 }}</ref>  In December that year, Gordy was invited to the [[Miss World]] ceremony to perform his single "We Love Girls" with KG Superstar. Sky released an album, ''Chaos to Consciousness'' under his new artist name '''8ky 6ordy''' (pronounced Sky Gordy) in 2016.<ref name=ds/>

==Personal life==
Gordy is the grandson of [[Motown]] founder [[Berry Gordy]]. He is the son of Berry Gordy IV and Valerie Robeson. He is the brother of DJ and singer Mahogany "Lox" Cheyenne Gordy. Other relatives include singer-songwriters [[Rockwell (musician)|Rockwell]] and [[Rhonda Ross Kendrick]], who are his uncle and aunt via his grandfather Berry Gordy, respectively. According to Gordy, he attended 12 different schools growing up and ultimately dropped out of high school without taking the GED.<ref name=mb/>

In January 2009, [[Interscope Records]] arranged a studio session for [[Paradiso Girls]] with LMFAO where Chelsea Korka met Sky Blu. A friendship which then bloomed into a long term relationship shortly after. Chelsea was featured in many of LMFAO's music videos as Sky Blu's Love Interest <ref>{{Citation|last=LMFAOVEVO|title=LMFAO - La La La|date=2009-11-25|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=clSv4TzbRCs|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=LMFAOVEVO|title=LMFAO - Sorry For Party Rocking|date=2012-02-22|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SkTt9k4Y-a8|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=LMFAOVEVO|title=LMFAO - Sexy and I Know It|date=2011-09-16|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wyx6JDQCslE|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=LMFAOVEVO|title=LMFAO - Yes|date=2010-07-23|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k39ju0Z5VmE|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref> and they were very public about their affection for one another.<ref>{{Citation|last=HellaGeo|title=LMFAO f/ Chelsea of the Paradiso Girls LIVE @ Six Flags 9-11-09|date=2009-09-19|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VrKnY-k95ro|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=PopCandiesTv|title=Sky Blu at Interscope Cherry Tree Studio in Santa Monica|date=2010-07-16|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lySHMftBOfQ|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=xxBLACKxFIRExx|title=CHELSKY|date=2011-12-30|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nFhEJOVladE|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=minchimurakami|title=Lil Jon's "Crunk Rock" Album Release Party|date=2010-06-11|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wkfWMEP97gE|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|last=BIGBADUSUPPORT|title=Skyler Gordy Chelsea Korka|date=2013-04-28|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yP6uT21RrE8|access-date=2017-03-14}}</ref>
