'''Aaron Pointer''', better known by his [[stage name]] '''Abstract Rude''', is a [[rapping|rapper]] from [[Los Angeles, California]].

He is the [[Master of Ceremonies|MC]] of Abstract Tribe Unique, alongside dancers Zulu Butterfly and Irie Lion King, [[turntablism|turntablist]] DJ Drez and [[record producer|producer]] Fat Jack.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/album-reviews/id.363/title.abstract-tribe-unique-showtyme|title=Abstract Tribe Unique - Showtyme|publisher=[[HipHopDX]]|date=January 1, 2004}}</ref> He is also a member of [[Haiku D'Etat]] along with [[Myka 9]] and [[Aceyalone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hiphopcore.net/interviews/45%7Ceng-haiku-d-etat.html|title=Interview: Haiku D'Etat|publisher=Hip Hop Core|date=December 2004}}</ref> and The A-Team along with Aceyalone.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://exclaim.ca/Reviews/HipHop/a_team-who_framed_the_a_team|title=A-Team - Who Framed The A-Team?|publisher=[[Exclaim!]]|first=Thomas|last=Quinlan|date=July 2000}}</ref>

==History==

Aaron Pointer is one of the original spawns of L.A.'s historical [[Good Life Cafe]], a renowned [[open mic]] venue. Pointer would practice his rapping every Thursday night at Good Life. He is also a member of [[Project Blowed]]. In 2009, Abstract Rude released the album ''[[Rejuvenation (Abstract Rude album)|Rejuvenation]]'' on [[Rhymesayers Entertainment]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sputnikmusic.com/review/30411/Abstract-Rude-Rejuvenation/|title=Review: Abstract Rude - Rejuvenation|publisher=Sputnikmusic|first=Sobhi|last=Youssef|date=May 4, 2009}}</ref> It was entirely produced by Vitamin D.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.xlr8r.com/reviews/abstract-rude/rejuvenation|title=Rejuvenation|publisher=[[XLR8R]]|first=Rob|last=Geary|date=May 6, 2009}}</ref>
