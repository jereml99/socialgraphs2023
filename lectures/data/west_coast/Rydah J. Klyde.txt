'''Rydah J. Klyde''' is a [[Bay Area]] [[rapper]] from [[Pittsburg, California]] best known for his affiliation with the rap group [[Mob Figaz]]. Around 2002, Rydah signed a solo deal with Mac Dre's [[Thizz Entertainment]] label. In 2003, he released his album ''Tha Fly Gangsta''. In addition to releasing many songs on numerous Thizz [[Compilation album|compilations]], Rydah saw the release of three albums; the first was an album with friend Johnny Cash as the group Money Gang<ref>{{cite web
 |url= http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?f=/c/a/2007/03/31/BAGTTOVJGB26.DTL
 |title= Richmond rapper's slaying could be part of string of hip-hop violence
 |author= Henry K. Lee
 |work= SFGate.com
 |publisher= San Francisco Chronicle
 |date= March 31, 2007
}}</ref> titled ''Bang fo Bread'', another solo album titled ''What's Really Thizzin?'' and another group effort with Thizz affiliate Freako titled ''El Pueblo Children''. In 2006, the Money Gang released a [[mixtape]] titled ''2 Chain Gang Vol. 1''.
