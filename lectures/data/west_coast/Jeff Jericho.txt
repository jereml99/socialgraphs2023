'''Jeff Jericho'''<ref>[http://www.allmusic.com/artist/jeff-jericho-mn0003259593/discography Discography AllMusicGuide]</ref><ref>[http://jeffjericho.com/#/bio/ Jeff Jericho Biography from artist webpage]</ref> (born November 21, 1985),<ref>[https://musicbrainz.org/artist/350febdb-749c-4f98-ac6b-060b5272e8f7 Musicbrainz.org Artist & Album Info]</ref> is an American [[hip hop]] [[recording artist]] from [[Los Angeles, California]].<ref>[http://www.risinghype.com/video/jeff-jericho-ridin-slow/ Rising Hype Los Angeles, CA]</ref><ref>[https://musicbrainz.org/artist/350febdb-749c-4f98-ac6b-060b5272e8f7 Musicbrainz.org Artist & Album Info]</ref> Jericho gained his interest in music at an early age and began performing at school talent shows during his high school years.  After forming a relationship with producers J-Myth and DeUno the group of friends began working on their craft and learning each other's musical tastes prior to beginning work on Jericho's debut release<ref>[http://jeffjericho.com/#/bio/ Jeff Jericho Biography from artist webpage]</ref>

Jericho began to gain notable recognition in 2014, after his first retail release, titled The Jericho Experience.<ref>[https://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/jeff-jericho/id824305002 Official iTunes Releases]</ref> The first single that was released from the album was titled Get Crazy and the music video, directed by [[Aris Jerome]], premiered on the [[Vevo]] network a week prior to that album's official release date.<ref>[http://www.vevo.com/artist/jeff-jericho Official VEVO page and Bio]</ref><ref>[http://hiphopsince1987.com/2014/videos/jeff-jericho-get-crazy-video/ Get Crazy music video on HHS1987]</ref> The song which is widely considered to be Jericho's breakthrough song from the album was titled Ridin Slow and received nearly a million streams on sites such as [[Spotify]], [[Rdio]], and [[SoundCloud]].<ref>[https://play.spotify.com/artist/3m8xXnPwi7gxTnhRNsSdks?play=true Jeff Jericho on Spotify]</ref><ref>[http://www.rdio.com/artist/Jeff_Jericho/ Jeff Jericho Rdio]</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://soundcloud.com/jeff-jericho/ridin-slow|title = Ridin Slow}}</ref> The single received notable coverage across a number of digital media outlets and along with the [[Aris Jerome]] directed music video, the song helped to recognize Jericho as one of the new and rising hip-hop artists on the west coast.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://rapsandhustles.com/2014/08/20/jeff-jericho-ridin-slow/|title = Raps & Hustles - YouTube}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.spitfirehiphop.com/jeff-jericho-ridin-slow/videos.52882.php|title = 403 Access Forbidden}}</ref><ref>[https://genius.com/Jeff-jericho-ridin-slow-lyrics#note-4829615 Jeff Jericho Rap Genius]</ref>

Jericho's debut album, The Jericho Experience, was released on April 22, 2014. The album contained the singles "Get Crazy", "Ridin Slow", and "Drives Me Crazy". The album contained 3 official music video releases on [[Vevo]], [[MTV]], and [[MUZU.TV]] networks.<ref>[http://www.vevo.com/artist/jeff-jericho Official VEVO page and Bio]</ref><ref>[http://www.mtv.com/artists/jeff-jericho/ Jeff Jericho's MTV Artist page]</ref><ref>[http://www.muzu.tv/jeff-jericho/ridin-slow-music-video/2282159/ MUZU TV]</ref>

==Early life==
Jericho was born in [[Los Angeles, California]], to an immigrant mother from [[Europe]] and a military father from [[Greenville, Mississippi]].<ref>[http://www.grindofficial.com/blog-entry/inside-grind-presents-jeff-jericho Jeff Jericho's Bio & Influences on GrindOfficial]</ref><ref>[http://www.mtv.com/artists/jeff-jericho/ Jeff Jericho's MTV Artist page]</ref> Mostly raised in a single- parent household, Jericho is the only child of his parents, who split when he was 4 years of age.<ref>[http://jeffjericho.com/#/bio/ Jeff Jericho Biography from artist webpage]</ref> [[File:Jeff Jericho 02.jpg|thumb|right|Jeff Jericho 02]] Jericho's childhood years were spent in [[South Gate, California]] which is a city located in [[Los Angeles County, California]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.cityofsouthgate.org/259/History-of-South-Gate|title = History of South Gate &#124; South Gate, CA - Official Website}}</ref> As a teenager, Jericho attended [[Cathedral City High School]] in [[Cathedral City, California]], located in southern California's [[Coachella Valley]].  After graduating high school Jericho would return to the greater Los Angeles area, where he moved to [[Long Beach, California]]<ref>http://www.mtv.com/artists/jeff-jericho/ Jeff Jericho's MTV Artist page</ref>

==Music career==

===2014-2015===
On April 22, 2014 Jericho released his debut album The Jericho Experience.<ref>[https://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/jeff-jericho/id824305002 Official iTunes Releases]</ref>  After receiving notable coverage for his single Ridin Slow which premiered on [[Vevo]], [[MTV]], and [[MUZU.TV]] networks, Jericho positioned himself to be viewed as one of the exciting, new up and coming artists on the West Coast.<ref>[http://www.vevo.com/artist/jeff-jericho Official VEVO page and Bio]</ref><ref>[http://www.mtv.com/artists/jeff-jericho/ Jeff Jericho's MTV Artist page]</ref><ref>[http://www.muzu.tv/jeff-jericho/ridin-slow-music-video/2282159/ MUZU TV]</ref><ref>[http://www.risinghype.com/video/jeff-jericho-ridin-slow/ Rising Hype Los Angeles, CA]</ref>
[[File:Jeff Jericho 03.jpg|thumb|left|Jeff Jericho 03]]
Later that year in October, Jericho released a stand-alone single titled She's On It.<ref>[http://allunsigned.tumblr.com/post/99650458641/jeff-jericho-jeffreyjericho-shes-on-it Jeff releases She's On It]</ref>  The single was only issued as a digital release and was not accompanied by a music video; however, the single did quite well on most streaming sites while piggy backing off the success of Ridin Slow.  Jericho is currently working on his next studio release titled Cake Dreams which was scheduled to be released during the late spring or early summer months of 2015.<ref>[http://nikkisiixx.com/2015/03/11/music-video-jeff-jericho-drives-me-crazy/ Drives'Me Crazy on NikkiSiixx]</ref>
