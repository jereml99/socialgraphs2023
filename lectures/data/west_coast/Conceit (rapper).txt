'''Conceit''' (fl. c. 2007) is an American [[rapper]] from [[San Francisco]].

He won the [[YouTube]] "On The Rise" video contest, {{clarify <!-- anything to do with the band in our OTR article?-->|date = November 2015}}{{when|date= November 2015}} where the finalists were selected by [[50 Cent]], and in 2007 a record deal with [[Interscope Records]] and a gift certificate{{Vague <!-- i.e., for how much $?-->|date= November 2015}} with [[Guitar Center]].

Conceit has  played with  [[Ol' Dirty Bastard|ODB]] (of [[Wu-Tang Clan]]), [[KRS-One]], [[Hieroglyphics (group)|Hieroglyphics]], [[Living Legends (group)|Living Legends]], [[The X-Ecutioners|X-Men]],{{clarify|reason= In absence of more context, i tentatively inferred the appropriate link from existence of our article on *that* band, but unless a source, re this article's "X-Men", clearly means *that band*, we should avoid further confusion by not mentioning them AT ALL|date= November 2015 <!-- This tag is about X-Men, not Fatlip!         -->}} [[Fatlip]], [[Immortal Technique]], [[Jurassic 5]], [[Quannum]], [[Devin The Dude]], [[Triple Threat Deejays]], [[Zion I]], and others. He also has appeared in both The [[Rock Steady Crew|Rock Steady]] 25th Anniversary and [[International Turntablist Federation|I.T.F.]] World Championships.
