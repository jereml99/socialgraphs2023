'''Gerard Damien Long''' (born November 9, 1990), better known by his [[stage name]] '''Hodgy''' (formerly '''Hodgy Beats'''), is an American rapper, record producer and music video director. He is best known for being a founding member of the hip hop collective [[Odd Future]].

==Music career==

Hodgy was an original member of Odd Future along with [[Tyler, the Creator]], [[Left Brain]], [[The Super 3]], and [[Casey Veggies]]. Hodgy was the first member of Odd Future to release a solo record, with ''The Dena Tape'' in 2009. As a member of the duo [[MellowHype]] with [[Left Brain]], he has released four albums; ''[[YelloWhite]]'', ''[[BlackenedWhite]]'', ''[[Numbers (Mellowhype album)|Numbers]]'', and ''INSA''.

Hodgy has been featured on various albums including ''[[Bastard (Tyler, The Creator album)|Bastard]]'', ''[[Goblin (album)|Goblin]]'' and ''[[Wolf (Tyler, The Creator album)|Wolf]]'' by [[Tyler, The Creator]], ''[[Earl (album)|Earl]]'' by [[Earl Sweatshirt]], ''[[Rolling Papers (Domo Genesis album)|Rolling Papers]]'' by [[Domo Genesis]], ''[[119 (album)|119]]'' by [[Trash Talk (band)|Trash Talk]], and ''[[The Odd Future Tape]]'', ''[[Radical (mixtape)|Radical]]'' and ''[[The OF Tape Vol. 2]]'' with [[Odd Future]]. He appeared on "Outta Control", a song from [[Stones Throw Records|Stones Throw]] rapper M.E.D.'s album ''Classic'', produced by fellow Los Angeles native [[Madlib]]. Hodgy also appeared in the music video for that song which was released after the song was released.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hFowz5b5GLM |title=M.E.D. feat. Hodgy Beats - Outta Control (Official Video) |publisher=[[YouTube]] |date=2012-04-24 |access-date=2016-12-28}}</ref>

Hodgy has released five solo mixtapes: ''The Dena Tape'', ''[[Untitled Hodgy Beats EP|Untitled EP]]'', ''Untitled 2 EP'', ''Dena Tape 2'', and ''They Watchin' LoFi Series 1''. On October 31, 2013, [[MellowHigh]] (Left Brain, Hodgy Beats, Domo Genesis) released their debut album ''[[MellowHigh (album)|MellowHigh]]'' on Odd Future Records. The album debuted at number 89 on the US [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]].<ref>{{cite web |last=Tardio |first=Andres |url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/news/id.26130/title.hip-hop-album-sales-week-ending-11-03-2013- |title=Hip Hop Album Sales: Week Ending 11/03/2013 |publisher=[[HipHopDX]] |date=2013-11-06 |access-date=2016-12-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131109045059/http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/news/id.26130/title.hip-hop-album-sales-week-ending-11-03-2013- |archive-date=2013-11-09 |url-status=dead }}</ref> In 2015, Hodgy revealed that he was almost done with his solo debut album. He also starred as a feature artist on Alison Wonderland's remix of The Buzz, by Hermitude.

His debut album, ''[[Fireplace: TheNotTheOtherSide]]'', was released in December 2016.<ref name="allmusic1">{{cite web|author=Andy Kellman |url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/fireplace-thenottheotherside-mw0003002038 |title=Fireplace: TheNotTheOtherSide - Hodgy &#124; Songs, Reviews, Credits |publisher=[[AllMusic]] |date=2016-12-23 |access-date=2016-12-28}}</ref>

On March 24th, 2022 after six years of inactivity, Hodgy released a single titled "Into Someone" and announced an upcoming project, ''Entitled'', which was released on May 20, 2022.<ref>{{Cite web |author=Mohammad Halim|date=2022-03-24 |title=Hodgy Announces New Album Entitled For May 2022 Release, Debuts Cinematic New Song And Video “Into Someone” - |url=https://music.mxdwn.com/2022/03/24/news/hodgy-announces-new-album-entitled-for-may-2022-release-debuts-cinematic-new-song-and-video-into-someone/ |access-date=2023-08-09 |website=mxdwn Music |language=en-US}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Gerard Long was born and raised in [[Trenton, NJ|Trenton]], New Jersey. He is of [[Jamaicans|Jamaican]], [[Fiji]]an and [[Philippines|Filipino]] descent{{CN|date=April 2022}}. At the age of nine, he moved to Southern California with his sister after his mother got remarried.<ref>Phillips, Rashad. [http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/interviews/id.1732/title.mellowhype-chordaroy-life "MellowHype: Chordaroy Life"] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120303181216/http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/interviews/id.1732/title.mellowhype-chordaroy-life |date=2012-03-03 }}, HipHopDX, July 15, 2011. Accessed March 30, 2012. "'''DX:''' Now as far as the L.A. scene, I read that you are actually from out East. '''Hodgy Beats:''' Yeah, I was born in East Lawrence, New Jersey and raised in Trenton until I was eight."</ref> He attended [[Pasadena High School (California)|Pasadena High School]] in [[Pasadena, California]].

On August 1, 2011, his long-time ex-girlfriend Cortney Brown, gave birth to their son, named Trenton Gerald Long. Vyron Turner (Left Brain) is Trenton's godfather.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allwesteverything.com/2011/08/baby-news-guess-which-odd-future-member-just-became-a-father/ |title= Hodgy Beats became a father |publisher= All West Everything |date= August 1, 2011}}</ref>

Following the release of ''Entitled'', Long revealed in an interview that he had committed to a [[Drug rehabilitation|rehab]] program in early 2020, before moving to [[Toronto]] after his release. He currently lives there with his daughter and two sons.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-07-06 |title=“I’d Rather Not Know Than to Know It All”: An Interview With Hodgy |url=https://www.passionweiss.com/2022/07/06/id-rather-not-know-than-to-know-it-all-an-interview-with-hodgy/ |access-date=2023-08-09 |website=Passion of the Weiss |language=en-US}}</ref>
