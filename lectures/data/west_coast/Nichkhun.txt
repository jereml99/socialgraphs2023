==Early life==
Nichkhun was born in [[Rancho Cucamonga, California]], U.S. to [[Thai Chinese]] parents and was raised in Thailand.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://newamericamedia.org/2013/10/a-pan-asian-star-is-born.php|title=Nichkhun: A Pan Asian Star is Born - New America Media|website=newamericamedia.org|access-date=December 1, 2016}}</ref>{{Dead link|date=December 2022}}<ref name=hainan>{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gKqqqGHFXR8  |archive-url=https://ghostarchive.org/varchive/youtube/20211215/gKqqqGHFXR8 |archive-date=2021-12-15 |url-status=live|title=140612 Youku All Star - Nichkhun's Talk Show |work=YouTube |date=June 12, 2014 |access-date=April 21, 2019}}{{cbignore}} (at 0:48)</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sbs.com.au/popasia/blog/2016/05/02/16-k-pop-stars-who-arent-actually-korea|title=16 K-pop stars (who aren't actually from Korea)|website=sbs.com.au|date=May 2, 2016|access-date=April 29, 2019}}</ref> His mother's family is from [[Hainan]], and he has said that he has extended family and distant ancestors from [[Guangzhou]].<ref name=hainan/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://twicsy.com/i/84jUud|title=[WEIBO] I found out that I have family in Guang Zhou!!!! and my ancestors'|work=Twicsy|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150701165325/http://twicsy.com/i/84jUud|archive-date=July 1, 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Nickhun's mother, Yenjit Horvejkul (nÃ©e Mekworewut), is the chief executive officer of a Thai pharmaceutical company, Union Medical, established in 1976, which acts as an importer and distributor.<ref name="soompi.com">{{cite web | url=http://www.soompi.com/2011/05/30/2pm-nichkhuns-mother-is-a-pharmaceutical-company-ceo/ | title=2PM Nichkhun's Mother is a Pharmaceutical Company CEO | publisher=Soompi | date=May 2, 2011 | access-date=November 3, 2015}}</ref>{{unreliable source?|date=June 2018}} He comes from a wealthy background, which earned him the nickname "Thai Prince" in South Korea.<ref name="soompi.com"/> Nichkhun has one older brother, Nichan Bert (born September 11, 1986),<ref>{{cite web |title=The birth of Nichan Bert Horvejkul |url=https://www.californiabirthindex.org/birth/nichan_bert_horvejkul_born_1986_17414263 |access-date=January 27, 2019}}</ref> and two younger sisters, Nichthima Yanin (born July 22, 1990)<ref>{{cite web |title=The birth of Nichthima Yanin Horvejkul |url=https://www.californiabirthindex.org/birth/nichthima_yanin_horvejkul_born_1990_19950753 |access-date=January 26, 2019}}</ref> and Nachjaree (Chereen) (born October 20, 1994). At the age of five, he moved to Thailand from the United States with his family and studied at Dhepkanjana School and Tangpiroondham School. At the age of twelve, he studied at [[Wanganui Collegiate School]] in New Zealand for a year and a half and moved back to the United States to finish his schooling at [[Los Osos High School]] in Rancho Cucamonga, California. He was scouted by a scout manager of [[JYP Entertainment]] at the Los Angeles Korean Music Festival.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.philstar.com/shopping-guide/2013/02/22/911530/how-thai-rapper-nickhun-2pm-became-k-pop-star|title=How Thai rapper Nickhun of 2PM became a K-Pop star|last=Subijano|first=Cai|newspaper=philstar.com|access-date=December 1, 2016}}</ref>

After passing an audition hosted by JYP Entertainment, he signed a ten-year contract with the company but later re-signed an eight-year contract, not including training, and was brought to South Korea in 2006 as a trainee, being put into a class of 24 other students. [[Park Jin-young]] informed him that he was required to learn how to sing and dance, learn [[Korean language|Korean]] and [[Standard Chinese|Mandarin Chinese]], and bulk up. He speaks fluent Thai, English, Korean, Japanese and basic Chinese. He first appeared through [[Mnet (TV channel)|Mnet]]'s ''Hot Blood'', a program that showed the intense physical training that 13 male trainees had to go through for the opportunity to debut in either a four-member ballad group ([[2AM (band)|2AM]]) or a seven-member dance group ([[2PM]]) under JYP Entertainment.<ref>[http://www.newent.com ì—´í˜ˆë‚¨ì•„ JYP 13ì¡°, ìŒ“ì•„ì˜¨ ì‹¤ë ¥ ì²« ê³µê°œ!] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101002114733/http://www.newent.com/ |date=October 2, 2010 }}. ==Personal life==
As a male citizen of Thailand, he was subject to the military draft lottery in 2009 and subsequently received an exemption from military service.<ref>{{cite web |title=[Past And Present] A Prince From Thailand, Nichkhun |url=http://www.bntnews.co.uk/app/news.php?nid=16749 |access-date=December 1, 2016 |website=BNTNews UK}}</ref>

On July 24, 2012, Nichkhun was involved in a vehicular collision with a motorcyclist in Gangnam. It was reported that the singer had been [[driving under the influence]] of alcohol when he crashed into a motorcycle. His BAC measured at 0.056%, thus his license was suspended.
