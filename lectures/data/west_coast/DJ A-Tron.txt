'''Aaron Williams''' (born April 10, 1993), best known as '''DJ A-Tron''', is an American [[Disc jockey|DJ]], radio host, web personality, event producer, and entrepreneur. He was referred to by the ''[[LA Weekly]]'' as "The Twerk King".<ref name="laweekly1">{{cite web|url=https://www.laweekly.com/music/a-tron-is-the-party-starting-twerk-king-of-la-4167937|title=A-Tron Is the Party-Starting Twerk King of L.A.|first=Jeff|last=Weiss|date=November 13, 2013|website=L.A. Weekly}}</ref> His business portfolio also includes a creative marketing and branding agency, management company, as well as an online jewelry store, The Work Shop.<ref name="FJ">{{cite web|url=http://blog.fashionjunkiez.com/404.html|title=404 Error|website=blog.fashionjunkiez.com|access-date=April 26, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190426071145/http://blog.fashionjunkiez.com/404.html|archive-date=April 26, 2019|url-status=dead}}</ref>

== Early life ==
When A-Tron was seven years old, he realized he loved to DJ and got his first set of turntables.<ref name="jenesus">{{cite web|url=http://www.jenesismagazine.com/feature-dj-a-tron-talkin-business/|title=[Feature] DJ A-Tron: Talkin' Business|date=February 17, 2014|publisher=}}</ref> He started DJing a lot more as he got older. He DJed in clubs and house parties.

A-Tron uses the DJ software Serato. A-Tron started creating mixes online. Starting on air at the age of 16 on his own internet radio show JerkinRadio which covered more than jerkin and also about being open and proud of who he was to the city, A-Tron split time as a teen between school and music. After graduating from [[St. Bernard High School (Los Angeles, California)]] in 2011, A-Tron started headlining at Hollywood clubs and throwing massive underground functions.<ref name="laweekly1" />

== DJ career ==
Performing, A-Tron has toured worldwide, and performed at official events for the [[GRAMMY Award]]s, and [[BET Awards]].

JerkinRadio is an internet radio station A-Tron opened in 2008 while in high school. The station grew to 100,000 monthly listeners due to endorsements from the jerkin scene's major figures.<ref name="laweekly1" />

A-Tron does many celebrities kids birthday parties and tours High School around California. He also performed at SXSW in Austin, Texas.<ref name="sxsw">{{cite web|url=https://schedule.sxsw.com/2014/events/event_MS27381|title=Schedule – sxsw.com|website=SXSW Schedule 2014}}</ref>

As a mixtape DJ and producer, A-Tron has released many mixtapes during his career. In 2013, A-Tron released "Skinny Ni**as" featuring [[Nipsey Hussle]].<ref name="skinny">{{cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/dj-a-tron-skinny-niggas-feat-nipsey-hussle-squad-5150-song.1614048.html|title=DJ A-Tron – Skinny Niggas Feat. Nipsey Hussle & Squad 5150|website=HotNewHipHop|date=October 2013}}</ref> It was announced in 2013 that A-Tron would release his debut mixtape with features from [[Nipsey Hussle]], Joe Moses, [[TeeFlii]], among others.<ref name="SM">{{cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/articles/lifestyle|title=Hot New Hip Hop|website=HotNewHipHop}}</ref>
[[XXL (magazine)]] called DJ A-Tron "The Most Popular underground DJ" in Los Angeles.<ref name="xxl">{{cite web|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/news/2014/01/yg-dj-mustard/|title=YG And DJ Mustard Prime For A Mainstream Hip-Hop Takeover – XXL Issue 152 – XXL|first=X. X. L.|last=Staff|website=XXL Mag|date=January 8, 2014 }}</ref>

== Business career ==
Outside of his career as a DJ and personality, A-Tron is active in the entertainment business as a business entrepreneur working with [[Fox Sports]] and multi-platinum production band 1500orNothin.

In September 2013, A-Tron opened his online retail jewelry store, The Work Shop.<ref name="FJ" />
