'''Aaron Rivera''' (born July 17, 1996),<ref name="NOISETRADE">{{cite web|title=Asaiah Ziv|url=http://noisetrade.com/kidd|publisher=NoiseTrade|access-date=February 24, 2015|archive-date=February 24, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150224084755/http://noisetrade.com/kidd|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref name="IM">{{cite web|title=Asaiah Ziv |url=http://infiltratemusic.com/artists/kidd/ |publisher=Infiltrate Music |access-date=February 24, 2015 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150221054400/http://infiltratemusic.com/artists/kidd/ |archive-date=February 21, 2015 }}</ref><ref name="CRP">{{cite web|title=KIDD Artist Profile|url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/artists/KIDD/25468/|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]]|access-date=February 24, 2015}}</ref><ref name="NRTP">{{cite web |title=KIDD Artist Profile |url=http://www.newreleasetuesday.com/artistdetail.php?artist_id=4473|publisher=New Release Tuesday|access-date=February 24, 2015}}</ref> who goes by the stage name '''Asaiah Ziv''' (pronounced: Uh-sy-uh Zihv). Rivera, formerly known as '''KIDD''', is an American [[hip hop music]]ian. His first studio album, ''Murder My Flesh'', was released by [[Christian hip hop]] label Infiltrate Music in 2013, and was a ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' chart breakthrough release.<ref name="NRTA">{{cite web|title=Murder My Flesh by KIDD|url=http://www.newreleasetuesday.com/albumdetail.php?album_id=13305|publisher=New Release Tuesday|access-date=February 24, 2015}}</ref> He subsequently released a free [[extended play]] in 2014, ''Hipsavvy: The Introduction'', also with the label Infiltrate Music.<ref name="CRA">{{cite web|title=Review: Hipsavvy: The Introduction – KIDD |url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/products/KIDD/Hipsavvy_The_Introduction/151856/|publisher=[[Cross Rhythms]] |access-date=February 24, 2015}}</ref> On February 1, 2016, [[Rapzilla]], which is run by the owners of Infiltrate, revealed that Rivera no longer identified as a Christian, and that he was released from his contract.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title = Asaiah Ziv (fka Kidd) makes statement about no longer claiming Christianity|url = http://www.rapzilla.com/rz/news/38-backstage/12473-asaiah-ziv-fka-kidd-makes-statement-about-no-longer-claiming-christianity|website = Rapzilla|access-date = February 2, 2016|first = David|last = Daniels}}</ref>

== Early life ==

Asaiah Ziv was born Aaron Michael Rivera on July 17, 1996, in [[Las Vegas Valley|Las Vegas, Nevada]].<ref name="NOISETRADE"/> He was born to a Puerto Rican father and a Hungarian mother.<ref name="IM"/>

== Music career ==

He was inspired by Lupe Fiasco to get involved in the music industry as shown in his song "Black Sheep [Yung Lupe]".

His music career started in 2011, yet his first release was ''Murder My Flesh'', with Infiltrate Music that released on September 3, 2013.<ref name="NRTA"/> This album was his breakthrough release on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' charts, at No. 49 on the [[Christian Albums]],<ref name="BILLBOARDCHR">{{cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/5694402/kidd/chart?f=310|title=Kidd – Chart history : Christian Albums|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=February 24, 2015}}</ref> and at No. 14 on the [[Top Gospel Albums]] charts.<ref name="BILLBOARDGOS">{{cite magazine |url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/5694402/kidd/chart?f=323|title=Kidd – Chart history : Gospel Albums|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=February 24, 2014}}</ref><ref name="AMA">{{cite web |url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/murder-my-flesh-mw0002566654/awards|title=Murder My Flesh – Kidd : Awards|publisher=[[AllMusic]] |access-date=February 24, 2015}}</ref> He later released, ''Hipsavvy: The Introduction'', in 2014 which hinted the start of both his musical and personal transition."<ref name="CRA"/> The album featured artists such as Angelisa, [[JGivens]], [[John Givez]], Derek Luh, DJ Aktual, Kiya Lacey and Ruzel as well as producers Wontel, DVIOUSMINDZ, Ace the Vig and Rxn.

In March 2015, he changed his stage name to Asaiah Ziv, which means "God created brilliance/radiance." Rivera is now working on branding his new collective titled "Hipsavvy." The most recent release which launched his name change was the song "Ziv?".<ref>{{cite web |title=Free Download: Asaiah Ziv – Ziv? |url=http://rapzilla.com/rz/music/freemp3s/10408-asaiah-ziv-ziv |author=Staff |publisher=[[Rapzilla]] |access-date=April 1, 2015 |date=March 16, 2015}}</ref> On February 1, 2016, Infiltrate allowed Rivera to step away from his contract, as Rivera no longer identified with the Christian beliefs promulgated by the label.<ref name=":0" />

On February 1, 2016, he released "Free Your Soul Side A: Life" and announced that "Side B: Love" would be released later in the year.{{Citation needed|date=August 2017}} On April 16, 2016, Ziv released the single, "Ether", In which he dissed previous label Infiltrate Music and previous Hipsavvy member Lee Williams.{{Citation needed|date=August 2017}} He has since released the singles "Worf" and "Derrick Rose" both which contained a featured from rapper Kenny!{{Citation needed|date=August 2017}} In August, Ziv acted as Executive Producer for Las Vegas rapper, Joey Wolfe's Debut EP, ''Long Summer EP''.{{Citation needed|date=August 2017}} On October 31, Hipsavvy Artist, ArtyBasqiyah (formerly BraveArt) released a new single called "Tale of The DM" which Ziv produced and featured on.{{Citation needed|date=August 2017}} November 10, 2016, Ziv released another new single titled "Yaya", the first to be released from Side B and stated the project would be pushed back due to losing much of his work due to a crashed hard drive and lap top.{{Citation needed|date=August 2017}} November 15, he released limited edition pieces from his ''Free Your Soul'' collection as well a collaborative single with Doanman: "Ferr".{{Citation needed|date=August 2017}}

On January 24, 2017, Ziv announced his new single would be released on January 31, 2017, titled "Take My Cup".{{Citation needed|date=August 2017}} On February 9, 2017, he announced that ''I'm Depressed, but Happy'' would be released sometime in 2017.{{Citation needed|date=August 2017}} He released the first single, "Lemonade" on April 25, 2017{{Citation needed|date=August 2017}} and "Face", feature rappers Waldo & Ali-47, on July 28, 2017.{{Citation needed|date=August 2017}}
