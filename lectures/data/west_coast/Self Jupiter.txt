'''Ornette Glenn''' (born March 16, 1970), better known by his stage name '''Self Jupiter''', is an American rapper from [[Los Angeles]], [[California]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/music_blog/2010/06/knowledge-of-self-selfjupiter-talks-freestyle-fellowship-reunion-going-back-to-school-and-west-coast.html|title=Knowledge of self: Self Jupiter talks Freestyle Fellowship reunion, returning to school, and the West Coast underground|website=[[Los Angeles Times]]|first=Jeff|last=Weiss|date=June 18, 2010|access-date=January 19, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dailycal.org/2012/09/12/goin-off-self-jupiter/|title=Goin' Off: Kleening with Jupracobrah|website=[[The Daily Californian]]|first=James|last=Bell|date=September 13, 2012|access-date=January 19, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://areyouthereason.com/west-coast-man-of-magic-and-mystery-ornette-glenn-self-jupiter-burgundy-fats/|title=West Coast Man of Magic and Mystery: Ornette Glenn (Self Jupiter, Burgundy Fats)|website=Why Blue Matters|first=Lital|last=Khaikin|date=October 18, 2013|access-date=January 19, 2014}}</ref> He is a member of [[Freestyle Fellowship]] alongside [[Myka 9]], [[Peace (rapper)|P.E.A.C.E.]], and [[Aceyalone]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://exclaim.ca/Reviews/HipHop/freestyle_fellowship-temptations|title=Freestyle Fellowship - Temptations|website=[[Exclaim!]]|first=Del F.|last=Cowie|date=November 1, 2001|access-date=January 19, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.laweekly.com/westcoastsound/2011/09/27/having-already-influenced-every-rapper-you-like-freestyle-fellowship-are-back|title=Having Already Influenced Every Rapper You Like, Freestyle Fellowship Are Back |website=[[LA Weekly]]|first=Jeff|last=Weiss|date=September 27, 2011|access-date=January 19, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://exclaim.ca/News/freestyle_fellowship_return_with_promise |title=Freestyle Fellowship Return with 'The Promise' |website=[[Exclaim!]]|first=Josiah|last=Hughes|date=July 27, 2011|access-date=January 19, 2014}}</ref> He is also one half of The Kleenrz alongside [[Kenny Segal]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.urb.com/2012/12/03/kleenrz-nasty-self-jupiter-kenny-segal/|title=Kleenrz – Nasty (Self Jupiter & Kenny Segal) (Video)|website=[[URB (magazine)|URB]]|first=Jason|last=Chang|date=December 3, 2012|access-date=January 19, 2014}}</ref>

==Career==
Self Jupiter released a collaborative album with producer [[Kenny Segal]], titled ''The Kleenrz'', on [[Hellfyre Club]] in 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.okayplayer.com/news/the-kleenrz-filthy-video.html|title=Video: the Kleenrz (Self Jupiter x Kenny Segal) "Filthy"|website=[[Okayplayer]]|author=Shamz|date=July 3, 2012|access-date=January 19, 2014}}</ref> It featured guest appearances from [[Subtitle (rapper)|Subtitle]], [[Murs (rapper)|Murs]], [[Volume 10 (rapper)|Volume 10]], and [[Abstract Rude]], among others.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.okayplayer.com/news/the-kleenrz-mr-sandman-video.html|title=Video Premiere: the Kleenrz (Self Jupiter & Kenny Segal) "Mr. Sandman"|website=[[Okayplayer]]|author=Shamz|date=August 8, 2012|access-date=January 19, 2014}}</ref> The duo's second album, titled ''Season 2'', was released on The Order Label in 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.laweekly.com/music/the-kleenrzs-self-jupiter-and-kenny-segal-share-a-free-track-breakup-breakfast-7042518|title=The Kleenrz's Self Jupiter and Kenny Segal Share a Free Track, "Breakup Breakfast"|website=[[LA Weekly]]|first=Max|last=Bell|date=June 21, 2016|access-date=September 22, 2018}}</ref> In 2019, Self Jupiter released a studio album, titled ''Sexy Beast''.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.50463/title.dxclusive-freestyle-fellowships-self-jupiter-preps-new-lp-with-before-i-kill-the-beat|title=#DXCLUSIVE: Freestyle Fellowship's Self Jupiter Preps New LP With "Before I Kill The Beat"|website=[[HipHopDX]]|first=Kyle|last=Eustice|date=March 1, 2019|access-date=July 21, 2019}}</ref>
