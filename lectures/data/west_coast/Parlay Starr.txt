'''Deshawn Carter''' (born 1982), better known by his stage name '''Parlay Starr''', is an American [[rapper]] from [[South Los Angeles, California]], United States. 

==Early life==
Born '''Deshawn Carter''' in 1982, he grew up in the Pueblos Housing Projects of south-central Los Angeles.<ref name="Hill">{{cite news |last1=Hill |first1=Pat |title=South Central Rapper Parlay Starr Finds His Groove in Bozeman |url=https://montanapioneer.com/south-central-rapper-parlay-starr-finds-his-groove-in-bozeman/ |access-date=27 January 2022 |work=Montana Pioneer |publisher=Montana Pioneer Publishing |date=June 2011}}</ref> He attended [[Crenshaw High School]], where he played basketball, and thus met some of the actors and rap artists who came to watch the school games.<ref name="Hill"/> While at school he considered becoming a rap artist, though he initially chose basketball as a career.<ref name="Hill"/> He attended Blaine College in Augusta, Georgia, on a basketball scholarship, but left after two years to care for his grandmother.<ref name="Hill"/> After his grandmother's death he returned to college, but found he was seven degree credits short.<ref name="Hill"/>

==Musical career==
In 1999, Carter met and began to perform with the group [[2nd II None]].<ref name="Hill"/> Soon thereafter he was introduced to [[DJ Quik]], and began to tour nationally.<ref name="Hill"/> In 2005, Carter connected with [[Tha Realest]], and they went on to perform together for several years.<ref name="Hill"/>

Since then Carter has collaborated with [[Snoop Dogg]] on "All Out of Love",{{Citation needed|date=January 2022|reason=Each facts in a biography of a living person needs a citation to verify that fact. Uncited facts may be removed.}} and has met several other artists, including [[Too Short]], [[Jim Jones]], [[Terrence Howard]], and the [[Outlawz]], who encouraged him to break into his own solo career.<ref name="Hill"/>

In 2009, Carter created Most Hated Mob Entertainment, and began to release his own mixtapes.<ref name="Hill"/> In 2010, he released the album ''Welcome to my World'',<ref name="Hill"/> featuring guest appearances by [[Tha Realest]], [[Yukmouth]], and [[Outlawz]], and the track "Like You" with [[Chris Brown (American singer)|Chris Brown]], and [[Tyga]], also featured on Chris Brown's "Takers" Mixtape.

In 2012, Carter released a mixtape entitled ''Starrface'', hosted By [[Dj Whoo Kid]] from Gunit Records.{{Citation needed|date=January 2022|reason=Each facts in a biography of a living person needs a citation to verify that fact. Uncited facts may be removed.}}

==Personal life==
Carter relocated to [[Bozeman, MT|Bozeman]], Montana in 2011 with his family. As his partner originates from the [[Gallatin County, Montana|Gallatin Valley]] of southwest Montana, they decided to relocate back there after their son was born, as they did not want him growing up on the city streets of South Central Los Angeles.<ref name="Hill"/>

Carter is a devout [[Christians|Christian]].{{cn|date=June 2023}}
