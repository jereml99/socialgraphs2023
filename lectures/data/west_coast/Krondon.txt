'''Marvin Jones III''' (born July 9, 1976), also known as '''Krondon''',  is an American rapper and actor from [[Los Angeles, California]].<ref name="tvovermind.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.tvovermind.com/entertainment-news/5-things-didnt-know-marvin-krondon-jones-iii|title=5 Things You Didn't Know About Marvin Krondon Jones III|date=18 August 2017|access-date=18 January 2018}}</ref> He is a member of the group [[Strong Arm Steady]], along with rappers [[Phil Da Agony]] and [[Mitchy Slick]]. As an actor Jones portrays the character [[Tobias Whale]] in [[The CW]] superhero television series ''[[Black Lightning (TV series)|Black Lightning]]'', and voices [[Tombstone (comics)|Tombstone]] in the superhero film ''[[Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse]]''.

==Early life==
Jones grew up in [[Historic South Central Los Angeles|South Central]], [[Los Angeles]].<ref name="tvovermind.com"/> He is an African American with [[albinism]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tvovermind.com/5-things-didnt-know-marvin-krondon-jones-iii/|title=5 Things You Didn't Know About Marvin Krondon Jones III|last=Nat|date=2017-08-18|website=TVOvermind|language=en-US|access-date=2019-04-13}}</ref>

==Career==
===Music career===
[[Strong Arm Steady]] formed in 2003,<ref name="hotnewhiphop.com">{{cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/StrongArmSteady/profile/|title=Strong Arm Steady|website=HotNewHipHop|access-date=18 January 2018}}</ref> and released its debut album ''[[Deep Hearted]]'' in 2007 followed by the mixtape ''Gang Mentality'', the conceptual album ''[[In Search of Stoney Jackson]]'' and their most recent installment, ''[[Arms & Hammers]]''.<ref name="hotnewhiphop.com"/>

Krondon's debut album, ''Everything’s Nothing'', came out in 2013.<ref name="ew.com">{{cite magazine|url=http://ew.com/tv/2017/08/10/black-lightning-krondon-tobias-whale/|title='Black Lighting' Casts L.A. Rapper Krondon as Big Bad Tobias Whale|magazine=[[Entertainment Weekly]]|access-date=18 January 2018}}</ref><ref name="hollywoodreporter.com">{{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/live-feed/black-lightning-taps-rapper-krondon-as-key-villain-1028337|title='Black Lightning' Taps Rapper Krondon as Key Villain|website=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]|access-date=18 January 2018}}</ref>

Krondon collaborated with Shafiq Husayn ([[Sa-Ra|Sa-Ra Creative Partners]]) under the group name ''White Boiz,'' releasing the 2015 album ''Neighborhood Wonderful'' on [[Stones Throw Records]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/artist/4652108-White-Boiz|title=White Boiz {{!}} Discography {{!}} Discogs|date=2 October 2015|website=Discogs.com|access-date=20 January 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.stonesthrow.com/news/krondon-shafiq-white-boiz/|title=Krondon And Shafiq Husayn Are WHITE BOIZ {{!}} Stones Throw Records|date=2 October 2015|website=Stones Throw Records}}</ref>

Krondon also [[ghostwrite]]s for other rappers, such as [[Snoop Dogg]],<ref name="hotnewhiphop.com"/> [[Dr. Dre]], and [[Xzibit]].<ref name="ew.com"/><ref name="hollywoodreporter.com"/>

===Personal life===

Krondon maintains a friendship with Bishop [[T. D. Jakes]] and fellow actor [[Mahershala Ali]]
