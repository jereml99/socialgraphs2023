'''Blake Christopher Young''' (born November 13, 1986), who used to go by the stage name '''Young Chozen''', is a Christian hip-hop artist.

==Early life==
Born and raised by a single mother in [[Los Angeles]], [[California]],<ref>http://www.youngchozen.com/#!bio/aboutPage {{Dead link|date=March 2022}}</ref> Young grew up dealing with [[divorce]], [[sexual molestation]], [[drugs]], [[gang violence]], and [[bullying]].<ref>{{cite web|last=Cummings|first=Tony|title=Young Chozen: The Los Angeles rapper with the "La La Love" radio hit|url=http://www.crossrhythms.co.uk/articles/music/Young_Chozen_The_Los_Angeles_rapper_with_the_La_La_Love_radio_hit/50190/p1/|publisher=Crossrhythms.co.uk|access-date=28 December 2013}}</ref>

==Career==
Young has appeared on the Disney Channel series [[The Suite Life on Deck]] and guest hosted ESPN's Elite 30.<ref>{{cite web|title=90k Watts Will Elect Young Chozen "Class President" on March 6th |url=http://www.jesusfreakhideout.com/news/2012/02/01.90k%20Watts%20Will%20Elect%20Young%20Chozen%20Class%20President%20on%20March%206th.asp|publisher=JesusFreakHideout.com|access-date=29 December 2013|author=Nashville Publicity}}</ref> His song "Glow" reached number 3 in the Netherlands Christian and Gospel Music Chart in February 2013.<ref>{{cite web|title=Young Chozen Music Chart Performance Summary|url=http://www.music-chart.info/artist/233025/Young-Chozen|publisher=MusicChart.info|access-date=28 December 2013}}</ref> He toured Australia in 2013, supporting [[Evermore (band)|Evermore]] in Perth.<ref>{{cite web|title=Young Chozen - past tour dates|url=http://www.bandsintown.com/YoungChozen/past_events|publisher=Bandsintown.com|access-date=28 December 2013}}</ref>

Young appeared in a motivational speech with other public figures, where he went to schools like Willetton Senior High School on 24 August 2016.
