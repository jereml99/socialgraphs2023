'''Gary C. Bagby''' (born August 20, 1979), who goes by the stage name '''Foreknown''' and formerly '''Foreknown Apostolic''', is an American [[Christian hip hop]] rapper. He started making [[hip hop music]] in 2009, with his first [[studio album]], ''Ornithology'', releasing in 2014 from Humble Beast Records.

==Early life==
Gary C. Bagby was born on August 20, 1979,<ref name="BDAY">{{cite web|title=Me and @ddlovato  share the same birthday, but we flipped a coin and she won the Trending Topic. Oh well. There's always next year. |url=https://twitter.com/Foreknown/status/21711285129 |author=Foreknown (@Foreknown) |publisher=[[Twitter]] |accessdate=March 1, 2016 |date=August 20, 2010}}</ref> and raised in [[Alhambra, California]] by his parents, where he graduated from [[Alhambra High School (Alhambra, California)|Alhambra High School]] in 1998.<ref name="LA Times">{{cite web|first=Gary C. |last=Bagby |title=Youth / OPINION |journal=[[Los Angeles Times]] |accessdate=March 1, 2016 |date=January 10, 1994 |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1994-01-10/local/me-10296_1_mental-peace}}</ref> He is an avid ''[[Star Wars]]'' fanatic.<ref name="Hope AUS">{{cite web|first1=Clare|last1=Chate|first2=Sam|last2=Robinson|url=http://hope1032.com.au/stories/faith/2015/the-struggle-is-real-why-gary-fears-missing-star-wars/ |title=The Struggle Is Real: Why Gary Bagby Fears Missing Star Wars |publisher=[[Hope 103.2]] |accessdate=March 1, 2016 |date=December 22, 2015}}</ref>
