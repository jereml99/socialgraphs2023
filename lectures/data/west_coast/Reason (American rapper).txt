'''Robert Lee Gill Jr.''', better known by his stage name '''Reason''', is an American rapper and songwriter. He is signed to hip hop record label [[Top Dawg Entertainment]], where he started to gain recognition. His TDE debut, ''[[There You Have It (album)|There You Have It]]'', was released on September 28, 2018, a re-release of an album from 2017.<ref>{{cite web |title=Reason Signs to TDE, Shares "The Soul" |url=http://exclaim.ca/music/article/reason_signs_to_tde_shares_the_soul |website=Exclaim!}}</ref> His major label debut album ''[[New Beginnings (Reason album)|New Beginnings]]'' was released on October 9, 2020.<ref>{{cite web |title=Reason's "New Beginnings" Features Ab-Soul, J.I.D, Isaiah Rashad & More |url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/reasons-new-beginnings-features-ab-soul-jid-isaiah-rashad-and-more-news.119080.html |website=Hotnewhiphop}}</ref>

==Career==
Reason released his first mixtape, ''In The Meantime'', on January 1, 2015.<ref>{{cite web |title=In The Meantime – REASON |url=https://genius.com/albums/Reason/In-the-meantime |website=Genius}}</ref> On January 16, 2016, he released his next mixtape titled ''The Free Album'', while releasing a compilation mixtape called ''The Proof'' on his [[SoundCloud]] later in 2016.<ref>{{cite web |title=The Free Album |url=https://www.datpiff.com/Reason-The-Free-Album-mixtape.760505.html |website=Datpiff}}</ref> On February 9, 2018, he was featured on the [[Top Dawg Entertainment]] produced film soundtrack for the [[Marvel Studios]] superhero film ''[[Black Panther (film)|Black Panther]]'' (2018), titled ''[[Black Panther (soundtrack)|Black Panther: The Album]]'', on the song "Seasons".<ref>{{cite web|last1=Tiffany|first1=Kaitlyn|title=Kendrick Lamar produced the soundtrack for Black Panther|url=https://www.theverge.com/2018/1/4/16849056/kendrick-lamar-all-the-stars-black-panther-soundtrack-marvel-sza|website=[[The Verge]]|accessdate=January 4, 2018|date=January 4, 2018}}</ref>

On August 8, it was announced that he was signed to TDE. That same day, he released a single titled "The Soul", with a music video released the next week.<ref>{{cite web |title=CALIFORNIA RAPPER REASON SIGNS TO TOP DAWG ENTERTAINMENT |url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2018/08/california-rapper-reason-signs-top-dawg-entertainment/ |website=XXL}}</ref> On August 29, he released the second single titled "Better Dayz", a song that had already made an appearance on his first mixtape.<ref name="hiphopdx.com">{{cite web |title=TDE's New Signee REASON Drops "Better Dayz" Video |url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.48233/title.tdes-new-signee-reason-drops-better-dayz-video# |website=Hip Hop DX}}</ref> On September 11, he released the third single titled "Summer Up", also accompanied by a music video.<ref name="revolt.tv">{{cite web |title=Reason shares his latest track "Summer Up" |url=https://revolt.tv/stories/2018/09/11/reason-shares-latest-track-summers-07001485cd |website=Revolt}}</ref> He was also featured on Los Angeles rapper JAG's album with [[Cozz]] on "Black Boy Rise". He is also scheduled to go on tour with [[Jay Rock]] on the "Big Redemption Tour". Reason released his mixtape, ''[[There You Have It (album)|There You Have It]]'', on September 28, 2018.<ref>{{cite web |title=Top Dawg Announces Release Date For Reason's TDE Debut "There You Have It" |url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/top-dawg-announces-release-date-for-reasons-tde-debut-there-you-have-it-news.60343.html |website=Hotnewhiphop}}</ref> The album was originally released on May 19, 2017, but upon signing with TDE, the label decided to re-release the album.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Phillips |first1=Yoh |title=A REASON to Believe: The Inspiring Story of TDE's Newest Signee Achieving His Dream |url=https://djbooth.net/features/2018-10-10-reason-tde-interview |website=DJ Booth}}</ref>

On January 6, 2019, it was announced that Reason was invited to the [[Dreamville Records|Dreamville]] recording sessions for the compilation album ''[[Revenge of the Dreamers III]]''.<ref>{{cite web |title=These 106 Artists Are Working on Dreamville's New Compilation LP |url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2019/01/artists-invited-record-dreamville-revenge-dreamers-iii-album/ |website=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]] |accessdate=January 8, 2019 |date=January 8, 2019}}</ref> He later announced that he will be joining the first leg of [[JID]]'s "Catch Me If You Can Tour".<ref>{{cite web |last1=Findlay |first1=Mitch |title=Reason Will Join JID On "Catch Us If You Can" Tour |url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/reason-will-join-jid-on-catch-us-if-you-can-tour-news.68871.html |website=HotNewHopHop |accessdate=January 15, 2019}}</ref>

On February 14, 2020, Reason announced the title of his album ''[[New Beginnings (Reason album)|New Beginnings]]''.<ref>{{cite tweet|title=New Beginnings Coming Soon! Pre TDE|number=1228449508767326208|user=reasonTDE|date=February 14, 2020}}</ref> On April 24, as part of [[Top Dawg Entertainment]]'s Fan Appreciation Week, Reason released the song "Might Not Make It".<ref>{{cite web |last=Ingvaldsen |first=Torsten |title=REASON Details His Doomsday Schedule on New Single "Might Not Make It" |url=https://hypebeast.com/2020/4/reason-might-not-make-it-single-stream |website=Hypebeast |accessdate=April 24, 2020}}</ref> On October 9, 2020, Reason released the album ''New Beginnings''. He explained the album's delay: "I had to get rid of every fear, anxiety, doubt, and negative mindset to make this project".<ref name="Release">{{cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/reason-announces-new-beginnings-album-release-date-news.118716.html?_amp|title=Reason Announces "New Beginnings" Album Release Date|work=HotNewHipHop|last=Findlay|first=Mitch|date=September 29, 2020|accessdate=October 1, 2020}}</ref>

===Influences===
In an interview, Reason named [[Lil Wayne]], [[J. Cole]], [[Eminem]], [[Fabolous]], and [[Lupe Fiasco]] as his inspirations and favorite hip hop artists.<ref>{{cite web |title=Reason Describes His Journey From Hungry MC To TDE’s Next Top Dawg (Video)|url=https://ambrosiaforheads.com/2018/09/reason-story-signing-tde-top-dawg-roster-video/|website=AmbrosiaForHeads}}</ref>
