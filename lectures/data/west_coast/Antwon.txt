'''Antonio Williams'''<ref name="sfweekly-zoo">{{cite web|url=http://www.sfweekly.com/sanfrancisco/at-the-zoo-with-antwon/Content?oid=2949550|title=At the Zoo With Antwon|work=[[SF Weekly]]|first=Ian S.|last=Port|date=May 7, 2014|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref> (born October 25, 1986),<ref name="bbc">{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/music/artists/42012890-61a5-4c58-a97b-b93ef26effd3|title=Antwon|work=[[BBC Music]]|access-date=May 7, 2019}}</ref> better known by his stage name '''Antwon''', is an American rapper based in [[San Jose, California]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/17838-antwon-in-dark-denim/|title=Antwon: In Dark Denim|work=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]]|first=Ian|last=Cohen|date=March 6, 2013|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref> In 2013, he was included on ''[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]''{{'}}s "15 Unsigned Rappers Who Should Get a Deal After SXSW" list.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2013/03/15-unsigned-rappers-who-should-get-a-deal-after-sxsw/antwon|title=15 Unsigned Rappers Who Should Get a Deal After SXSW - Antwon|work=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]|first1=David|last1=Drake|first2=Ernest|last2=Baker|date=March 16, 2013|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref> He was also included on ''Complex''{{'}}s "25 New Rappers to Watch Out For in 2014" list.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2014/08/25-new-rappers-to-watch-out-for-in-2014/antwon|title=25 New Rappers to Watch Out For In 2014 - Antwon|work=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]|first=Chris|last=Mench|date=August 2, 2014|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref>

==Biography==
Born in [[Key West, Florida]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sfweekly.com/2013-03-06/music/hip-hop-artistantwon-on-sex-rap-punk-vibes-and-kid-rock/|title=Antwon's Twisted Vision: Sex Rap, Punk Vibes, and Kid Rock|work=[[SF Weekly]]|first=Phillip|last=Mlynar|date=March 6, 2013|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref> to an [[African-Americans|African-American]] father and a [[Filipino Americans|Filipino]] mother,<ref name="sfweekly-zoo"/> Antwon grew up in [[Sunnyvale, California]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://noisey.vice.com/blog/first-dates-antwon|title=A First Date With... Antwon|work=[[Vice (magazine)|Vice]]|first=Tish|last=Weinstock|date=March 17, 2014|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref> He was a member of the Philadelphia-based hardcore band Leather,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.papermag.com/2013/06/antwon_genre_benders.php|title=Antwon: genre benders|work=[[Paper (magazine)|Paper]]|first=Eli|last=Schmitt|date=June 27, 2013|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref> as well as the pop punk bands Sour Patch and How to: Summer.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://noisey.vice.com/en_us/article/rgd446/getting-to-know-you-white-lungs-mish-way-x-antwon|title=Getting to Know You: White Lung's Mish Way x Antwon|work=[[Vice (magazine)|Vice]]|first=Mish|last=Way|date=August 16, 2013|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref>

Antwon's first mixtape, ''Fantasy Beds'', was released in 2011.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.eastbayexpress.com/oakland/antwon-on-the-fringe/Content?oid=3487495|title=Antwon on the Fringe|work=[[East Bay Express]]|first=Whitney|last=Phaneuf|date=March 13, 2013|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref> In that year, he also released ''My Westside Horizon''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sfbg.com/2012/09/11/dream-90s|title=Dream of the '90s (1/2)|work=[[San Francisco Bay Guardian]]|first=Emily|last=Savage|date=September 11, 2012|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref>

In 2012, Antwon released a mixtape, ''End of Earth''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.stereogum.com/1112412/download-antwon-end-of-earth-mixtape/mp3s/|title=Download Antwon End Of Earth Mixtape|work=[[Stereogum]]|first=Tom|last=Breihan|date=August 2, 2012|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref> ''[[SF Weekly]]'' included it on the "10 Best Bay Area Hip-Hop Records of 2012" list.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sfweekly.com/shookdown/2012/12/21/the-10-best-bay-area-hip-hop-records-of-2012|title=The 10 Best Bay Area Hip-Hop Records of 2012|work=[[SF Weekly]]|first=Rachel|last=Swan|date=December 21, 2012|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref> In 2013, he released a mixtape, ''In Dark Denim''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.thefader.com/2013/02/14/download-antwons-in-dark-denim-mixtape|title=Download Antwon's In Dark Denim Mixtape|work=[[The Fader]]|first=Duncan|last=Cooper|date=February 14, 2013|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref> It was listed as ''[[Spin (magazine)|Spin]]''{{'}}s "Rap Release of the Week".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.spin.com/2013/02/rap-release-of-the-week-antwons-in-dark-denim/|title=Rap Release of the Week: Antwon's 'In Dark Denim'|work=[[Spin (magazine)|Spin]]|first=Brandon|last=Soderberg|date=February 20, 2013|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref>

His first official studio album, ''[[Heavy Hearted in Doldrums]]'', was released on UNIF and Nature World in 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cmj.com/reviews/antwon-heavy-hearted-doldrums/|title=Antwon - Heavy Hearted In Doldrums|work=[[CMJ]]|first=Lizzie|last=Plaugic|date=May 13, 2014|access-date=June 26, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150109233513/http://www.cmj.com/reviews/antwon-heavy-hearted-doldrums/|archive-date=January 9, 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref> In 2016, he released an EP, ''Double Ecstasy'', on [[Anticon]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://consequenceofsound.net/2016/02/top-10-songs-of-the-week-219/10/|title=Top 10 Songs of the Week (2/19): 01. Antwon – "Luv"|work=[[Consequence of Sound]]|first=Karen|last=Gwee|date=February 20, 2016|access-date=April 20, 2016}}</ref> In 2017, he released a mixtape, ''Sunnyvale Gardens''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://exclaim.ca/music/article/antwon-sunnyvale_gardens_mixtape|title=Antwon 'Sunnyvale Gardens' (mixtape)|work=[[Exclaim!]]|first=Josiah|last=Hughes|date=October 6, 2017|access-date=May 19, 2018}}</ref> [[Red Bull]] placed it at number 20 on the "20 Best Hip-Hop Mixtapes of 2017" list.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.redbull.com/us-en/20-best-hip-hop-mix-tapes-of-2017|title=20 Best Hip-Hop Mixtapes of 2017|work=[[Red Bull]]|first=Yu-Cheng|last=Lin|date=December 15, 2017|access-date=June 6, 2018}}</ref>

==Style and influences==
''[[Spin (magazine)|Spin]]'' described Antwon as "an affable guy who might have one of rap's biggest, best-sounding voices, which he usually focuses on goofy jokes and surprisingly sweet bedroom raps."<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.spin.com/2012/07/first-spin-antwon-slow-jams-apocalypse-new-album-end-earth/|title=First Spin: Antwon Slow Jams The Apocalypse On New Album, 'End Of Earth'|work=[[Spin (magazine)|Spin]]|first=Brandon|last=Soderberg|date=July 31, 2012|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref> He has often been compared to [[The Notorious B.I.G.]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sfweekly.com/shookdown/2013/03/14/san-jose-rapper-antwon-breaks-through-a-hellish-set-at-sxsw|title=San Jose Rapper Antwon Breaks Through a Hellish Set at SXSW|work=[[SF Weekly]]|first=Ian S.|last=Port|date=March 14, 2013|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref> He noted [[Kid Rock]] as his greatest influence.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.aux.tv/2014/01/qa-california-rapper-antwon-does-things-the-punk-way/|title=Q&A: California rapper Antwon does things the punk way|work=[[Aux (TV channel)|Aux]]|first=Mish|last=Way|date=January 18, 2014|access-date=June 26, 2015}}</ref>

==Controversy==
In 2016, Antwon formed Secret Circle along with [[Wiki (rapper)|Wiki]] and [[Lil Ugly Mane]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/secret-circle-wiki-antwon-and-lil-ugly-mane-break-up/|title=Secret Circle (Wiki, Antwon, and Lil Ugly Mane) Break Up|work=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]]|first1=Matthew|last1=Strauss|first2=Noah|last2=Yoo|date=May 18, 2018|access-date=May 7, 2019}}</ref> Since then, the group released several tracks and played live performances across North America.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/05/18/lil-ugly-mane-secret-circle-break-up-album|title=Lil Ugly Mane: "Secret Circle is a wrap"|work=[[The Fader]]|first=Jordan|last=Darville|date=May 18, 2018|access-date=May 7, 2019}}</ref> In 2018, Lil Ugly Mane announced the breakup of the group, not initially indicating a specific reason for the breakup.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.stereogum.com/1997391/secret-circle-break-up/news/|title=Antwon, Wiki, And Lil Ugly Mane Release Statements After Secret Circle Break Up|work=[[Stereogum]]|first=Tom|last=Breihan|date=May 22, 2018|access-date=May 7, 2019}}</ref> Antwon posted a statement on [[Twitter]], alluding to unnamed allegations.<ref name="pitchfork2018">{{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/wiki-and-lil-ugly-mane-indicate-secret-circle-broke-up-over-antwons-treatment-of-women/|title=Wiki and Lil Ugly Mane Indicate Secret Circle Broke Up Over Antwon's Treatment of Women|work=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]]|first=Matthew|last=Strauss|date=May 21, 2018|access-date=May 7, 2019}}</ref> Wiki and Lil Ugly Mane subsequently indicated that the allegations mentioned in Antwon's statement, including abusive treatment of women, were the impetus for the breakup.<ref name="pitchfork2018"/>
