'''Dusty Locane''' (often stylized in all uppercase as '''DUSTY LOCANE'''; born June 11, 1999) is an American rapper from [[Brooklyn, New York]]. He began gaining recognition in 2020 and 2021 when his singles "[[Rollin n Controllin Freestyle]]" and "Rolando (Caught in the Rain)" began getting popular on various [[social media]] platforms,<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-01-09|title=Pop Smoke Sound-A-Like Dusty Locane Gets Spotlight Treatment From Akademiks|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.59973/title.pop-smoke-sound-a-like-dusty-locane-gets-spotlight-treatment-from-akademiks|access-date=2022-01-03|website=HipHopDX}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|author=richyfig|date=April 14, 2021|title=Dusty Locane Is On The Rise From NYC|url=https://www.elevatormag.com/dusty-locane-is-on-the-rise-from-nyc|url-status=live|access-date=3 January 2022|website=Elevator Mag|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210413223309/https://www.elevatormag.com/dusty-locane-is-on-the-rise-from-nyc |archive-date=April 13, 2021 }}</ref> most notably [[TikTok]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-12-02|title=Dusty Locane interview: Brooklyn drill sensation finds lessons in all of life's challenges|url=https://ourgenerationmusic.com/exclusive/dusty-locane-interview-brooklyn-drill-sensation-finds-lessons-in-all-of-lifes-challenges/|access-date=2022-01-03|website=Our Generation Music}}</ref> He has been noted by ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' for his "raspy, [[Drill music|drill]] sound,"<ref>{{Cite web|title=R&B/Hip-Hop Fresh Picks of the Week: Dvsn, Dusty Locane, Mariah the Scientist & More|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/rb-hip-hop/rb-hip-hop-fresh-picks-dvsn-dusty-locane-9597066/|first=Hannah|last=Dailey|date=7 June 2021|publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=4 January 2022}}</ref> and by ''[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]]'', who called "Rolando (Caught in the Rain)" a "must-hear" rap song.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-01-11|title=The Ones: Dusty Locane's "Rolando (Caught in the Rain)"|url=https://pitchfork.com/thepitch/dusty-locane-rolando-new-song-listen/|access-date=2022-01-04|website=Pitchfork}}</ref>

==Early and personal life==
Dusty Locane was raised in "a good home," not "subjected to the harsh realities of living in the hood," in [[Canarsie, Brooklyn]], New York.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Edwards|first=Derrius|date=2021-12-06|title=INTERVIEW: DUSTY LOCANE Is in Control|url=https://www.dirty-glove.net/dusty-locane-interview/|access-date=2022-01-03|website=Dirty Glove Bastard}}</ref> His father is Haitian and his mother is [[Trinidadians and Tobagonians|Trinidadian]], which led to him listening to [[soca music]] as a child, in addition to hip hop and R&B.<ref>{{Cite web|last=III|first=Robby Seabrook|title=The Break Presents: Dusty Locane|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/dusty-locane-interview-the-break/|access-date=2022-01-03|website=XXL Mag|language=en}}</ref> He attended college for a year before getting expelled and initially wanted to pursue a career in basketball. He then spent some time in jail, and started considering a music career seriously upon being released.<ref>{{Cite web|title=In Search Of: Brooklyn Drill Rapper Dusty Locane Is Shaking Off Pop Smoke Comparisons & Going Viral|url=https://genius.com/a/who-is-dusty-locane-pop-smoke-comparisons-name-mean|access-date=2022-01-03|website=Genius}}</ref> He is affiliated with the [[Rollin' 60s Neighborhood Crips]].<ref>{{Citation|title=Dusty Locane on the Rolling 60 Crips & having the LA gang welcome him w/ open arms|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ArYWy21sMdY|via=YouTube|access-date=2022-01-03}}</ref> The rapper has three children, a son born on March 23, 2022, a daughter born June 11, 2022, and another son born in August 2022.<ref name=mixtapez_interview/>

==Career==
As of August 2023 he has released two albums, two collaborative albums, one mixtape, two extended plays and 28 singles (including 13 as a featured artist).

Dusty Locane released his debut single, "[[Rollin n Controllin Freestyle]]", on August 7, 2020. On January 8, 2021, [[DJ Akademiks]] posted two videos of Locane rapping to his millions of followers on Instagram,{{Citation needed|date=July 2022}} thus introducing him to new listeners. By December 2021, the song had 200 million streams and was certified gold by the [[Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://theknockturnal.com/exclusive-dusty-locane-talks-untamed-working-with-empire-switching-lanes/|title=Exclusive: Dusty Locane Talks 'Untamed,' Working With Empire & Switching Lanes|first=Nishat|last=Baig|date=11 December 2021|website=theknockturnal.com|access-date=4 January 2022}}</ref><ref name = RIAA>{{Cite web|url=https://www.riaa.com/classof-2021/?q=gold_singles|title=#Classof 2021 - RIAA|publisher=[[Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA]]|access-date=4 January 2022}}</ref>

Dusty Locane announced that his debut album, ''Untamed'', was to be released on November 19, 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/dusty-locane-shows-us-canes-world-before-next-weeks-album-new-song.1993267.html|title=Dusty Locane Shows Us "CANES WORLD" Before Next Week's Album|first=Thomas|last=Galindo|date=November 12, 2021|website=hotnewhiphop.com|access-date=November 23, 2021}}</ref> The album was postponed, however, out of respect for rapper [[Young Dolph]], who was murdered on November 17.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-12-14|title=Here's everything we know about the death of Young Dolph|url=https://www.fox13memphis.com/news/local/rapper-young-dolph-shot-killed-memphis-law-enforcement-sources-confirm/Y37EQYI6TJBHHMLLBAR6KSMPNA/|access-date=2022-01-03|website=FOX13 News Memphis}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|date=2021-12-03|title=Dusty Locane releases his debut album 'UNTAMED'|url=https://ourgenerationmusic.com/news/dusty-locane-to-drop-his-debut-album-untamed-on-friday/|access-date=2022-01-03|website=Our Generation Music}}</ref> It was finally released on December 3, 2021.<ref name=":1" /> The album contained 20 songs and was generally received rather positively by critics. The album was supported by 6 singles and featured guest appearances from OMB Jaydee, 3Kizzy, [[Yung Bleu]], 8ANDITT, [[Rah Swish]] and KG MORE BREESH.

On September 2, 2022 Dusty Locane released a 7 track EP titled ''Rollin N Controllin''. The EP featured the singles "[[Rollin n Controllin Freestyle]]", "Rolando (Caught in the Rain)", "Rollin n Controllin, Pt. 2 (Picture Me)" and "Rollin N Contollin, Pt.3 (Been Rollin)". The EP featured a sole appearance from Kajun Waters. 

On September 23, 2022 Dusty Locane released a collaboration album alongside fellow rappers [[Ron Suno]], [[Rah Swish]] and OnPointLikeOP titled ''Say Dat''. The album contained 8 songs.

On October 31, 2022 Dusty Locane released an album titled ''Nightmare On Da Fifth''. The album was supported by the single "Cremate (Run Outta Lucc)" which featured Stelly Hundo, OMB Jay Dee and 3Kizzy. The album featured 15 songs and guest appearances from 3Kizzy, Kajun Waters, SFIV5, TaTa, Stelly Hundo and OMB Jay Dee. 3 of the albums songs were released on previous Dusty Locane projects such as "PRESSURE" and "ROLANDO 2 (Catch The Rain)" both coming from ''Rollin N Controllin'' and "WAY BACC" coming from ''Say Dat''.

On December 5, 2022 Dusty Locane released an EP titled ''Catch Da Flu''. The EP featured 6 songs.
