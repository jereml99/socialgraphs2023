'''Hee-Sun Lee''' (born June 4, 1983),<ref name="CDBABY">{{cite web|title=HeeSun Lee : Re:Defined. |url=http://www.cdbaby.com/cd/heesunlee |publisher=NoiseTrade |accessdate=February 24, 2014}}</ref><ref name="FB">{{cite web|title=Not too shabby for 31 ... thank u to everyone for all of the bday wishes today! ==Early life==
HeeSun Lee was born Hee-Sun Lee on June 4, 1983, in [[Seoul]], South Korea, but was abandoned by both of her birth parents at four months old.<ref name="INMYCITY">{{cite web|url=http://inmycityrecords.com/artists/heesun-lee/|title=HeeSun Lee|publisher=In My City Records|accessdate=February 24, 2015}}</ref> She was then adopted by Chinese-American immigrants, who brought her to live and reside in [[Staten Island]], New York.

==Music career==
Her music career got started in 2008, with the release of ''Re:Defined.'' by Jahrock'n Productions.<ref name="CDBABY"/> She released her second album, ''Stereotypes'', on January 21, 2014, with In My City Records.<ref name="NRTA"/> This would be her ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' chart breakthrough album on the [[Top Gospel Albums]] chart at No. 25.<ref name="BILLBOARD"/> The album was reviewed by Wade-O Radio where they called it "a great one", Jam the Hype receiving a 7.6 out of ten, and Reel-Gospel receiving a three out of five star rating.<ref>{{cite web|title=Album Review: HeeSun Lee – Stereotypes|url=http://wadeoradio.com/album-review-heesun-lee-stereotypes/|first=Aubrey|last=McKay|publisher=Wade-O Radio|accessdate=March 5, 2015|date=January 30, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=HeeSun Lee – Stereotypes|url=http://jamthehype.com/hee-sun-lee-stereotypes/|author=Lucy|publisher=Jam the Hype |accessdate=March 5, 2015|date=January 27, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Album Review: HeeSun Lee – Stereotypes|url=http://reel-gospel.com/2014/02/14/album-review-heesun-lee-stereotypes/|first=Sam|last=Robinson|publisher=Reel-Gospel|accessdate=March 5, 2015|date=February 14, 2014}}</ref>
