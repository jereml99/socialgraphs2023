'''Scott Keeney''' (born 15 November 1983), better known by his [[stage name]] '''DJ Skee''', is an American artist, television host, radio personality, and entrepreneur. Skee rose to fame as the first DJ to discover and play superstar artists on the radio including [[Kendrick Lamar]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.startribune.com/st-paul-s-dj-skee-on-becoming-the-oprah-of-music/329573831/|title=St. Paul's DJ Skee on becoming 'the Oprah of music'|website=[[Star Tribune]]}}</ref> [[Justin Bieber]], [[Akon]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.startribune.com/st-paul-s-dj-skee-on-becoming-the-oprah-of-music/329573831/|title=St. Paul's DJ Skee on becoming 'the Oprah of music'|website=[[Star Tribune]]}}</ref> [[Lorde]], and [[Lady Gaga]] amongst many others.<ref>Lady Gaga [http://www.mynuvotv.com/blog/lady-gaga-gives-dj-skee-a-shout-out "Lady Gaga Gives DJ Skee A Shout-Out"] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140810070214/http://www.mynuvotv.com/blog/lady-gaga-gives-dj-skee-a-shout-out |date=2014-08-10 }}, ''[[NuvoTV]]'', Los Angeles, March 2014. Retrieved on 31 March 2014.</ref>

== Radio career ==
In 2015, Skee officially launched [[Dash Radio]], which has since become world's largest all original digital radio network with investments from the likes of [[L.A. Reid]], [[Adrian Peterson]], Kevin Colleran, [[Michael Lazerow]] and more. Now reaching over 5 million listeners worldwide, Dash Radio powers over 75 stations completely free and curated by real DJs, artists and tastemakers with zero traditional commercials.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/business/6221875/dj-skee-debuts-dash-radio-taps-east-village-radio-co-founder-la-reid-as |title=DJ Skee Debuts DASH Radio, Taps East Village Radio Co-Founder, L.A. Reid As Investors |magazine=[[Billboard Magazine]]|date=2014-08-19 |access-date=2015-08-27}}</ref> Dash Radio has partnered with the likes of [[Snoop Dogg]], [[Kylie Jenner]], [[Odd Future]], [[The GRAMMYs]], [[EA Sports]], [[Tionne Watkins|T-Boz]] ([[TLC (group)|TLC]]), [[Stevie Wonder]], [[Wu-Tang Clan]], [[XXL Magazine]], Strange Music Inc., The Computer Game Show, multiple music festivals, and many more.<ref>{{cite web|last=Reed |first=Ryan |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/news/odd-future-launch-24-7-radio-channel-20140912 |title=Odd Future Launch 24/7 Channel on Dash Radio |publisher=[[Rolling Stone]]|date=2014-09-12 |access-date=2015-08-27}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allaccess.com/net-news/archive/story/137255/dash-radio-launches-grammy-awards-station |title=DASH Radio Launches Grammy Awards Station |publisher=AllAccess.com |date=2015-01-14 |access-date=2015-08-27}}</ref>

== Business career ==

Outside of his career as a DJ and TV personality, Skee is active as an entrepreneur being named one of [[Billboard Magazine]]'s influential "Power Players: 30 Under 30"<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.billboard.biz/bbbiz/content_display/industry/e3i724a018466b89411a672e7d84ad5df47|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20101119212223/http://www.billboard.biz/bbbiz/content_display/industry/e3i724a018466b89411a672e7d84ad5df47|archive-date = 19 November 2010|title = Pro Posts}}</ref> in the 21 August 2010 issue. Skee was also named as an honorable mention ''[[Forbes]]'' magazine's influential "Power Players: 30 Under 30"<ref name="forbes30under30">{{cite news|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/zackomalleygreenburg/2011/12/21/30-under-30-in-music-honorable-mentions/2/|title=Forbes 30 Under 30 List|last=Langhorne|first=Cyrus|date=21 December 2011|work=[[Forbes]]|access-date=2012-01-10}}</ref> in 2012. In September 2012, Skee opened the second and flagship location of his retail store chain, Tradition, in the [[Beverly Center]] in [[Beverly Hills]], CA.

Known as the man behind the marketing campaigns and promotions for the popular [[T-Mobile Sidekick]] in the mid-2000s, Skee has orchestrated campaigns for [[Daimler-Chrysler]] (and specifically the launch of the [[Chrysler 300]] and [[Dodge Magnum]] in 2004), Google's Android Platform, [[Nike Inc|Nike]], nearly every major record label in the world, and more.  Additionally, he has written pieces for magazines such as Forbes, Billboard, Recode<ref>Pandora's Rdio Purchase Good for Listeners and Artists?</ref>

In February, 2021 DJ Skee partnered with [[Topps]] for its Project 70 baseball card collection.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.topps.com/archive/project70r-archive.html?p=49|title = Project70® Archive - Archive}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.cardboardconnection.com/2021-topps-project70-baseball-cards|title = 2021 Topps Project70 Baseball Cards Checklist Breakdown|date = 27 December 2021}}</ref>  As a mixtape DJ, Skee created curated playlists for each of his cards on [[Spotify]].

On May 24, 2022, DJ Skee released the book "The Metaverse Handbook". Co-authored with Quharrison Terry with a foreword written by [[Paris Hilton]].<ref>[https://irishtechnews.ie/the-metaverse-handbook-the-next-tectonic-shift/ book]</reF><reF>{{cite web |title=The Metaverse Summer Reading List: Here Are The Books Every Professional Should Read This Summer |website=[[Forbes]] |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221018131544/https://www.forbes.com/sites/cathyhackl/2022/07/15/the-metaverse-summer-reading-list-here-are-the-books-every-professional-should-read-this-summer/?sh=574bf6cbd5ea |archive-date=2022-10-18 |url-status=live |url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/cathyhackl/2022/07/15/the-metaverse-summer-reading-list-here-are-the-books-every-professional-should-read-this-summer/?sh=574bf6cbd5ea}}</reF>

== DJ career ==

As a mixtape DJ & producer, Skee has released over 100 mixtapes during his career. In 2008, Skee was nominated in eight categories, twice as much as any other DJ, at Justo's Mixtape Awards.<ref name="nominations">{{cite news|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/homepage-highlight/2008/02/2008-justo-mixtape-awards-nominations-announced-ceremony-delayed/|title=11th Annual Mixtape Awards Nominations Announced, Ceremony Delayed|date=8 February 2008|work=XXL Mag|access-date=2011-01-18}}</ref> Skee also won west coast mixtape DJ of the year at the 2013 Global Spin Awards.

Skee provided the sonic and musical identity for the 2012 [[Dew Tour]] in conjunction with [[NBC]] and [[Mountain Dew]] as well as the athletes on the tour.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.marketwatch.com/story/dew-tour-announces-expanded-music-initiative-including-djathlete-collaborations-with-dj-skee-for-original-event-inspired-songs-2012-08-15|title=Dew Tour Announces Expanded Music Initiative Including DJ/Athlete Collaborations With DJ SKEE for Original Event Inspired Songs|last=Langhorne|first=Cyrus|date=15 August 2012|work=MarketWatch.com|access-date=2012-08-25}}</ref>

It was announced in 2012 that Skee would be involved with the music for ''[[Halo 4]]'' and the [[Microsoft]] [[Xbox 360]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://multiplayerblog.mtv.com/2012/08/23/space-guns-and-music-pt-2-dj-skee-on-remixing-halo-4/|title=Space, Guns, and Music Pt. 2: DJ Skee on Remixing 'Halo 4' List|last=Langhorne|first=Cyrus|date=23 August 2012|publisher=MTV|access-date=2012-09-06}}</ref>

On November 27, 2021, DJ Skee was the guest DJ for the [[NCAA]] football game in [[Michigan]] between The [[Michigan Wolverines football]] team and The [[Ohio State Buckeyes football]] team.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://saturdaytradition.com/michigan-football/michigan-announces-guest-dj-will-be-performing-throughout-the-game/|title = Michigan announces guest DJ will be performing throughout the Game|date = 24 November 2021}}</ref> Skee will return to [[Michigan Stadium]] for Michigan's game versus the [[Penn State Nittany Lions football]] team on October 15, 2022.

On February 6, 2022 DJ Skee became the first DJ to DJ live during a [[NASCAR]] race for the [[Busch Light Clash at The Coliseum]] in [[Los Angeles]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ca.sports.yahoo.com/news/dj-skee-nascar-history-busch-181139179.html|title = DJ Skee to make NASCAR history at Busch Light Clash at the Coliseum| date=26 January 2022 }}</ref>

== Television ==

Now in its 5th season on Fuse, "Skee TV" is a one-hour original series that provides insider access to the hottest musical artists, athletes, celebrities and cultural tastemakers via exclusive interviews and live performances.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2014/04/11/skee-live-renewed-by-axs-tv-for-second-season/253068/ |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140413230544/http://tvbythenumbers.zap2it.com/2014/04/11/skee-live-renewed-by-axs-tv-for-second-season/253068/ |url-status=dead |archive-date=2014-04-13 |title='Skee Live' Renewed by AXS TV for Second Season - Ratings |publisher=TVbytheNumbers.Zap2it.com |date=2014-04-11 |access-date=2015-08-27}}</ref>

On June 21, 2021, DJ Skee threw out the first pitch at [[Petco Park]] before the game between the [[Padres]] vs [[Dodgers]].<ref>{{cite tweet|number=1407158253335678977|user=TheCardLifeTV|title=Step 1: Release the most epic card of...|date=22 June 2021}}</ref>

On July 15, 2021, [[Billboard Magazine]] released an article announcing that a documentary on the death of [[Eazy-E]] would be airing on [[MeTV]] with DJ Skee as one of the Executive Producers.<ref>{{Cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/9601492/eazy-e-death-investigation-mysterious-death-of-eazy-e-series-we-tv/|title=How Did Eazy-E Die?
