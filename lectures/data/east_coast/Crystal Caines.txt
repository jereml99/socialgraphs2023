'''Crystal Caines''' is a [[Guyanese Americans|Guyanese]]–American [[rapper]], record producer, artist, songwriter and [[Audio engineer|engineer]] based in [[Harlem, New York]].<ref name="nadeska">{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/news/2011172/asap-ferg-forever-mixtape-breakdown/|title=A$AP Ferg's Ferg Forever Mixtape: A Track-By-Track Breakdown|work=MTV Rap Fix|first=Nadeska|last=Alexis|date=November 29, 2014}}</ref> She has Produced and been featured on tracks by [[Bia (rapper)|BIA]],<ref name="nostro">{{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2013/08/crystal-caines-trap-lord-producer|title=Meet Crystal Caines, the Female Producer Who Worked on A$AP Ferg's "Trap Lord" Album|work=Complex Media|first=Lauren|last=Nostro|date=November 29, 2014}}</ref> [[Baauer]], [[M.I.A. (rapper)|MIA]], [[Jack Harlow]], [[Bryson Tiller]], Ransom, Nick Hook and more.

== Career ==
Crystal Caines is an Artist, Producer and Engineer. She began her production career in 2012. Caines went on to artist develop and produce for ASAP Ferg. In 2013, Caines produced the Bone Thugs-n-Harmony-featuring "Lord" on A$AP Ferg's debut album, Trap Lord, Continuing on to also engineering and premixing vocals for A$AP Ferg's future singles, "Work", "Shabba” and assisted in sonically shaping the majority of the TrapLord Album.<ref name="nadeska"/> Caines draws her inspiration from artists like [[Eve (rapper)|Eve]] and producers like No ID.<ref name="nostro" />

In 2014, she released her debut single and Video, "Whiteline".<ref>{{cite web|last1=Hurdle|first1=Gregston|title=Crystal Caines ft. Ferg "Whiteline" Video|url=http://massappeal.com/crystal-caines-ft-aap-ferg-whiteline-video/|website=massappeal.com|accessdate=November 30, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141204132636/http://massappeal.com/crystal-caines-ft-aap-ferg-whiteline-video/|archive-date=December 4, 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>
Then went on to produce for other artist such as Bia, [[Ro James]], Johnny Cinco, [[Smoke DZA |DZA]], Abby Jasmine, Jack Harlow, Bryson Tiller, Leeky Bandz and more.
