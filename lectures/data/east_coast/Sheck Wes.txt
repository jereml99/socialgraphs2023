'''Khadimou Rassoul Cheikh Fall''' (born September 10, 1998), known professionally as '''Sheck Wes''', is an  <!-- do NOT add Senegalese, ethnicity does not belong in the lead section per [[MOS:BLPLEAD]] -->American rapper and singer. He is best known for his 2017 song "[[Mo Bamba (song)|Mo Bamba]]", which became popular in 2018.<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/09/04/arts/music/sheck-wes-mo-bamba.html|title=The Story of 'Mo Bamba': How a SoundCloud Rap Track Goes Viral |last=Coscarelli |first=Joe |newspaper=The New York Times |access-date=September 6, 2018 |date=September 4, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite AV media |date=August 13, 2018 |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C5-VimMlxhY |title=Mo Bamba Explains How Sheck Wes' Song Was Made |publisher=Complex News |via=[[YouTube]] |access-date=May 8, 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210508162517/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C5-VimMlxhY |archive-date=May 8, 2021 |url-status=live}}</ref>

In February 2018, Wes signed signed a joint record deal with [[Travis Scott]]'s [[Cactus Jack Records|Cactus Jack]] and [[Kanye West]]'s [[GOOD Music]], under the aegis of [[Interscope Records]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://pigeonsandplanes.com/interviews/2018/02/sheck-wes-interview|title=The Fascinating Rise of Sheck Wes: "I'm a Mudboy, I Came From Nothing" |last=Louis |first=Nate |website=Pigeons and Planes |access-date=September 6, 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://pitchfork.com/features/rising/sheck-wes-wants-to-be-the-first-rappermodelnba-starnobel-prize-winner-in-history/ |title=Sheck Wes Wants to Be the First Rapper/Model/NBA Star/Nobel Prize Winner in History |last=Aku |first=Timmhotep |website=Pitchfork |access-date=September 6, 2018}}</ref> Wes has been regarded as a [[one-hit wonder]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=Stewart |first=Owen |title=Trending down: Sheck Wes |url=https://hhsmedia.com/27759/uncategorized/trending-down-sheck-wes/ |access-date=2023-01-04 |website=HHS Media}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title="5 Recent Hip-Hop One Hit Wonders" |url=https://www.boomplay.com/buzz/3776727}}</ref>

== Early life ==
Khadimou Rassoul Cheikh Fall was born on September 10, 1998, in the [[Harlem]] neighborhood of [[New York City]] to parents of [[Senegal]]ese descent. Fall was raised in a Muslim household.<ref name="genius.com">{{Cite web|url=https://genius.com/a/sheck-wes-says-mo-bamba-was-partially-inspired-by-an-islamic-saint|title=Sheck Wes Says "Mo Bamba" Was Partially Inspired By An Islamic Saint|date=February 14, 2019|website=Genius}}</ref> At age five, he and his mother, Ella Kingsland, moved to [[Milwaukee]], [[Wisconsin]], where he lived for nine years. At 14, he moved back to New York.<ref name="MarcuzFader">{{cite web |url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/07/03/sheck-wes-mo-bamba-interview |title=Live Sheck Wes}}</ref> Fall began making music at age 11, to relieve stress due to his troubled childhood as he was raised in a hostile environment. While in high school, basketball became a major extracurricular pursuit, but he attracted interest from a fashion talent scout, leading him to skip a playoff game to participate in the [[Madison Square Garden]] unveiling of the [[Yeezy Season]] 3 collection.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/sheck-wes-mn0003710905/biography|title=Sheck Wes {{!}} Biography & History|website=AllMusic|language=en-us|access-date=September 22, 2019}}</ref>

== Music career ==
=== 2018–2019: ''Mudboy'' ===
On February 2, 2018, Wes signed to [[Kanye West]]'s [[GOOD Music]] and [[Travis Scott]]'s [[Cactus Jack Records]] label alongside Interscope.<ref>{{cite tweet |last=Wes |first=Sheck |author-link=Sheck Wes |user=sheckwes |number=959193336455327745 |date=February 1, 2018 |title=I signed a record deal with Kanye West and Travis Scott under the INTERSCOPE Imprint. I’m very excited . I hope you are too. |language=en |access-date=May 8, 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220209225928/https://twitter.com/sheckwes/status/959193336455327745 |archive-date=February 9, 2022 |url-status=live}}</ref> Wes made a guest appearance on Scott's third studio album ''[[Astroworld (album)|Astroworld]]'' on the song "[[No Bystanders]]" alongside American rapper [[Juice Wrld]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.thefader.com/2018/08/03/travis-scott-astroworld-credits|title=Here are the full album credits for Travis Scott's ASTROWORLD|last=Renshaw|first=David|website=[[The Fader]]|date=August 3, 2018|access-date=February 27, 2020}}</ref> On October 2, Wes announced his debut studio album titled ''[[Mudboy (album)|Mudboy]]'' which was released just three days after.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/sheck-wes-reveals-release-date-for-new-album-mudboy/|title=Sheck Wes Reveals Release Date for New Album MUDBOY|last=Strauss|first=Matthew|website=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]]|date=October 2, 2018|access-date=February 27, 2020}}</ref> On March 19, Wes released a new single under the Cactus Jack label called YKTS.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/do-that-single/1359003415|title=Do That – Single by Sheck Wes on Apple Music|via=[[Apple Music]]|date=March 19, 2018|access-date=February 27, 2020}}</ref>

=== 2019–2021: ''Hell 2 Paradise'' ===
Following the release of ''Mudboy'', Wes announced that his second studio album was in the works.<ref>{{cite tweet |last=Wes |first=Sheck |author-link=Sheck Wes |user=sheckwes |number=1239624547529351169 |date=March 16, 2020 |title=We announced HELL 2 PARADISE in 2018 , a day after MUDBOY , it’s the second album, dgaf about what’s happening or the outcome , it’s just gonna outdrum your eardrum, |language=en |access-date=May 8, 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220225014551/https://twitter.com/sheckwes/status/1239624547529351169 |archive-date=February 25, 2022 |url-status=live}}</ref>

In January 2019, Wes was part of the opening act for [[Eminem]]'s concert in [[Hawaii]] alongside fellow American rapper [[Logic (rapper)|Logic]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/eminem-taps-logic-and-sheck-wes-to-open-at-hawaii-concert-news.70566.html?_amp|title=Eminem Taps Logic & Sheck Wes To Open At Hawaii Concert|last=Findly|first=Mitch|website=HotNewHipHop|date=January 29, 2019|access-date=February 27, 2020}}</ref> On July 18, Wes released the singles "Sadio Mane",<ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/sadio-man%C3%A9-ynwa-single/1472610536|title=Sadio Mané (YNWA) – Single by Sheck Wes on Apple Music|via=[[Apple Music]]|date=July 18, 2019|access-date=February 27, 2020}}</ref> and "Losing My Mind".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/losing-my-mind-single/1472609772|title=Losing My Mind – Single|via=[[Apple Music]]|date=July 18, 2019|access-date=February 27, 2020}}</ref> The following day on July 19, he appeared on Chase B and [[Young Thug]]'s song "Mayday". On October 31, he released the song "YKTS" which was accompanied by a music video. On December 27, Wes made a guest appearance on the Cactus Jack compilation album ''[[JackBoys]]'' on the song "[[Gang Gang (JackBoys and Sheck Wes song)|Gang Gang]]" also featuring [[Don Toliver]], [[Travis Scott]], and Luxury Tax 50.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/jackboys/1492785997|title=JACKBOYS by JACKBOYS & Travis Scott on Apple Music|via=[[Apple Music]]|date=December 27, 2019|access-date=February 27, 2020}}</ref>

On November 6, 2020, Wes released the single "Rich One Day" and released the single "#BeenBallin" on November 19, 2020.

Wes released "GFU" with record label [[YSL Records]] and American rapper Yak Gotti, featuring American rapper [[Yung Kayo]], which serves as the second single from the label's compilation album, ''[[Slime Language 2]]'', on January 29, 2021.

== Legal issues ==
On January 31, 2019, Ridge Productions claimed Wes had refused to pay for the video for his forthcoming single "Mudboy". Ridge Productions posted the original edit of the video, with substituted audio mocking Wes and his crew for reneging on their agreement.<ref>{{cite news |last=Williams |first=Aaron |date=February 1, 2019 |title=Sheck Wes Gets Trolled By A Video Producer Who Says Sheck Didn't Pay Him For Shooting The 'Gmail' Video |url= https://uproxx.com/hiphop/sheck-wes-gmail-video-trolled-ridge-production |access-date=February 1, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Beschizza |first=Rob |date=February 1, 2019 |title=Production company posts funny remix after rapper refuses to pay for music video |url=https://boingboing.net/2019/02/01/production-company-posts-funny.html |access-date=February 1, 2019}}</ref>

In February 2019, Wes was caught on video stalking his former partner, singer [[Justine Skye]]. After Skye came out publicly about Wes abusing her, former classmates of Wes' high school stated that he did abuse other women before at school. In April 2019, he dated social media personality India Love. In June 2019, Love posted and deleted a picture of a black eye that hinted Wes abused her after their breakup. Later on in the year, they rekindled their relationship. In June 2020, they had a public dispute. Love even hinted that Wes abused her after she posted a picture with a black eye with the caption "This is why we are over Sheck, stop telling people I cheated on you". She later took the post down.<ref name=":0">{{Cite news |url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2019/feb/12/rapper-sheck-wes-accused-of-stalking-and-domestic-violence |title=Rapper Sheck Wes accused of stalking and domestic violence |last=Beaumont-Thomas |first=Ben |date=February 12, 2019 |work=The Guardian |access-date=February 17, 2019 |language=en-GB |issn=0261-3077}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://metro.co.uk/2019/02/12/sheck-wes-justine-skye-accused-abusing-8549014/ |title=Who is Sheck Wes, whom Justine Skye has accused of abusing her? |date=February 12, 2019 |website=Metro |language=en |access-date=February 17, 2019}}</ref> Wes was removed from an advertising campaign for [[Major League Soccer]] in response to the allegations.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.vibe.com/2019/02/sheck-wes-mls-campaign-removed |title=Sheck Wes Pulled From Campaign After Abuse Allegations |date=February 14, 2019 |website=Vibe |language=en |access-date=February 17, 2019}}</ref> Skye was subsequently reported to have requested a [[restraining order]] against Wes.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.spin.com/2019/02/justine-skye-sheck-wes-restraining-order/ |title=Justine Skye Requests Restraining Order Against Sheck Wes [UPDATE] |date=February 15, 2019 |website=Spin |access-date=February 17, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://theblast.com/justine-skye-restraining-order-sheck-wes/ |title=Singer Justine Skye Files Restraining Order Against Sheck Wes |last1=Trock |first1=Gary |last2=Walters |first2=Liz |date=February 15, 2019 |website=The Blast |language=en-US |access-date=February 17, 2019}}</ref> Wes has denied the allegations and no charges were ever brought forward.<ref name=":0" /> Skye's one-time boyfriend, rapper [[GoldLink]], has repeatedly insulted and criticized Wes publicly over the abuse allegations.<ref name=Complex>{{cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/goldlink-disses-sheck-wes-new-album|title=GoldLink Disses Sheck Wes on "White Walls," Announces New Album|first=FNR|last=Tigg|work=Complex|date=June 9, 2021|access-date=November 18, 2021}}</ref>

On May 21, 2020, Wes was arrested on drug and gun charges in New York.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Sheck Wes Arrested In New York|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/sheck-wes-arrested-in-new-york-news.110881.html|access-date=July 5, 2020|website=HotNewHipHop|date=May 21, 2020}}</ref> He was later released.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Sheck Wes Has Been Released Following Arrest|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/sheck-wes-has-been-released-following-arrest-news.110896.html|access-date=July 5, 2020|website=HotNewHipHop|date=May 21, 2020}}</ref>
