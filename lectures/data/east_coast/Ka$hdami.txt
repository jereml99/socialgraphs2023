'''David Wallace III''' (born December 16, 2004), known professionally as '''Kashdami''' (often stylized as '''KA$HDAMI'''), is an American rapper from Prince George’s County, Maryland. He rose to popularity with his songs "Reparations", "Look N The Mirror" and "14" with [[tana (rapper)|tana]] which gained attraction on [[SoundCloud]] and [[TikTok]]. He released his first major label mixtape ''Epiphany'' in June 2021<ref name="Best New Artists">{{Cite web|title=Best New Artists|url=https://www.complex.com/pigeons-and-planes/best-new-artists-may-2021/|access-date=2021-11-26|website=Complex|language=en}}</ref>

== Early life ==
David Wallace III was born on December 16, 2004, in Las Vegas, Nevada, and was raised by his parents. He gained early experience with music through his father, who owned a studio and worked in the industry. He died when Wallace was seven. His family moved to [[Maryland]] around this time.<ref name="Best New Artists"/> Wallace has a twin sister.<ref>{{Citation|title=Buzzing 16 Year Old Rapper KA$HDAMI Talks "Reparations" & "Kappin Up", Mexiko Dro, Since99|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kGWZiSLN6pk|language=en|access-date=2021-11-26}}</ref>

== Career ==
===2018: Beginnings===
Wallace started making music at the age of eight through the now-defunct video chat app [[ooVoo]]. At thirteen, he bought a microphone and recorded on his mother's computer.<ref>{{Citation|title=KA$HDAMI on Buying House at 16, Losing Pi'erre Bourne's Beats, Mexikodro (Interview)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X753FcpE9Qc|language=en|access-date=2021-11-26}}</ref> He released the song "Kappin Up" and ''Findin' Out'' in 2018 but became demotivated and took a break. Wallace returned when "Kappin Up" became popular on TikTok in 2020, with the help of a music video by [[WorldStarHipHop]].<ref name="break">{{Cite web|last=Seabrook III|first=Robby |title=The Break Presents - Ka$hdami|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/kashdami-interview-the-break/|access-date=2021-11-26|website=XXL Mag|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|title=KA$HDAMI - "Kappin Up" (Official Music Video - WSHH Exclusive)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZLLlcEsybl0|language=en|access-date=2021-11-26}}</ref>

===2020: Breakthrough===
With newfound motivation, Wallace released the mixtapes #''KashDontMiss'' and ''16'' in 2020, which grew his online fanbase. In January 2021, his breakout single "Reparations!" released, which gained popularity on various platforms including [[TikTok]] and [[SoundCloud]]. His follow-up single "Look N The Mirror!" did similarly well. Wallace signed a deal with [[Republic Records]] in May 2021.<ref name=break/>

=== 2021–present: ''Epiphany'', ''HYPERNOVA'', ''WORLD DAMINATION'', and ''18'' ===
On June 2, 2021, the mixtape ''Epiphany'' released through Republic Records, with features from [[tana (rapper)|tana]], [[yvngxchris]], [[SSGKobe]], Riovaz and D'mari Harris<ref name="Best New Artists"/><ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-06-04|title=6 Albums Out This Week You Should Listen to Now|url=https://pitchfork.com/news/6-new-albums-you-should-listen-to-now-japanese-breakfast-loraine-james-rostam-and-more/|access-date=2021-11-29|website=Pitchfork|language=en-US}}</ref> Wallace and fellow rapper [[Baby Santana|tana]], formerly BabySantana, featured on the [[Lyrical Lemonade]] YouTube channel with their track "14". on July 20, 2021.<ref>{{Citation|title=BabySantana & KA$HDAMI - 14 (Directed by Cole Bennett)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Lubd7UtmBtE|language=en|access-date=2021-11-29}}</ref> In September, Wallace released the single "Public", and followed it up with "Intermission" in October.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-10-18|title=KA$HDAMI Drops Off His Latest Single "Intermission"|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/kashdami-drops-off-his-latest-single-intermission-new-song.1993003.html|access-date=2021-11-29|website=HotNewHipHop|language=en}}</ref> They were the lead singles to his next mixtape ''Hypernova'', it was released on November 12, 2021, with a sole feature from [[Trippie Redd]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-11-20|title=KA$HDAMI keeps winning on new album 'hypernova'|url=https://ourgenerationmusic.com/featured/kahdami-keeps-winning-on-new-album-hypernova/|access-date=2021-11-26|website=Our Generation Music|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2021-11-13|title=KA$HDAMI Shares 11-Track Album "HYPERNOVA." Including Trippie Redd Feature|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/kashdami-shares-11-track-album-hypernova-including-trippie-redd-feature-new-mixtape.122173.html|access-date=2021-11-26|website=HotNewHipHop|language=en}}</ref> 

In March, Wallace released the single "posed2be" and followed it with "Phoenix" in April. He Later released "Famine" with XLOVCLO and then released the final single of his album WORLD DAMINATION with "komission" on September 9. A week later, WORLD DAMINATION was released on September 16, 2022. WORLD DAMINATION features the following artists: [[midwxst]], XLOVCLO, NoCap, ilyfall and [[Slump6s]]. After had releasing a slew of singles off SoundCloud, on December 16, 2022, Wallace released an EP titled 18. [[Matt Ox]] serves as the EP's sole feature.
