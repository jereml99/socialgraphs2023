'''Myles Mills'''<ref>{{cite web|title=Skizzy Mars Named 'Next Up' Artist, Preps for Minneapolis Showcase|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/7468414/skizzy-mars-next-up-minneapolis|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|publisher=[[Prometheus Global Media]]|access-date=November 27, 2016|date=August 15, 2016}}</ref> (born June 8, 1993),<ref>{{cite web|title=Skizzy Mars (Biography & History)|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/skizzy-mars-mn0003202498/biography|website=[[AllMusic]]|publisher=[[All Media Network]]|access-date=November 27, 2016}}</ref> better known by his stage name '''Skizzy Mars''', is an American [[rapper]], songwriter, and record producer from [[Harlem, New York]].<ref name="Diep">{{cite web|last1=Diep|first1=Eric|title=Skizzy Mars Wants to Redefine the Sound of New York City Hip-Hop|url=http://www.villagevoice.com/music/skizzy-mars-wants-to-redefine-the-sound-of-new-york-city-hip-hop-7958409|website=[[The Village Voice]]|publisher=Village Voice, LLC.|access-date=November 27, 2016|date=December 10, 2015}}</ref>

==Early life==
Mills was born and raised in [[Harlem, New York]] to his father, a therapist, and his mother, who operated two daycare centers in [[Manhattan]].<ref name="Diep"/> He attended an all-boys school [[St. Bernard's School]] during his elementary years and later graduated from the [[Browning School]]. Mills attended [[Union College]] to originally pursue a [[sports journalism]] major,<ref name="Diep"/> but he [[dropped out]] ten weeks after starting classes to begin his rapping career.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Gase|first1=Zach|title=Skizzy Mars details the life of a young rapper|url=http://national.suntimes.com/national-music/7/72/1771685/skizzy-mars-details-life-young-rapper/|website=Sun Times National|access-date=November 28, 2016|date=September 4, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Interview: Skizzy Mars Talks Indie Rock, College, and His Favorite Rapper|url=http://pigeonsandplanes.com/in-depth/2012/02/interview-skizzy-mars-talks-indie-rock-college-and-his-favorite-rapper|website=PigeonsandPlanes|publisher=Complex Media Inc.|access-date=November 27, 2016|date=February 8, 2012}}</ref>
