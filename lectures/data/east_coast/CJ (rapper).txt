NME|website=[[NME]]|date=January 13, 2021}}</ref>), better known by his stage name '''CJ''', is an American rapper from [[Staten Island]], [[New York City|New York]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.59843/title.Draco-says-pop-smokes-absence-inspired-whoopty-he-wants-cardi-b-to-power-the-remix|title=CJ Says Pop Smoke's Absence Inspired 'Whoopty' — & He Wants Cardi B To Power The Remix|work=HipHopDX|date=January 2021}}</ref> He is best known for his 2020 breakout single "[[Whoopty]]", which peaked at number 3 on the [[UK Singles Chart]]<ref name=offcharts>[https://www.officialcharts.com/artist/58749/cj/ Official Charts: CJ Singles on UK Singles Chart]</ref> and at number 10 on the US [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]].<ref>{{Cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/lyrics/9507958/cj-whoopty-lyrics/|title=Here Are the Lyrics to CJ's 'Whoopty'|magazine=Billboard|date=January 7, 2021}}</ref> The song was later included on his debut [[Extended play|EP]], ''Loyalty over Royalty'', released in February 2021.

== Career ==
Soriano began rapping as a hobby at the age of 9, and began recording and uploading songs to [[YouTube]] and [[SoundCloud]] at the age of 14.<ref name="XXL">{{cite web|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/Draco-interview-the-break/|title=The Break Presents: Draco|website=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]|last=Seabrook III|first=Robby|date=November 25, 2020|accessdate=November 27, 2020}}{{Dead link|date=June 2023 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

In July 2020, Soriano [[Independent music|self-released]] his first commercial breakthrough single, titled "[[Whoopty]]". The song contains a sample from [[Arijit Singh]]'s "Sanam Re", with CJ rapping over the beat that he found on YouTube (later the rights were cleared). The song peaked at number 10 on the US [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] in February 2021, and the UK top three.<ref name="spotify">{{cite web|url=https://open.spotify.com/artist/7arQA31aZVS8yS6zUveWzb|title=CJ Bio|work=Spotify}}</ref> He also reached the top of the ''Billboard'' Emerging Artists chart.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/business/chart-beat/9506993/cj-tops-emerging-artists-chart/|title=CJ Hits No. 1 on Emerging Artists Chart as 'Whoopty' Reaches the Hot 100's Top 40|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|author=Zellner, Xander|date=January 5, 2021}}</ref> He later signed a record deal with [[Warner Records]] and his uncle, record executive, James Cruz, on his record label, called Cruz Control Entertainment.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/9510088/cj-whoopty-warner-records-interview/|title=Whoopty' Star CJ Announces Partnership With Warner Records, Discusses New Songs & Cardi B Co-Sign: Exclusive|magazine=Billboard|date=January 13, 2021}}</ref> He stated that various labels reached out to him, however, Warner had the "best situation".<ref name="Bop">{{cite web|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.60239/title.whoopty-rapper-cj-strikes-with-follow-up-bop-single-after-signing-major-label-deal|title='Whoopty' Rapper CJ Strikes With Follow-Up 'BOP' Single After Signing Major Label Deal|work=[[HipHopDX]]|last=Walker|first=Joe|date=January 26, 2020|accessdate=January 26, 2021}}</ref> On January 26, 2021, CJ released the single, titled "Bop", the follow-up to "Whoopty".<ref name="Bop" /> His debut [[extended play]], titled ''Loyalty Over Royalty'' was released on February 19, 2021. It was executive produced by [[French Montana]].<ref name="EP">{{cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/cjs-debut-project-loyalty-over-royalty-gets-release-date-news.126571.html%3f|title=CJ's Debut Project "Loyalty Over Royalty" Gets Release Date|work=HotNewHipHop|last=Findlay|first=Mitch|date=February 12, 2021|accessdate=February 17, 2021}}</ref>

== Influences ==
His music influences include [[50 Cent]], [[Jay-Z]], [[Wu-Tang Clan]], and [[Pop Smoke]].<ref name="spotify" />

== Personal life ==
He is of Puerto Rican descent.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/lyrics/9507958/cj-whoopty-lyrics|title=Here Are the Lyrics to Draco's 'Choppa Town Remix'|magazine=Billboard|author=Mamo, Heran|date=January 7, 2021}}</ref>
