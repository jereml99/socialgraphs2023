'''Christopher Saint Victor''' (born November 16, 1995), better known by his stage name '''Flipp Dinero''', is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He is best known for his 2018 single "Leave Me Alone", which is certified 4× platinum by the [[Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA]], and has since received over 600 million plays on [[Spotify]]. Being from [[Brooklyn]], New York, he was later able to connect with another Brooklyn, New York rapper, [[Joey Badass]] who later co-signed him along with the record labels [[Cinematic Music Group]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/flipp-dinero-mn0003748030/biography|title=Artist Biography|last=Thomas|first=Fred|website=[[AllMusic]]|access-date=February 8, 2020}}</ref> [[Epic Records]], and [[We the Best Music Group|We the Best Music group]].<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=http://thesource.com/2018/08/29/flipp-dinero-signs-deal/|title=Flipp Dinero Signs Deal with We The Best, Epic Records & Cinematic Music Group|date=August 29, 2018|website=The Source|access-date=September 28, 2018}}</ref>

In 2018, Dinero gained recognition when football wide-receiver [[Odell Beckham Jr.]] danced to "[[Leave Me Alone (Flipp Dinero song)|Leave Me Alone]]" and when [[National Basketball Association|NBA]] champion [[Jordan Bell]] rapping to the song on [[Instagram]] Causing the song to go viral.<ref name="bb23">{{Cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8466763/flipp-dinero-leave-me-alone-song-tory-lanez-interview|title=Flipp Dinero on How His Haitian Background Shaped His Artistry & What He Learned From Touring With 'Big Brother' Tory Lanez|magazine=Billboard|access-date=September 28, 2018}}</ref> The song peaked at 20 on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] in 2019<ref name="Hot 1003">{{cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/biz/search/charts?f[0]=ts_chart_artistname%3AFlipp%20Dinero&f[1]=itm_field_chart_id%3AHot%20100&f[2]=ss_bb_type%3Achart_item&type=2&artist=Flipp%20Dinero|title=Chart Search: Flipp Dinero|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=February 5, 2019}}</ref> and later went [[triple platinum]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.riaa.com/gold-platinum/|title=Gold & Platinum|website=RIAA}}</ref>

==Early life==
Flipp Dinero is from the [[Canarsie, Brooklyn|Canarsie]] neighborhood of Brooklyn and of [[Haitians|Haitian]] descent. He was raised in a Christian home and grew up attending private school in New York.<ref name=":4">{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XO7vAXYIKTg&t=1s|title=Flipp Dinero Talks Joey Bada$$ & Pressure To Follow Up "Leave Me Alone"|date=November 27, 2019|website=[[YouTube]]|access-date=February 8, 2020}}</ref>

== Career ==
Flipp Dinero's music combines R&B and gospel singing with rapping, hip-hop, and trap music. His music career began in 2016 with support of his brother, Lows, a sound engineer. Lows promoted Flipp's music and connected him with other artists.<ref name=":72">{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y_zmH2UI9WM|title=Flipp Dinero Talks About Being Versatile|date=May 31, 2018|website=YouTube|publisher=All Def Music|access-date=February 10, 2020}}</ref> During this time, Flipp Dinero received early recognition from [[Brooklyn|Brooklyn-based]] rapper [[Joey Badass]] who had heard his tracks, Smoke2This, I Do, and others.<ref name=":72" /> Flipp Dinero signed with Cinematic Music Group that summer of 2016<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/flipp-dinero-mn0003748030/biography|title=Flipp Dinero – Biography & History|website=AllMusic|access-date=September 28, 2018}}</ref><ref name="bb23" /> and then later signed with record labels, Epic Records and [[DJ Khaled|DJ Khaled's]] We the Best Music Group.

In 2017, Flipp Dinero was featured five times on Spotify's popular hip-hop 'Most Necessary' playlist. Later in 2017, Flipp made his first performance on Complex, with his single "I do." This led to the release of his debut EP titled ''The Guala Way'' which was released in June 2017 and which reached over 2 million streams.<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Goddard|first=Kevin|date=June 21, 2017|title=The Guala Way|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/flipp-dinero-the-guala-way-new-mixtape.117291.html|journal=HotNewHipHop - the #1 Source for Hip-hop Songs, Mixtapes, Video, News|via=HNHH - HotNewHipHop}}</ref>

With the help of his brother, Flipp Dinero recorded and released his hit song, "[[Leave Me Alone (Flipp Dinero song)|Leave Me Alone]]" in 2018. The song was written as an expression of his troubles with a woman that would not leave him alone.<ref name=":6">{{cite magazine|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/hip-hop/8482694/flipp-dinero-interview-leave-me-alone|title='Leave Me Alone' Rapper Flipp Dinero On Getting a Drake Co-Sign, Working With DJ In 2019, Flipp Dinero was featured on hip-hop media company [[XXL (magazine)|XXL]] 2019 Freshman class after performing his freestyle "What I do".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/news/2019/12/blueface-polo-g-rico-nasty-write-rhymes-freestyle/|title=Blueface, Polo G, Rico Nasty and More Choose Writing Rhymes or Freestyling|date=December 5, 2019|website=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]|publisher=XXL Magazine|access-date=February 16, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zfffx423xY4|title=Flipp Dinero Freestyle - What I Do|date=October 19, 2018|website=[[YouTube]]|publisher=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]|access-date=February 16, 2020}}</ref> Later in 2019, Flipp released his album Love for Guala,<ref name=":12">{{Cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/stream-flipp-dineros-debut-album-love-for-guala-ft-kodak-black-lil-baby-and-more-new-mixtape.119810.html|title=Stream Flipp Dinero's Debut Album "LOVE FOR GUALA" Ft. Flipp includes the word "Guala" into his album titles. The lifestyle term is a reference to Gods, Unique, Accolade, Life, Acquired and holds great meaning to him.<ref name=":4" />
