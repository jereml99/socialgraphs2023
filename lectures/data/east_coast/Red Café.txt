'''Jermaine Alfred Denny'''<ref name="Watkins" /><ref name="Konvict">[http://www.redcafe.konvictmuzik.net/ Red cafe Profile], Konvict Music. Accessed October 10, 2011.</ref> (born September 19, 1976), better known by his stage name '''Red Café''', is a [[Guyanese-American]] rapper. At various points in his career, he was signed to [[Fabolous]]' Street Family Records, [[Sean Combs|Puff Daddy]]'s [[Bad Boy Records]], [[Akon]]'s [[Konvict Muzik]], [[Mack 10]]'s [[Hoo-Bangin Records]], and [[Trackmasters]]' namesake record label.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.discogs.com/artist/212524-Red-Cafe|title = Red Cafe|website = [[Discogs]]}}</ref> He has also ghostwritten for many popular rappers over the years.<ref name="Rawlins" />

== Personal life ==
Red Café is an [[East Coast hip hop|East Coast]] rapper, and has been closely identified with [[Brooklyn]], New York City throughout his career.<ref name="Cordor">Cordor, Cyril. {{AllMusic|class=artist|id=p697019}}, Macrovision Corporation Accessed October 10, 2011.</ref> His nickname "Red" was his father's nickname; "Red Café" is what was stamped on bags of his [[Illegal drug trade|drug merchandise]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.vibe.com/2011/08/red-cafes-journey-majors-pt1-getting-shot-neck-prison-his-1st-deal-violator/|title=Red Café's Journey To The Majors Pt.1: Getting Shot In The Neck, Prison, His 1st Deal With Violator|work=Vibe|date=August 3, 2011|access-date=March 25, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url= http://www.sixshot.com/articles/6524/|title= Who is Red Café?|work= Sixshot.com|year= 2006|archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20061020113131/http://www.sixshot.com/articles/6524/|archive-date= October 20, 2006|access-date= December 25, 2011}}</ref> Café stated that he was born in [[Guyana]], but he and his family moved to Brooklyn at the age of six.<ref name="vibe.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.vibe.com/article/red-caf%C3%A9s-journey-majors-pt1-getting-shot-neck-prison-his-1st-deal-violator |title=Red Café's Journey to the Majors Pt.1: Getting Shot in the Neck, Prison, His 1st Deal with Violator &#124; Vibe |access-date=September 26, 2014 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141006113500/http://www.vibe.com/article/red-caf%C3%A9s-journey-majors-pt1-getting-shot-neck-prison-his-1st-deal-violator |archive-date=October 6, 2014 }}</ref>

He was imprisoned for four years starting in 1992.<ref name="vibe.com" />

He released an album with [[DJ Envy]], ''The Co-Op'', in 2007.<ref name="Cordor" /> [[Remy Ma]] was featured on the album. Red Cafe made an original song, "Stick'm" for the video game ''[[Grand Theft Auto IV]]''.

On March 17, 2011, Café released his retail mixtape, ''Above the Cloudz''. On January 13, 2012, Café released his mixtape ''Hell's Kitchen''. On December 12, 2012, Café released his mixtape ''American Psycho''. ''American Psycho'' has featurings coming from [[French Montana]], [[Chief Keef]], [[Fabolous]], [[Jeremih]], [[T-Pain]], [[Trey Songz]], [[The Game (rapper)|The Game]], and [[2 Chainz]], with production from [[Young Chop]], Reefa, Soundsmith Productions, and others.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/news/id.22191/title.red-cafe-american-psycho-mixtape-download-stream|title=Red Cafe "American Psycho" Mixtape Download & Stream|author=HipHopDX|date=December 12, 2012|work=HipHopDX|access-date=March 9, 2015}}</ref>

On February 17, 2014, Café released his mixtape ''American Psycho 2''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.datpiff.com/Red-Cafe-American-Psycho-2-mixtape.576315.html|title=Red Cafe – American Psycho 2|work=DatPiff|access-date=March 9, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hotnewhiphop.com/red-cafe-american-psycho-ii-new-mixtape.107675.html|title=Red Cafe – American Psycho II|author=Rose Lilah|work=HotNewHipHop|date=February 18, 2014|access-date=March 9, 2015}}</ref> On July 29, 2014, Café released his latest single entitled "Pretty Gang" featuring frequent collaborator and close friend [[Fabolous]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/pretty-gang-feat.-fabolous/id900280129|title=iTunes – Music – Pretty Gang (feat.
