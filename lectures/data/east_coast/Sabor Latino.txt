'''Robert Torres''', also known as '''Sabor Latino''' (born June 21, 1972) is an American [[hip hop]] artist and author. He is from [[Jamaica, Queens, New York]].<ref name="Positive hip-hop from “Sabor Latino”">{{cite news | title = Positive hip-hop from "Sabor Latino" | url = http://voxxi.com/2013/11/30/positive-hip-hop-sabor-latino/ | author = Veronique de Miguel | access-date = 11 May 2014}}</ref><ref name="Sabor Latino Makes Large Impact In The Communities As Well In The World">{{cite news | title = Sabor Latino Makes Large Impact In The Communities As Well In The World | url = http://www.hiphopempiremagazine.com/2014/01/sabor-latino-makes-large-impact-in.html | author = Vietnam Jones | access-date = 11 May 2014}}</ref><ref name="La Voz">{{cite news | publisher = La Voz | date = April 2014 | title = Sabor Latino}}</ref> Sabor Latino creates music to inspire individuals to become productive members of society.<ref name="La Voz"/><ref>{{cite news | title = Sabor Latino Goes Global In The Industry | url = http://vis4vietnam.blogspot.com/2014/04/sabor-latino-goes-global-in-hip-hop.html?m=0 | author = Vietnam Jones | access-date = 11 May 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Sabor Latino: Hip Hop Makes Large Impact in Latin Communities|work=Hip Hop Empire Magazine|pages=28–32}}</ref>

==Career==
Sabor Latino worked as a social worker for a foster care agency in [[South Jamaica, Queens]].<ref name="Positive hip-hop from “Sabor Latino”"/><ref name="Sabor Latino Makes Large Impact In The Communities As Well In The World"/><ref name="Hip-hop with a positive message"/><ref name="Biography"/><ref name="Sabor Latino quiere llegar a los jovénes con su hip hop positivo"/> He released his first album, ''Observaciones de Mi Vida, Vol. 1'' on February 28, 2013.<ref name="Observaciones de Mi Vida, Vol. 1">{{cite news | title = Observaciones de Mi Vida, Vol. 1 | url = http://www.cdbaby.com/m/cd/saborlatino | publisher = CD Baby | access-date = 11 May 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://extranews.net/inspiring-youth-through-positive-hip-hop.html|title=Inspiring youth through positive hip-hop|author=Silvia Foster-Frau|date=1 May 2014|access-date=5 July 2014}}</ref> The album was mixed by producer Lee Evans.<ref name="Positive hip-hop from “Sabor Latino”"/><ref name="Sabor Latino quiere llegar a los jovénes con su hip hop positivo"/> The experiences Sabor Latino witnessed while working for the foster care agency influenced his first album.<ref name="Biography"/><ref>{{cite news | title = El rapero latino que le canta al cambio | url = http://lavozhispanact.com/actualidad/espectaculo/9736-el-rapero-latino-que-le-canta-al-cambio | author = Alejandro Navarro | publisher = La Voz | language = es | access-date = 11 May 2014}}</ref> The album consists of eight songs and two poems that are meant to inspire children, adolescents, parents and adults to become productive members of society.<ref name="Positive hip-hop from “Sabor Latino”"/><ref name="Observaciones de Mi Vida, Vol. 1"/><ref>{{cite news | title = Caracteristicas | url = http://www.elbarriomagazine.org/#!el-barrio-magazine-latino/c2154 | publisher = El Barrio Magazine | access-date = 11 May 2014}}</ref> Sabor Latino chose to produce the album in Spanish in attempt to create a positive image in the Latin American community.<ref name="Sabor Latino quiere llegar a los jovénes con su hip hop positivo"/> In 2014, Sabor Latino was recognized by [[WQHT|Hot 97]] on its "Who's Next" list.<ref>{{cite web | url = http://whosnext.hot97.com/artist/8928 | title  = Sabor Latino | publisher = [[WQHT|Hot 97]] | access-date = 16 August 2014 }}</ref> In 2014, Sabor Latino announced the release of his autobiography titled ''Sabor Latino: My Life''.<ref name=latinola>{{cite web | url = http://latinola.com/story.php?story=12970 | title = Latino Hip Hop Artist Sabor Latino to Release Autobiography | publisher = LatinoLA | date = 22 September 2014 | access-date = 25 September 2014 }}</ref>

In November 2014, Sabor Latino released his biography, ''Sabor Latino: My Life'', which combines his personal experiences with his social-work career to inspire youth.<ref name = voxxi>{{cite web |url = http://voxxi.com/2014/09/29/sabor-latino-biography-book/ | title = 'Sabor Latino' motivates Latino youth with his intimate biography | publisher = Voxxi | access-date = 26 October 2014}}</ref> Commenting on his music career, Torres said "he still identifies as a social worker, just of a different kind than before."<ref name = brandz>{{cite web | url =http://extranews.net/inspiring-youth-through-positive-hip-hop.html | title = Inspiring youth through positive hip-hop | publisher = Extra News| access-date = 26 October 2014}}</ref> He announced that his single, "Anything In Life Is Possible," and his second book, ''Sabor Latino's Greatest Poems & Songs'', would be released in 2016.<ref>{{cite web| url=http://www.yoraps.com/next2blow1.php?subaction=showfull&id=1430323044&archive=&start_from=&ucat=4&| title=Sabor Latino - My Life| date=April 29, 2015| publisher=YoRaps| access-date=June 4, 2015}}</ref> His second book was eventually titled "Mis Grandes Canciones y Poemas" and was released in 2017; his third, titled "125 Quotes Gathered from my Life Observations" was released in 2018. His fourth book, "My alma mater Lehman College" is set for publication in February 2019.<ref name=books>{{cite news |last1=Mohamed |first1=Carlotta |title=Jamaica hip-hop artist's latest book stresses the importance of college education |url=https://www.timesledger.com/stories/2019/2/robertotorres_2019_01_11_q.html |access-date=24 January 2019 |date=14 January 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Featured Interview With Roberto Torres |url=https://bookreadermagazine.com/featured-author-roberto-torres-4/ |access-date=24 January 2019 |publisher=Book Reader Magazine}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Priolo |first1=Teresa |title=Sabor Latino Hip Hop Spreads Positive Messages about Education |url=http://amp.fox5ny.com/news/sabor-latino-hip-hop-positive-messages |access-date=24 January 2019 |publisher=Fox 5 NY |date=8 January 2019}}</ref> Sabor Latino released his second album “Observaciones de mi Vida Vol. 2,” in October 2021.<ref name="South Jamaica hip-hop artist releases second album to inspire and uplift youth in the community">{{cite web |last1=Tommaselli |first1=Benedetta |title=South Jamaica hip-hop artist releases second album to inspire and uplift youth in the community |url=https://qns.com/2021/11/south-jamaica-hip-hop-artist-releases-second-album-to-inspire-and-uplift-youth-in-the-community/ |website=qns.com |access-date=4 February 2022}}</ref>
