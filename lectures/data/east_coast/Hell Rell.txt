'''Durrell Mohammad''' (born May 3, 1979), better known by his stage name '''Hell Rell''', is an American rapper.

==Career==

In 2007, he signed with [[Koch Records]] and began working on his debut album, ''[[For the Hell of It]]''. The album reached No. 5 on both the [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'']] Top Independent Albums and Top Rap Albums chart, and No. 10 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums.<ref>.[http://www.billboard.com/#/album/diplomats-present-hell-rell/for-the-hell-of-it/778668 For the Hell of It (2006) Chart history]. ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]''. Retrieved on August 6, 2009.</ref>
