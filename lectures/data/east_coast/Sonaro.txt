'''Christopher Darnell Cook''' (born October 5, 1988), professionally known as '''Sonaro''', is an American [[music producer]] from [[Far Rockaway, Queens|Far Rockaway]], [[New York (state)|New York]]. He is perhaps best known for producing songs for Brooklyn-bred rapper [[Fabolous]]. He has also produced for several other rappers, such as [[French Montana]], [[Rich Homie Quan|Jeezy]], [[Jadakiss]], [[Styles P]], [[Flipp Dinero]], [[Lloyd Banks]], [[Paul Cain (musician)|Paul Cain]], [[Prodigy (rapper)|Prodigy]], [[Jim Jones]], and [[Meek Mill]].<ref>http://istandardproducers.com/news/sonaro-2/</ref>

==Life and career==
===Early life and career beginnings===
Sonaro was born in [[Far Rockaway, Queens|Far Rockaway]] and raised in [[Brooklyn]]. Interested in [[music production]] from an early age, Sonaro became an in-house producer for [[Needlz]] where he learned to craft his sound.<ref>http://istandardproducers.com/news/sonaro-2/</ref> During this time, Sonaro landed his first placement on [[Stack Bundles]] ''Biddin' War Vol 2.'' hosted by [[DJ Clue]], producing the song "Rockin' it” featuring [[Jim Jones]] and [[Max B]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.datpiff.com/Stack-Bundles-Biddin-War-Vol-2-mixtape.405.html|title = Biddin War Vol 2 Mixtape by Stack Bundles Hosted by DJ Clue}}</ref> Sonaro soon followed this up with "Thang On Me" from [[Lil' Kim]]’s ''[[Ms. G.O.A.T.]]'' project, which featured [[Maino (rapper)|Maino]], [[Mister Cee]], and Sha Dash.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.datpiff.com/Lil-Kim-Ms-Goat-2-mixtape.11872.html|title = Ms. G.o.a.t. 2 Mixtape by Lil' Kim Hosted by DJ Whoo Kid}}</ref>

===Commercial Breakthrough===
On March 5, 2010, [[Fabolous]] and [[DJ Drama]] released the [[mixtape]] ''There Is No Competition 2: The Funeral Service'' which featured the Sonaro produced street single "Body Ya."<ref>{{cite web| url = http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/mixtape/id.1045/title.dj-drama-fabolous-there-is-no-competition-2-the-funeral-service| url-status = dead| archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20100307082709/http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/mixtape/id.1045/title.dj-drama-fabolous-there-is-no-competition-2-the-funeral-service| archive-date = 2010-03-07| title = DJ Drama & Fabolous - There Is No Competition 2: The Funeral Service {{!}} Download New Hip Hop Mixtapes & Rap Mixtapes {{!}} HipHopDX}}</ref> The song was quickly added to [[WQHT|Hot 97]],<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.thisis50.com/profiles/blogs/fabolous-body-ya-official-2 |title=Fabolous - Body Ya [Official Music Video]- Click Inside - ThisIs50.com |website=www.thisis50.com |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100401055307/http://www.thisis50.com/profiles/blogs/fabolous-body-ya-official-2 |archive-date=2010-04-01}}</ref> and [[Def Jam]] officially released the song on ''[[There Is No Competition 2: The Grieving Music EP]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/there-is-no-competition-2/id388674217|title = There is No Competition 2: The Grieving Music Mixtape by Fabolous|website = [[iTunes]]| date=January 2010 }}</ref> The [[Extended play|EP]] featured additionally production from Sonaro, and new contributions from [[Lex Luger]], [[Ryan Leslie]], and [[Jahlil Beats]].

Later work with [[Fabolous]] from this period includes "[[The S.O.U.L. Tape|Leaving You]]," "[[There Is No Competition 3: Death Comes in 3's|Swag Champ]],"<ref>Archived at [https://ghostarchive.org/varchive/youtube/20211208/h5g0qpt88d4 Ghostarchive]{{cbignore}} and the [https://web.archive.org/web/20120714000514/http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h5g0qpt88d4&gl=US&hl=en Wayback Machine]{{cbignore}}: {{cite AV media| url = https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h5g0qpt88d4| title = Fabolous - Swag Champ (Official Video) | website=[[YouTube]]}}{{cbignore}}</ref> "[[There Is No Competition 3: Death Comes in 3's|B.E.T.]]" featuring [[Jadakiss]] and [[Styles P]], and "[[The S.O.U.L. Tape 2|Want You Back]]" featuring [[Joe Budden]] and [[Teyana Taylor]]. Sonaro also contributed production to projects by [[Lloyd Banks]], [[Jim Jones]], Nya Lee, [[Meek Mill]], and [[French Montana]], and licensed several instrumentals for the TV show [[Love & Hip Hop]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://sonaromusic.blogspot.com/2013/11/check-out-this-x-love-hip-hop-clip-from.html|title=Sonaro: Check Out This x Love & Hip Hop Clip from Ep. 4 x Nya Lee raps to a Sonaro beat in the studio with Erica Mena #LHHNY|date=12 November 2013}}</ref>

===''The S.O.U.L. Tape 3''===
On December 25, 2013 [[Fabolous]] released ''The S.O.U.L. Tape 3'' which featured Sonaro on three songs; "Playa," "Situationships" featuring [[Mack Wilds]], and "Cuffin' Season."<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2013/12/fabolous-releases-new-mixtape-s-o-u-l-tape-3/|title = Fabolous Releases New Mixtape 'S.O.U.L. Tape 3′ - XXL}}</ref> "Cuffin' Season" was instantly added to radio and received major press from [[WQHT|Hot 97]]'s [[Funkmaster Flex]] who called it "the hottest record in the Big Apple."<ref>http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Y8bIUvz14UM/Uvl2CSa6QoI/AAAAAAAABGc/OQkg4ppPyl0/s1600/1924783_10203176406154996_552113827_n.jpg {{Bare URL image|date=March 2022}}</ref>

The official video for "Cuffin' Season"  premiered on [[Revolt (TV network)|REVOLT]] on April 25, 2014, and features Sonaro performing the chorus alongside Fabolous.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2014/04/fabolous-cuffin-season-video|title = Watch Fabolous' Video for "Cuffin' Season"|website = [[Complex Networks]]}}</ref>

===''Summertime Shootout''===
On November 26, 2015 [[Fabolous]] released ''[[Summertime Shootout]].'' The project contains four songs produced by Sonaro; "For The Summer," "Vanilla" featuring [[Rich Homie Quan]], "Motivation," and "The Plug."<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.xxlmag.com/rap-music/new-music/2015/11/stream-fabolous-new-mixtape/|title=Stream Fabolous' 'Summertime Shootout' Mixtape - XXL}}</ref>

On November 27, the [[music video]] for the Sonaro produced "The Plug" was released exclusively to [[World Star Hip Hop]] and gained over 1,800,000 views in less one day.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.worldstarhiphop.com/videos/video.php?v=wshh6ebWjcEfA93udJ3G|title = Fabolous - the Plug &#124; Video}}</ref>
