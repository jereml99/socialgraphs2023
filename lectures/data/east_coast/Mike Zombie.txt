'''William Michael Coleman''' (born July 8, 1992), professionally known as '''Mike Zombie''', is an American record producer and rapper from [[Willingboro Township, New Jersey]]. Since 2012, Coleman has been an in-house producer for [[Drake (musician)|Drake]]'s label [[OVO Sound]]. His biggest production for the label is "[[Started from the Bottom]]" in 2013. His other major production credit is "[[They Don't Love You No More]]" by [[DJ Khaled]], released a year later.

== Career ==

He is best known for the production of Drake's single "[[Started from the Bottom]]". He sampled a track titled "Ambessence piano & drones 1" by Bruno Sanfilippo & Mathias Grassow for the song. "Started from the Bottom" was the winner of "Track of the Year" at the [[2013 BET Hip Hop Awards]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bet.com/shows/hip-hop-awards/2013/nominees/track-of-the-year.html|title=Track of the Year|date=23 September 2013|work=BET.com}}</ref> The song was played during [[NBA All-Star Weekend]], [[LeBron James]]' "LeBron Face" [[Sprite (soft drink)|Sprite]] commercial", video game [[NBA 2K14]], and video game [[Assassin's Creed 4]]. The song was also nominated for "Best Rap Song" at [[56th Annual Grammy Awards|The 56th Annual Grammy Awards]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.uwishunu.com/2013/12/the-56th-grammy-awards-nominations-are-in/|title=The 56th GRAMMY Awards® Nominations Are In|first=Ashley|last=Coleman|date=December 9, 2013|website=Uwishunu - Philadelphia Blog About Things to Do, Events, Restaurants, Food, Nightlife and More}}</ref> In July 2013, BET named him one of the "top 10 young producers to watch out for".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bet.com/music/photos/2013/07/10-young-producers-on-the-rise.html#!073113-music-producer-mike-zombie|title=10 Young Producers On the Rise|date=1 August 2013|work=BET.com}}</ref> In October 2013, ''[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]'' named him one of the "25 new producers to watch for".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2013/10/best-new-rap-producers/mike-zombie|title=Mike Zombie - 25 New Producers To Watch For - Complex|date=7 October 2013|work=Complex}}</ref>
He previously collaborated with battle rapper [[Hollow Da Don]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.datpiff.com/Hollow-Da-Don-Loyalty-Is-A-Way-Of-Life-mixtape.248667.html|title=Hollow Da Don - Loyalty Is A Way Of Life|work=DatPiff}}</ref> and Hollow played an important part putting Drake in contact with Mike Zombie.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.axs.com/mike-zombie-reveals-how-he-got-signed-to-drake-s-ovo-label-20601|title=Mike Zombie reveals how he got signed to Drake's OVO Label|date=21 September 2014|work=AXS}}</ref>

Zombie released his first mixtape, ''The End of the Beginning'', on December 22, 2013. Recently he produced [[DJ Khaled]]'s song "[[They Don't Love You No More]]" featuring [[Meek Mill]], [[Rick Ross]], [[Jay-Z]] and [[French Montana]]. In the summer of 2014 he released his first music video from "The End of the Beginning" "616" on MTV Jams. On January 27, 2015 Zombie released a new project called "Rebel Without a Cause". In 2016, he dropped a few singles and two projects: "Humble Genius" (July 1, 2016) and a collaborate album with Benzi Ayo "Ea$tside Story" (October 1, 2016).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2013/09/ovo-guide/mike-zombie|title=Mike Zombie - A Guide to Who's Who in OVO - Complex|date=25 September 2013|website=Complex.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://rapgenius.com/Drake-started-from-the-bottom-lyrics#note-1566138|title=Drake – Started From The Bottom|website=Genius.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://philly360.visitphilly.com/events/2013/12/17/mike-zombie-mixtape-release-party-december-17-2013|title=Mike Zombie Mixtape Release Party - December 17, 2013|work=Philly360.visitphilly.com}}</ref> In 2019, Zombie produced a song for [[The Game (rapper)|The Game]] titled "A.I. With The Braids" featuring [[Lil Wayne]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://hiphop-n-more.com/2019/06/the-game-ai-with-the-braids-feat-lil-wayne-new-song/|title=New The Game & Lil Wayne Collaboration 'A.I. With The Braids' Surfaces Online|website=Hiphop-n-more.com|date=June 20, 2019}}</ref>
