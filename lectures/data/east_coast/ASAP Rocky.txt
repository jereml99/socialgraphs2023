'''Rakim Athelaston Mayers'''<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.augsburger-allgemeine.de/panorama/Es-wird-eng-fuer-den-US-Rapper-ASAP-Rocky-in-Stockholm-id55015861.html |title=Es wird eng für den US-Rapper ASAP Rocky in Stockholm |last=Anwar |first=André |website=Augsburger Allgemeine |language=de |access-date=July 25, 2019 |archive-date=July 25, 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190725212843/https://www.augsburger-allgemeine.de/panorama/Es-wird-eng-fuer-den-US-Rapper-ASAP-Rocky-in-Stockholm-id55015861.html |url-status=live}}</ref> (born October 3, 1988), known professionally as '''ASAP Rocky''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|eɪ|s|æ|p}} {{respell|AY|sap}}; stylized as '''A$AP Rocky'''), is an American rapper. Born and raised in Harlem, he embarked on his musical career as a member of the [[hip hop music|hip hop]] collective [[ASAP Mob]], from which he adopted his [[moniker]]. In August 2011, Rocky's single "[[Peso (song)|Peso]]" was [[music leak|leaked online]] and within weeks began receiving radio airplay.<ref name="Caramanica" /> Rocky released his debut mixtape ''[[Live. Love. ASAP|Live. Love. A$AP]]'', later that year to widespread critical acclaim. The success of what is considered his breakout project led to a joint venture record deal with [[Polo Grounds Music]] and [[Sony Music]]'s [[RCA Records]].<ref name=":1" /><ref name="Coleman" />

His debut studio album ''[[Long. Live. ASAP|Long. Live. A$AP]]'' (2013) debuted at number one on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] and was later certified [[RIAA certification|double platinum]] by the [[Recording Industry Association of America]] (RIAA). The album was preceded by Rocky's breakout single, "[[Fuckin' Problems]]" (featuring [[Drake (musician)|Drake]], [[2 Chainz]] and [[Kendrick Lamar]]), which was nominated for [[Grammy Award for Best Rap Song|Best Rap Song]] at the [[2014 Grammy Awards]]. In 2015, Rocky released his second album, titled ''[[At. Long. Last. ASAP|At. Long. Last. A$AP]]''. The album was Rocky's second consecutive album to debut at number one on the ''Billboard'' 200 and received mostly positive reviews from music critics. The album's single, "[[LSD (ASAP Rocky song)|L$D]]", was nominated for [[Grammy Award for Best Music Video|Best Music Video]] at the [[58th Annual Grammy Awards|2016 Grammy Awards]]. In 2018, Rocky released his third album, ''[[Testing (album)|Testing]]'', which peaked in the top five of several countries, including the ''Billboard'' 200.

Rocky has won a [[BET Awards|BET Award]], two [[BET Hip Hop Awards]], an [[MTV Video Music Awards Japan|MTV Video Music Award Japan]] and an [[MTVU#MTVU Woodie Awards|MTVU Woodie Award]], as well as being nominated for two [[Grammy Award]]s, six [[World Music Awards]], three [[MTV Video Music Award]]s and two [[MTV Europe Music Award]]s. Rocky has also directed [[music video]]s for himself, [[Danny Brown]], and other A$AP Mob members.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://consequenceofsound.net/2011/11/video-danny-brown-blunt-after-blunt-directed-by-asap-rocky/|title=Video: Danny Brown – "Blunt After Blunt" (directed by ASAP Rocky) – Consequence of Sound|work=Consequence of Sound|date=November 28, 2011|access-date=August 14, 2013|archive-date=September 12, 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120912084858/http://consequenceofsound.net/2011/11/video-danny-brown-blunt-after-blunt-directed-by-asap-rocky/|url-status=live}}</ref> Furthermore, he produces records under the pseudonym '''Lord Flacko'''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.factmag.com/2012/12/12/aap-rockys-long-live-aap-to-feature-production-from-clams-casino-skrillex-and-more/|title=A$AP Rocky's Long.Live.A$AP to feature production from Clams Casino, Skrillex and more – FACT Magazine: Music News, New Music.|work=FACT Magazine: Music News, New Music.|date=December 12, 2012|access-date=August 14, 2013|archive-date=August 30, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170830064627/http://www.factmag.com/2012/12/12/aap-rockys-long-live-aap-to-feature-production-from-clams-casino-skrillex-and-more/|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Career==
===2007–2011: Career beginnings===
In 2007, A$AP Rocky joined the [[ASAP Mob|A$AP Mob]] crew, a Harlem-based collective of rappers, producers, music video directors, fashion designers, and bikers who shared similar interests in music, fashion, style, and art.<ref name="hypetrak1">{{cite web |last=Huynh |first=Davis |url=http://hypetrak.com/2012/01/asap-rocky-always-strive-and-prosper/ |title=A$AP Rocky – Always $trive and Prosper |publisher=Hypetrak |access-date=July 4, 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131016011341/http://hypetrak.com/2012/01/asap-rocky-always-strive-and-prosper/ |archive-date=October 16, 2013 |url-status=dead}}</ref> It had been formed by A$AP Yams, A$AP Bari and A$AP Illz.<ref name="vvblogs">{{Cite web|url=http://blogs.villagevoice.com/music/2012/08/meet_asap_mob.php|title=Meet The A$AP Mob: Talking To Ant, Ferg, J. === Alleged assault in Hollywood ===
On April 20, 2022, A$AP Rocky was arrested by [[LAPD]] officers upon arriving to the [[Los Angeles International Airport]] after a vacation with his partner, Rihanna. He was arrested for a potential assault with a deadly weapon in a Hollywood shooting on November 6, 2021. Bail was set at $550,000 and he was released shortly after.<ref>{{Cite news |date=2022-04-21 |title=A$AP Rocky arrested in connection with shooting |language=en-GB |work=BBC News |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/entertainment-arts-61174333 |access-date=2022-04-21}}</ref>

After his arrest, detectives executed a warrant to search his home, gaining entry by using a pry bar to force the gate open. Detectives took several boxes from his home to use as evidence.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-04-20 |title=Rapper ASAP Rocky arrested at LAX in connection with 2021 shooting in Hollywood |url=https://www.latimes.com/entertainment-arts/music/story/2022-04-20/asap-rocky-detained-lax-november-2021-shooting |access-date=2022-04-21 |website=Los Angeles Times |language=en-US}}</ref> On August 15, 2022, the [[Los Angeles County District Attorney]]'s office filed charges against Rocky.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Santiago |first1=Ricardo |title=Rakim Mayers aka A$AP Rocky Charged With Assaulting Former Friend |url=https://da.lacounty.gov/media/news/rakim-mayers-aka-aap-rocky-charged-assaulting-former-friend |website=Los Angeles County District Attourney's Office |access-date=16 August 2022 |date=16 August 2022}}</ref>
