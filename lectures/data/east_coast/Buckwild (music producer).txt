'''Anthony Best''' (born March 20,<ref>{{cite tweet|user=LordFinesseDITC|author=Lord Finesse|number=843948831226105857|date=March 20, 2017|title=Wishing my brother @BUCKWILD_DITC the best on his birthday üéâüçæüçæüçæüçæü•É}}</ref> 1968)<ref>{{cite web|first=Matt|last=Jost|url=http://www.rapreviews.com/archive/2007_04_diggincrates.html|title=Buckwild :: Diggin' In The Crates - Rare Studio Masters: 1993-1997 :: Ground Floor Recordings|work=RapReviews.com|date=April 24, 2007|access-date=October 9, 2014|archive-date=September 24, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924123129/http://www.rapreviews.com/archive/2007_04_diggincrates.html|url-status=live}}</ref> professionally known as '''Buckwild''', is an American [[hip hop music|hip hop]] producer.<ref>{{cite web|first=Sean|last=Ryon|url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/album-reviews/id.1557/title.celph-titled-buckwild-nineteen-ninety-now|title=Celph-Titled & Buckwild - Nineteen Ninety Now|work=[[HipHopDX]]|date=November 3, 2010|access-date=October 9, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101105124304/http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/album-reviews/id.1557/title.celph-titled-buckwild-nineteen-ninety-now|archive-date=November 5, 2010|url-status=dead}}</ref> Hailing from [[The Bronx]] borough of [[New York City]], he is a member of [[Diggin' in the Crates Crew]], along with [[Lord Finesse]], [[Showbiz and A.G.]], [[Diamond D]], [[Fat Joe]], [[Big L (rapper)|Big L]], and [[O.C. (rapper)|O.C.]]<ref>{{cite web|first=Nate|last=Patrin|url=http://pitchfork.com/features/starter/9229-ditc/|title=Starter: D.I.T.C.|work=[[Pitchfork Media]]|date=September 27, 2013|access-date=October 9, 2014|archive-date=October 12, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141012090247/http://pitchfork.com/features/starter/9229-ditc/|url-status=live}}</ref> He has produced a number of tracks for prominent rappers in the music industry, including [[The Notorious B.I.G.]]'s "I Got a Story to Tell" and [[Black Rob]]'s "[[Whoa! (Black Rob song)|Whoa!]]".<ref>{{cite web|first=Jaeki|last=Cho|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2011/04/interview-buckwild/|title=Buckwild Tells All: The Stories Behind His Classic Records|work=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]|date=April 12, 2011|access-date=October 9, 2014|archive-date=October 15, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141015113936/http://www.complex.com/music/2011/04/interview-buckwild/|url-status=live}}</ref> In 2013, he was described by ''[[HipHopDX]]'' as "one of Hip Hop's most prolific and acclaimed producers".<ref>{{cite web|first=Sean|last=Ryon|url=http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/news/id.22599/title.buckwild-recalls-trading-samples-with-lord-finesse|title=Buckwild Recalls Trading Samples With Lord Finesse|work=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]|date=January 19, 2013|access-date=October 9, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150422211931/http://www.hiphopdx.com/index/news/id.22599/title.buckwild-recalls-trading-samples-with-lord-finesse|archive-date=April 22, 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref>

==Career==
In 2010, Buckwild released ''[[Nineteen Ninety Now]]'', a collaborative album with [[Celph Titled]].<ref>{{cite web|first=Aaron|last=Matthews|url=http://exclaim.ca/Reviews/HipHop/celph_titled_buckwild-ninety_ninety_now|title=Celph Titled & Buckwild Ninety Ninety Now|work=[[Exclaim!]]|date=October 23, 2010|access-date=October 9, 2014|archive-date=November 3, 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101103232313/http://www.exclaim.ca/Reviews/HipHop/celph_titled_buckwild-ninety_ninety_now/|url-status=live}}</ref> In 2014, he released a collaborative album with [[Meyhem Lauren]], titled ''Silk Pyramids''.<ref>{{cite web|first=Zach|last=Frydenlund|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2014/05/stream-meyhem-lauren-and-buckwilds-silk-pyramids-album|title=Premiere: Stream Meyhem Lauren and Buckwild's "Silk Pyramids" Album|work=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]|date=May 27, 2014|access-date=October 9, 2014|archive-date=October 15, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141015113212/http://www.complex.com/music/2014/05/stream-meyhem-lauren-and-buckwilds-silk-pyramids-album|url-status=live}}</ref>
