'''Saga''' is an American [[MC]] who grew up in [[Canarsie]], Brooklyn, New York<ref name="popkiller.pl">{{cite web|url=http://www.popkiller.pl/2016-06-05,saga-do-polski-chce-sie-wracac-videowywiad|title=Saga "Do Polski chce się wracać" - videowywiad - Popkiller|work=popkiller.pl}}</ref> and now resides in [[Red Hook, Brooklyn]], New York. He is from [[Italian Americans|Italian American]] and [[Puerto Ricans in the United States|Puerto Rican]] descent.<ref name="thekitchenmix.com">[http://thekitchenmix.com/saga-chapter-one-self-knowledge/ Saga, Chapter One: Self-Knowledge | The Kitchen Mix<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref> He is the son of Richard Quarantello, a former gang member, musician and writer.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.broadwayworld.com/bwwbooks/article/Former-Gang-Member-Pens-Surviving-the-Warzone-20131017|title=Former Gang Member Pens 'Surviving the Warzone'|author=BWW News Desk|work=broadwayworld.com}}</ref> 
He is a vegan of fifteen years,<ref name="thekitchenmix.com"/> and practices yoga and meditation.

==Music career==

Saga first song to ever release is "Think Of You Now" produced by close friend [[Marco Polo (producer)]]<ref name="popkiller.pl"/> who invited him on his compilation album [[:fr:The Stupendous Adventures of Marco Polo]] released on [[Duck Down Music]].

"For a long time it just felt like the music industry was just – like I was on the shore and I was just watching the ships set sail. It was such a departure from the music that I love, from the version of Hip Hop that I love."<ref name="thekitchenmix.com"/>

A few years later, Saga and producer [[Marco Polo (producer)|Marco Polo]] kept working progressively on more music without any pretensions at first but then having a full batch of tracks that became a 10 songs album called "From Out of the Shadows".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://exclaim.ca/music/article/saga-from_out_of_shadows_album_stream|title=Saga 'From Out of the Shadows' (album stream)|work=exclaim.ca}}</ref> In March 2015, The first single of this project "City Streets"<ref>{{cite web|url=http://thesource.com/2015/03/09/premiere-listen-to-sagas-city-streets-ft-roc-marciano/|title=Premiere: Listen To Saga's "City Streets" Ft. Roc Marciano|last=notupstate|date=9 March 2015|work=thesource.com}}</ref> featured [[Roc Marciano]] then the full 10 songs album came out in April 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://upcominghiphop.net/2015/06/09/album-review-from-out-of-the-shadows-ep-saga/|title=[Album Review] FROM OUT OF THE SHADOWS EP - Saga- more than a hip hop blog|date=9 June 2015|work=upcominghiphop.net}}</ref>

In May 2015, Saga released an EP titled "Saga Ghanoush" which is an hommage to Alchemist "Israeli Salad" instrumental album.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://2dopeboyz.com/2015/06/01/saga-saga-ghanoush-ep/|title=Stream Saga's 'Saga Ghanoush' EP|last=Shake|date=1 June 2015|work=2dopeboyz.com}}</ref>

Since then Saga has been releasing series of single with producers like Marco Polo, Ayatollah, Exile and French producer 20Syl.

Saga announced for 2017 an upcoming EP with Chicago producer [[Thelonious Martin]] and an album with Virginia native Grammy Award-winning producer [[Nottz]].
