'''Justina Valentine''' <!-- please do not add birth date or year without a reliable source--> is an American television host, rapper and singer from [[Passaic County, New Jersey]], best known for her singles "Candy Land"<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://thesource.com/2015/07/03/listen-as-new-jerseys-own-justina-valentine-and-fetty-wap-link-up-on-candy-land/|title=Listen As New Jersey's Own Justina Valentine And Fetty Wop Link Up On 'Candy Land'|last=Rathon|first=Rocko|date=July 3, 2015|website=The Source|access-date=July 17, 2016}}</ref> featuring rapper [[Fetty Wap]], "All The Way" and "Unbelievable".<ref name=allmusic>{{Cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/justina-valentine-mn0003423792/biography|title=Justina Valentine – Biography & History|publisher=AllMusic|access-date=July 17, 2016}}</ref><ref name=revolt>{{Cite web|url=https://revolt.tv/stories/2016/05/16/spotlight-revolt-justina-valentine-ft-futuristic-0a7f1d7046|title=Spotlight on REVOLT: Justina Valentine "All The Way" Feat. Futuristic|date=May 16, 2016|publisher=REVOLT TV|access-date=July 17, 2016|archive-date=August 18, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160818132751/https://revolt.tv/stories/2016/05/16/spotlight-revolt-justina-valentine-ft-futuristic-0a7f1d7046|url-status=dead}}</ref> She is also known as a cast member on the improv comedy show ''[[Wild 'N Out]]'' (2016–present).

== Early life ==
Valentine was born in [[Passaic County, New Jersey]]. She is of Italian descent.<ref>{{Citation |title=Justina Valentine talks Growing up Italian in Jersey, New Movie & More |url=https://soundcloud.com/growingupitalian/justina-valentine |language=en |access-date=2022-03-22}}</ref> She grew up in a family of musicians and did theatre and dance as a child. Valentine always had a love for [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] and [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]]. In 2006, she started recording her own music.<ref name="stupiddope">{{cite web|title=Interview with Justina|url=http://www.stupiddope.com/2012/04/23/interview-w-justina|publisher=Jesse James|date=April 23, 2012|archive-url=https://archive.today/20161011050247/http://stupiddope.com/2012/04/23/interview-w-justina/|archive-date=October 11, 2016}}</ref><ref name="socialifestylemag">{{cite web|title=Justina|url=http://socialifestylemag.com/justina/|publisher=Olivia Rahim|access-date=September 12, 2016|date=May 24, 2014}}</ref>

== Career ==
Valentine released her first [[mixtape]] ''Route 80'' in 2012. The singles "Bubble Gum" and "Hip-hop Joan Jett" became popular hits on [[YouTube]]. Valentine released the [[Extended play|EP]] ''Valentine'' in 2013, debuting at number 38 on the iTunes R&B charts.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://issuu.com/wearejersey/docs/march2015|title=We Are Jersey: March 2015|access-date=September 8, 2016}} pages 29–38</ref> On July 8, 2014, the singer released her second mixtape, ''Red Velvet''. In 2016, she released her studio album, ''Scarlet Letter''. In 2017, Valentine released a third mixtape, ''Feminem,'' described as a homage to the rapper [[Eminem]].<ref>{{Citation |title=Feminem by Justina Valentine |date=2017-11-08 |url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/feminem/1495404224 |language=en-US |access-date=2022-03-22}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Feminem by Justina Valentine |url=https://genius.com/albums/Justina-valentine/Feminem |access-date=2022-03-22 |website=Genius |language=en}}</ref> She released the album ''Favorite Vibe'' in 2019, which debuted in the top 30 hip hop charts and gained over a million streams in its first two weeks.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Jones |first=Jiggy |date=2020-12-03 |title=The Source {{!}}Wild 'N Out's Justina Valentine Drops Latest Project, 'INFRARED' |url=https://thesource.com/2020/12/03/wild-n-outs-justina-valentine-drop-latest-project-infrared/ |access-date=2022-03-22 |website=The Source |language=en-US}}</ref> ''Favorite Vibe'' was shortly followed by the album ''Infrared'' in 2020, which debuted in the top 5 on the iTunes charts.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Justina Valentine – Indie Amplify |url=https://www.indieamplify.com/services/justina-valentine-vocal-feature/ |access-date=2022-03-22 |language=en-US}}</ref> ''Infrared'' is described as "an infusion of classic hip hop and R&B, with a pop sensibility."<ref>{{Cite web |title=Justina Valentine – "Infrared" (Album) – DJIceberg.com |url=https://djiceberg.com/justina-valentine-infrared-album/ |access-date=2022-03-22 |language=en-US}}</ref>

In 2016, Valentine was added to the cast of season 8 of ''[[Wild 'N Out]]'' on [[MTV]] (later [[VH1]]) and has been a member of the cast for every successive season since.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://comedyhype.com/mtv-to-bring-back-wild-n-for-season-8-on-august-4th-with-iggy-azalea-erykah-badu-omarion-and-more-watch-preview/|title=MTV To Bring Back Wild N' Out For Season 8 On August 4th With Iggy Azalea, Erykah Badu, Omarion And More; Watch Preview – ComedyHype.Com {{!}} Comedy ReFueled|date=June 9, 2016|access-date=September 8, 2016|archive-date=November 6, 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181106054938/https://comedyhype.com/mtv-to-bring-back-wild-n-for-season-8-on-august-4th-with-iggy-azalea-erykah-badu-omarion-and-more-watch-preview/|url-status=dead}}</ref> As of season 17, she is the longest running female cast member to appear on the show.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-02-28 |title=Justina Valentine Talks Wild N' Out Season 17 |url=https://www.koin.com/everydaynorthwest/wild-n-out/ |access-date=2022-03-22 |website=KOIN.com |language=en-US}}</ref>

Valentine competed on season 1 of the reality series special for [[MTV]]'s ''[[The Challenge (TV series)|The Challenge]]'' titled ''[[The Challenge: Champs vs. Stars (season 2)|Champs vs. Stars]], which premiered on November 21, 2017''.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://people.com/tv/the-challenge-champs-vs-stars-cast-reveal/ |title=Cast Revealed! Josh Murray, Shawn Johnson and More Stars to Compete in MTV's The Challenge: Champs vs. Stars |last=Russian |first=Ale |date=October 11, 2017 |website=[[People (magazine)|People]]  |access-date=October 11, 2017}}</ref> Valentine made it to the finale, finishing as a runner-up alongside fellow "Stars" [[Ultimate Fighting Championship|UFC]] fighter [[Michelle Waterson]] and ''[[The Bachelorette (American season 10)|The Bachelorette Season 10]]'' winner Josh Murray. In 2019, Valentine hosted the two-part reunion of ''[[The Challenge: War of the Worlds 2]]''.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Longeretta |first1=Emily |title=Wes Bergmann Talks Returning to MTV's 'The Challenge,' Playing Cupid and Johnny Bananas: 'He's This Season's Layup' |url=https://www.usmagazine.com/entertainment/news/the-challenges-wes-says-johnny-bananas-is-this-seasons-layup/ |website=US Weekly |date=February 6, 2019 |access-date=5 February 2021}}</ref>

In November 2018, Valentine was selected to co-host a relaunched version of the MTV dating game show ''[[Singled Out]]'' alongside rapper and fellow ''Wild 'N Out'' cast member [[Conceited (rapper)|Conceited]] that aired exclusively on the network's YouTube channel.

In August 2019, Valentine and fellow ''Wild 'N Out'' cast member Chico Bean stated that [[Sebastian Maniscalco]] was Valentine's "second freaking cousin".<ref>{{Cite web |title=Wild 'N Out Awards Road To VMAs Hosted by Justina Valentine & Chico Bean |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=URbrC_0yKM4 |access-date=2019-08-18 |website=YouTube |language=en}}</ref>

In 2021, Valentine began appearing weekly alongside [[Nick Cannon]] on his syndicated daytime talk show, ''Nick Cannon'', during "The Rap Up" and other segments.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Justina Valentine Weekly Rap Up |url=https://www.nickcannonshow.com/video/justina-valentine-weekly-rap-up/ |access-date=2022-03-22 |website=Nick Cannon |language=en}}</ref>

In February 2022, she became the third host of the [[iHeartRadio]] and MTV podcast series ''MTV's Women of Wild 'N Out,'' alongside co-hosts [[B. Simone]] and Pretty Vee.

Valentine has also gained prominence as a social media personality on [[TikTok]] where she actively posts content. As of September 2022, her account (@justinavalentine) has amassed over 10 million followers and over 200 million likes.<ref>{{Cite web |title=TikTok |url=https://www.tiktok.com/@justinavalentine |access-date=2022-09-09 |website=www.tiktok.com}}</ref>
