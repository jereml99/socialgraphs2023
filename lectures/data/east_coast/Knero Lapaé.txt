'''Daniel Boimah Kenney Knero Lapaé Edwards II''' (born May 19, 1985), better known as '''Knero Lapaé''' or simply '''Knero''' (stylized as '''kNERO'''; pronounced ''knee ro'' ''la pay''), is a Liberian rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, model, and author.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.frontpageafricaonline.com/old/lifestyle/entertainment/3452-kneros-rap-liberian-born-rapper-performer-on-cusps-of-mainstream-appeal.html |title=kNERO's Rap: Liberian-Born Rapper, Performer on Cusps of Mainstream Appeal :: FrontPageAfricaonline.com - All Things Africa 24/7 |publisher=FrontPageAfricaonline.com |date=2012-06-21 |access-date=2014-04-17 |archive-date=2014-04-18 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140418220518/http://www.frontpageafricaonline.com/old/lifestyle/entertainment/3452-kneros-rap-liberian-born-rapper-performer-on-cusps-of-mainstream-appeal.html |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://thatsenuff.com/2014/10/01/liberian-artist-knero-set-to-release-a-memoir-homeless-with-a-record-deal-the-moments-of-luxury/ |title=kNERO "Liberian Artist kNERO Set To Release A Memoir (Homeless With A Record Deal: The Moments Of Luxury) |publisher=Thatsenuff |date=2014-10-01 |access-date=2014-04-17 |archive-date=2014-10-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141002210038/http://thatsenuff.com/2014/10/01/liberian-artist-knero-set-to-release-a-memoir-homeless-with-a-record-deal-the-moments-of-luxury/ |url-status=live }}</ref> His music is a mixture of [[Hip hop music|Hip hop]] and [[Afrobeats]].

==Personal life and family members==
kNERO was born in [[Monrovia]], [[Liberia]] and raised in [[Brooklyn]], New York. His father is [[Gola people|Gola]] and his mother is from the [[Kpelle people|Kpelle]] tribe. He is one of four children of Sara Léla and Rev. D. Boimah Lapaé Edwards, who was the youngest Liberian to managed an American corporation in Liberia. His father is currently the President of the non-profit organization, The Bomi County Association.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theliberianjournal.com/index.php?st=news&sbst=details&rid=1221|title=National Bomi Association Concludes Convention in Minnesota|work=theliberianjournal.com|access-date=2013-07-30|archive-date=2013-08-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130828214317/http://www.theliberianjournal.com/index.php?st=news&sbst=details&rid=1221|url-status=live}}</ref> kNERO was brought up in a predominant political background. He is the nephew of Liberia's 24th president [[Ellen Johnson Sirleaf]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://g-momo-johnson.memory-of.com/About.aspx|title=G. Momo Johnson (1957 - 2010)|author=Lana M|work=memory-of.com|access-date=2012-11-28|archive-date=2012-12-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121201033019/http://g-momo-johnson.memory-of.com/About.aspx|url-status=live}}</ref> He has an uncle who was a judge of the Magistrate Court, a District Commissioner, and County Development Coordinator for the Ministry of Internal Affairs. He is also the first cousin of Richard B. Devine, a former Senator of Liberia.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tlcafrica.com/death_johnson_momo.htm|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100125211230/http://www.tlcafrica.com/death_johnson_momo.htm|url-status=usurped|archive-date=January 25, 2010|title=TLC Africa|work=tlcafrica.com}}</ref><ref>http://allafrica.com/stories/200603060844.html {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121130232040/http://allafrica.com/stories/200603060844.html |date=2012-11-30 }} AllAfrica.com 6 MARCH 2006 http://allafrica.com/stories/200603060844.html {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121130232040/http://allafrica.com/stories/200603060844.html |date=2012-11-30 }}</ref> His family was fortunate enough to escape the [[First Liberian Civil War|Liberian Civil War]] as a result of his father's achievements, but still suffers the loss of family and friends.

==Music career and performances==
kNERO released "Not Drunk Enuff" featuring Norwegian singer Adele Erichsen, a former contestant on ''[[The Voice – Norges beste stemme]]''. The single was released under CCAP records. Due to his political ties, he celebrated the [[Liberian Declaration of Independence]] by performing his single "A.F.R.I.C.A" at the Liberian Embassy in Washington, D.C.<ref>http://allafrica.com/stories/201107200947.html {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121130223901/http://allafrica.com/stories/201107200947.html |date=2012-11-30 }} allafrica.com http://allafrica.com/stories/201107200947.html {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121130223901/http://allafrica.com/stories/201107200947.html |date=2012-11-30 }}</ref> He performed at the Hip Hop of Pioneers event held at the [[Newark Symphony Hall]]; the event featured additional performances from [[MC Lyte]], [[Kool and the Gang]], [[Dana Dane]] and [[Slick Rick]].<ref>http://grandgood.com/2006/11/29/pioneers-of-hip-hop-show-in-nj-122-8pm/ {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121201013809/http://grandgood.com/2006/11/29/pioneers-of-hip-hop-show-in-nj-122-8pm/ |date=2012-12-01 }} Hip Hop of Pioneers http://grandgood.com/2006/11/29/pioneers-of-hip-hop-show-in-nj-122-8pm/ {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121201013809/http://grandgood.com/2006/11/29/pioneers-of-hip-hop-show-in-nj-122-8pm/ |date=2012-12-01 }}</ref>

Between 2006 and 2007, he performed at [[Fox Theatre (Atlanta)|The Fox Theatre]] in Atlanta for [[Dr. Malachi Z. York]]'s 11th Annual [[Nuwaupu]] Ball; his name was announced alongside [[Erykah Badu]], [[Jimmy Cliff]] and [[Musiq Soulchild]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ENPl4jrzLrw|title=kNERO'S PERFORMANCE|date=23 April 2007|work=YouTube|access-date=27 November 2016|archive-date=12 April 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160412004521/https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ENPl4jrzLrw|url-status=live}}</ref> kNERO released his single "My Thang" featuring [[Jamal Woolard|Jamal "Gravy" Woolard]] and former [[Roc-A-Fella]] artist [[Amil (rapper)|Amil]], which appeared on several urban blog sites.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://getwititmagazine.com/2012/12/24/new-music-knero-ft-gravy_ny-x-amil-my-thang/|title=New Music: kNERO ft Gravy X Amil -"My Thang"|work=Get Wit It Magazine - The Entertainment Site Of The New America|access-date=2012-12-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121230182513/http://getwititmagazine.com/2012/12/24/new-music-knero-ft-gravy_ny-x-amil-my-thang/|archive-date=2012-12-30|url-status=dead}}</ref>

In 2014, kNERO closed a deal to release his next single "King" under Universal Music Group.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://thatsenuff.com/index.php/2014/04/knero-ft-rikroot-king-audio/|title=kNERO ft. Rikroot 'King' [Audio]|work=THATSENUFF.COM|access-date=2014-04-28|archive-date=2014-04-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140429050017/http://thatsenuff.com/index.php/2014/04/knero-ft-rikroot-king-audio/|url-status=live}}</ref>

While growing up, kNERO listened to artists such as [[Buckshot (rapper)|Buck Shot]], [[Smif-N-Wessun|Smif N Wessun]], [[Jay Z]], [[The Notorious B.I.G.]], [[Nas]], [[Phil Collins]], [[Sade Adu]], [[Fela Kuti]], [[Prodigy (rapper)|Prodigy]], [[Big Daddy Kane]], [[Biz Markie]], [[Rakim]], [[Raekwon]], [[Ghostface Killah]], [[KRS-One]], [[50 Cent]], [[Kool G Rap]], [[Tracy Chapman]] and [[Bob Marley]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.frontpageafricaonline.com/old/lifestyle/entertainment/3452-kneros-rap-liberian-born-rapper-performer-on-cusps-of-mainstream-appeal.html|title=Sex Workers in Liberia Share Experience With FrontPageAfrica Newspaper|work=FrontPageAfrica|access-date=2018-05-04|language=en|archive-date=2014-04-18|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140418220518/http://www.frontpageafricaonline.com/old/lifestyle/entertainment/3452-kneros-rap-liberian-born-rapper-performer-on-cusps-of-mainstream-appeal.html|url-status=live}}</ref>

== Other works ==

=== Barber career ===
In 1995, kNERO fell in love with the art of [[barbering]]. To avoid street violence and other criminal activities, he learned to master his craft over the years. He then travels from state to state gaining connections in the hair industry, which helped his music career. His limitless experiences, becoming rapper [[Raekwon]] from the [[Wu-Tang Clan]] personal barber. Gaining clients as Comedian Kyle Grooms, Fashion Designer Toure Designs, Humanitarian/Motivational speaker George Green, Celebrity Hairstylist Keith Campbell, Middleweight Boxer [[Peter Quillin]], and more.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://ireplib.com/blog/entertainment/knero-s-barbering-and-music |title=KNERO's Barbering and Music |access-date=2015-03-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402190038/http://ireplib.com/blog/entertainment/knero-s-barbering-and-music |archive-date=2015-04-02 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

=== Modeling and clothing ===
In 2012, kNERO was a model for photos of The Toure Designs' fall season collection. Later that year, the model in The Toure Designs' Target First Saturdays "Carnival Inspired Fashion" themed at the [[Brooklyn Museum]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://thesnobette.com/2014/08/runway-butch-diva-at-brooklyn-museum/|title=Runway: Butch Diva At Brooklyn Museum|author=The Snobette|work=The Snobette|date=3 August 2014|access-date=7 March 2015|archive-date=2 April 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402163131/http://thesnobette.com/2014/08/runway-butch-diva-at-brooklyn-museum/|url-status=live}}</ref> He continued to walk the runway alongside actress/singer [[Demetria McKinney|Demetria Mckinney]], modeling for Toure Designs' for the 2015 Mercedes Benz Fashion week presented by ICU Network hosted by SB Stunts.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://getwititmagazine.com/2015/02/19/liberian-rapper-knero-seen-at-toure-designs-2015-fashion-week/ |title=Liberian Rapper kNERO Seen at Toure Designs 2015 Fashion Week |access-date=2015-03-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150405124629/http://getwititmagazine.com/2015/02/19/liberian-rapper-knero-seen-at-toure-designs-2015-fashion-week/ |archive-date=2015-04-05 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

=== Acting ===
kNERO Lapaé has made an appearance in the 2013 film ''Living With No Regrets'', with [[Clifton Powell]], [[Marc John Jefferies]], rapper [[AZ (rapper)|AZ]], [[Charlie Baltimore]], [[Treach]] and more.<ref>[https://www.imdb.com/title/tt3498658/?ref_=nv_sr_1 " Living with No Regrets (2013)"] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230117015846/https://www.imdb.com/title/tt3498658/?ref_=nv_sr_1 |date=2023-01-17 }}, IMDb.</ref>

In 2014, kNERO landed a role on ''Red Dot''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://whosnext.hot97.com/artist/2759|title=kNERO|work=hot97.com|access-date=2015-03-08|archive-date=2015-04-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402161917/http://whosnext.hot97.com/artist/2759|url-status=live}}</ref> a series about a group of trained killers, which expands into stories of many other individuals that they come into contact with. He first appeared in the ''Red Dot'' episode "The Walking Dead", as Fritzoy, a goon of a hit-man leader named Cassius who's out for revenge after finding out his friend Eddie was murdered.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://getwititmagazine.com/2014/03/23/red-dot-episode-2-walking-dead-director-nimi-hendrix/ |title=Red Dot: Episode 2 - "The Walking Dead" Director By Nimi Hendrix |work=IMDB |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150407200752/https://www.imdb.com/title/tt3463550/?ref_=nm_knf_t1 |archive-date=2015-04-07 }}</ref>
