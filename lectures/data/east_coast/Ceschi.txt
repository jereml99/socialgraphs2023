'''Julio Francisco Ramos''' (born July 24, 1981), better known as '''Ceschi Ramos''', or simply as '''Ceschi''' ({{IPAc-en|'|tS|3|s|k|i}} {{Respell|CHESS|kee}}),<ref name=newhavenregister/><ref name=villagevoice>{{cite web|url=http://blogs.villagevoice.com/music/2014/01/ceschi_prison_saga.php|title=The Prison Saga of Ceschi|work=[[The Village Voice]]|first=Chaz|last=Kangas|date=January 2, 2014|accessdate=March 25, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://exclaim.ca/Music/article/Ceschi-One_Man_Band_Broke_Up|title=Ceschi – The One Man Band Broke Up|work=[[Exclaim!]]|first=Thomas|last=Quinlan|date=October 18, 2010|accessdate=March 25, 2015}}</ref> is an American hip hop musician based in [[New Haven, Connecticut]].<ref name=courant2014>{{cite web|url=http://articles.courant.com/2014-03-24/entertainment/hc-ctn-ceschi-ramos-in-new-haven-0327-20140324_1_march-29-cafe-nine-18-months|title=Hip Hop's Ceschi Ramos Celebrates Freedom With Hometown Appearance|work=[[Hartford Courant]]|first=Michael|last=Hamad|date=March 24, 2014|accessdate=March 25, 2015}}</ref> He is a co-founder of [[Fake Four Inc.]]<ref name=villagevoice/> He won the Connecticut Music Awards for Best Hip Hop in 2014.<ref name=ctnews2014>{{cite web|url=http://blog.ctnews.com/ctgirlabouttown/2014/06/12/2014-connecticut-music-awards-grand-band-slam/|title=2014 Connecticut Music Awards + Grand Band Slam|work=[[Connecticut Post]]|first=Julie Bess|last=Hymans|date=June 12, 2014|accessdate=March 25, 2015}}</ref>

==Life and career==
Ceschi released his first solo album, ''Fake Flowers'', in 2004.<ref name=courant2015>{{cite web|url=http://www.courant.com/entertainment/music/hc-ceschi-ramos-celebrates-his-new-album-with-a-show-at-toads-place-0312-20150311-story.html|title=New Haven's Ceschi Ramos Celebrates New Release At Toad's|work=[[Hartford Courant]]|first=Michael|last=Hamad|date=March 11, 2015|accessdate=March 25, 2015}}</ref> ''They Hate Francisco False'' followed in 2006.<ref name=courant2015/>

In 2008, Ceschi and his brother David Ramos founded the record label [[Fake Four Inc.]]<ref name=villagevoice/>

In 2010, Ceschi was arrested<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nhregister.com/news/article/UPDATE-Christmas-presents-full-of-marijuana-11618936.php|title=UPDATE: Christmas 'presents' full of marijuana: Police seize 100 pounds of pot in New Haven, all wrapped up with festive bows (video, photos of 'presents')|last=Kaempffer|first=William|last2=Staff|first2=Register|date=December 8, 2010|website=New Haven Register|access-date=January 3, 2020}}</ref> and pleaded guilty to a 100-pound marijuana shipment that he accepted at the home of his grandfather in New Haven.

Ceschi released ''[[The One Man Band Broke Up]]'', a collaborative album with German producer DJ Scientist, in July 2010.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/the-one-man-band-broke-up-mw0001996593|title=Ceschi – The One Man Band Broke Up|work=[[AllMusic]]|first=Rick|last=Anderson|accessdate=March 25, 2015}}</ref> It was described by [[Tiny Mix Tapes]] as "music for the saddest party in recorded human history."<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tinymixtapes.com/music-review/ceschi-ramos-one-man-band-broke|title=Ceschi Ramos – The One Man Band Broke Up|work=[[Tiny Mix Tapes]]|first=Joe|last=Hemmerling|accessdate=March 25, 2015}}</ref>

In 2014, Ceschi won the Connecticut Music Awards for Best Hip Hop.<ref name=ctnews2014/> He released ''Broken Bone Ballads'', a collaborative album with Canadian producer [[Factor Chandelier]], in April 2015.<ref name=browardpalmbeach>{{cite web|url=http://www.browardpalmbeach.com/music/ceschis-time-in-prison-helped-shape-broken-bone-ballads-6412643|title=Ceschi's Time in Prison Helped Shape Broken Bone Ballads|work=[[New Times Broward-Palm Beach]]|first=Kat|last=Bein|date=February 2, 2015|accessdate=March 25, 2015}}</ref>  In 2019, Jahan Nostra and Ceschi won Best Music Video at the [[Hip Hop Film Festival]] for their video “El Chapo.”<ref>{{Cite web|title=Harlem’s Hip Hop Film Festival Provides A Safe Space For Black Creatives|url=https://www.bet.com/celebrities/news/2019/08/09/hip-hop-film-festival-recap.html?cid=facebook|access-date=2021-09-18|website=BET.com}}</ref><ref>{{Citation|title=Jahan Nostra: El Chapo (feat. Ceschi) (Video 2019) - IMDb|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt10135090/awards/|language=en|access-date=2021-09-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-04-01|title=#DXCLUSIVE: Jahan Nostra & Ceschi Honor César Chávez Day With "El Chapo" Video|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.50879/title.dxclusive-jahan-nostra-ceschi-honor-cesar-chavez-day-with-el-chapo-video|access-date=2021-09-18|website=HipHopDX}}</ref>
