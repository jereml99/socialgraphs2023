== Early years ==
Yu was born and raised in Brooklyn to Chinese immigrants, from [[Hong Kong]].<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite video|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ti6HBhOd8Xo&feature=youtu.be|title=China Mac Explains How to Smuggle Sneakers Into Prison|date=June 27, 2019|last=|first=|type=Video|language=en-US|publisher=Sole Collector|series=Full Size Run}}</ref> He moved into a [[group home]] at the age of 8.<ref name=":2">{{Cite web|title=Exclusive Interview with China Mac|url=https://musicexistence.com/blog/2014/11/17/exclusive-interview-with-china-mac/|last=Vicino|first=Stephen|date=2014-11-17|website=Music Existence|language=en-US|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141230155459/http://musicexistence.com:80/blog/2014/11/17/exclusive-interview-with-china-mac/ |archive-date=2014-12-30 |access-date=2020-02-18}}</ref> Yu joined the [[Ghost Shadows]] gang when he was 12.<ref name=":0" /> In his teenage years, he would partake in [[freestyle rap]] battles with other kids at the [[juvenile detention]] center.<ref name=":0" />

== Career ==

=== 2000–2013: Incarceration ===
At the age of 18, Mac was sentenced to three years in prison for gang related crimes in 2000.<ref name=":0" />

On November 9, 2003, Mac was involved in an altercation with [[MC Jin]] at a bar in [[Chinatown, Manhattan]], where he shot Jin's acquaintance, rapper Christopher "LS" Louie, in the back.<ref name=":3">{{Cite web|title=China Mac sits down for a talk with MC Jin 15 years after NYC shooting|url=https://sr-mag.com/2019/06/china-mac-sits-down-for-a-talk-with-mc-jin-15-years-after-nyc-shooting/|last=Druzkowski|first=Piotr|date=2019-06-25|website=SR-MAG.COM|language=en-US|access-date=2020-02-18}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Jin & China Mac Reconcile 15 Years After China Mac Shot Jin's Friend|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.51841/title.jin-china-mac-reconcile-15-years-after-china-mac-shot-jins-friend|last=Riley|first=Wallace|date=2019-06-22|website=HipHopDX|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190623215257/https://hiphopdx.com/news/id.51841/title.jin-china-mac-reconcile-15-years-after-china-mac-shot-jins-friend |archive-date=2019-06-23 |access-date=2020-02-18}}</ref> Mac later went [[Fugitive|on the run]] for over a year and was apprehended in [[Seattle]], Washington when he tried to leave the country with a fake passport.<ref name=":0" /> In prison, he was nicknamed "China Mac" by the Mac Ballers gang. He was released on parole in November 2013 and founded the Red Money Records record label and pet store with the money he saved up while in prison.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":3" /><ref name=":2" />

=== 2014–present: Music production & activism ===
Mac returned to prison for an accused parole violation and was later released in 2017.<ref name=":3" /> Since then, he has uploaded video content, including the food show ''Mac Eats'', onto his YouTube channel, China Mac TV.<ref name=":1" />

Mac released his album ''MITM'' in 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|title=M.I.T.M, by CHINA MAC|url=https://chinamacmusic.bandcamp.com/album/m-i-t-m|website=CHINA MAC|access-date=2020-02-20}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=EXCLUSIVE: China Mac on Chinese Mafia, Shooting Jin's Friend, Prison Time (Full Interview)|url=https://www.vladtv.com/article/236596/china-mac-on-chinese-mafia-shooting-jins-friend-prison-time-full|last=Santi|first=Christina|date=November 21, 2017|website=www.vladtv.com|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171121225145/http://www.vladtv.com/article/236596/china-mac-on-chinese-mafia-shooting-jins-friend-prison-time-full |archive-date=2017-11-21 |access-date=2020-02-20}}</ref>

In 2018, Mac was a prominent critic of [[Lil Pump]]'s single "[[Butterfly Doors]]", which used the pejorative [[ching chong]] slur.<ref name=":1" /><ref>{{Cite web|title=China Mac Puts Lil Pump on Instagram Blast for Mocking Asians in New Video|url=https://nextshark.com/china-mac-lil-pump-beef/|last=Casino|first=Khier|date=2018-12-18|website=NextShark|language=en-US|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181218032718/https://nextshark.com/china-mac-lil-pump-beef/ |archive-date=2018-12-18 |access-date=2020-02-18}}</ref>

In 2019, he released the dual EP, ''Yin'' and ''Yang.''<ref>{{Cite web|title=EXCLUSIVE: China Mac: I was in a Maximum Security Prison for 10 Years, I Suppressed a Lot|url=https://www.vladtv.com/article/257443/china-mac-i-was-in-a-maximum-security-prison-for-10-years-i-suppressed-a|last=Todd|first=Jessica|date=August 28, 2019|website=www.vladtv.com|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190829180202/https://www.vladtv.com/article/257443/china-mac-i-was-in-a-maximum-security-prison-for-10-years-i-suppressed-a |archive-date=2019-08-29 |access-date=2020-02-20}}</ref> That same year, Mac released a Chinese/Spanish record with [[Tali Goya]].<ref name=":1" />
