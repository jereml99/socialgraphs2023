== Life and career ==
Martins was born in [[Providence, Rhode Island]].<ref>{{cite web |first1=Gossamer |last1=Magazine |title=Theo Martins - Gossamer |url=https://www.gossamer.co/articles/theo-martins |website=Gossamer |publisher=Gossamer Magazine |access-date=15 December 2021}}</ref> Martins credits his parents for the musical and artistic exposure gained throughout his upbringing. He noted that his mother found [[Casting (performing arts)|casting calls]] searching local newspapers while his father was a trumpeter and avid music collector.<ref name="gossamer.co"/>
