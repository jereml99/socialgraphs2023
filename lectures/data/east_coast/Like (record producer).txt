'''Gabriel Stevenson''', professionally known as '''Like''', is an American record producer, rapper, DJ and songwriter. A three time [[Grammy Nominees|Grammy nominee]], he began working with the [[Pac Div|Pacific Division]], a [[hip hop]] trio, better known as Pac Div in 2006.

In 2012, he produced [[Kendrick Lamar]]'s "Sing About Me" on ''[[Good Kid, M.A.A.D City]]''. He received two Grammy Award nominations at the [[56th Grammy Awards]]: [[Grammy Album of the Year|Album of the Year]] and [[Best Rap Album]]. In 2016, he produced [[Anderson Paak|Anderson .Paak]]'s "Room in Here" featuring [[The Game (rapper)|The Game]] and [[Sonyae Elise]] on the ''[[Malibu (album)|Malibu]]'' album.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Garrett|first1=Ural|title=between-producing-for-kendrick-lamar-anderson-paak-like-of-pac-div-is-now-poised-to-be-a-solo-threat|url=http://hiphopdx.com/news/id.40662/title.between-producing-for-kendrick-lamar-anderson-paak-like-of-pac-div-is-now-poised-to-be-a-solo-threat|website=HipHopDX|date=30 September 2016}}</ref> The album received a Grammy nomination for [[Best Urban Contemporary Album]] at the [[59th Annual Grammy Awards]].<ref>{{cite magazine|last1=Unterberger|first1=Andrew|title="Grammy 2017: The Complete List of Nominees".|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/7597556/grammys-nominees-complete-list-2017.com|magazine=Billboard}}</ref> Like has production credit with artists such as [[Mac Miller]],<ref>{{cite web|last1=Rhymestyle|first1=Rhymestyle|title=Mac Miller ft. Talib Kweli - Family First|url=http://djbooth.net/tracks/review/mac-miller-family-first|website=DJBooth}}</ref> [[Ab-Soul]], [[Joey Badass]], Nick Grant, [[Asher Roth]], Ill Camille and [[Currensy]].

Like has released music as a solo artist.<ref>{{cite web|last1=Ducker|first1=Eric|title=WEED, SHROOMS, ETHIOPIA, & LIKE FROM PAC DIV'S NEW SOLO JOURNEY|url=http://frank151.com/weed-shrooms-ethiopia-like-from-pac-divs-new-solo-journey/?platform=hootsuite|website=frank151.com}} {{dead link|date=July 2017}}</ref> He released instrumental albums, ''Zesty'' and ''Lightwork'' in 2014 and ''Emeralds''<ref>{{cite web|title=Emeralds by Like on Apple Music|url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/emeralds/id1086810689|website=itunes.com|date=25 March 2016}}</ref> in 2016. On September 30, 2016, he released his first solo rap album, ''Songs Made While High'',<ref>{{cite web|last1=Hahn|first1=Bryan|title=PREMIERE: LIKE OF PAC DIV 'SONGS MADE WHILE HIGH' LP AND "MISS ME WITH THAT" VIDEO|url=https://massappeal.com/premiere-like-of-pac-div-songs-made-while-high-lp-and-miss-me-with-that-video/|website=Mass Appeal}}</ref> which features Anderson .Paak, Buddy, and [[Kali Uchis]].

In 2017, Pac Div regrouped and are scheduled to release an album at the end of the year.
