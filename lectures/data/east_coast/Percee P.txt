'''''Percee P''''' is an American [[rapper]] from the [[Bronx, New York]]. He first gained recognition in the hip-hop community in the early 1990s for his fast-paced, intricate rhymes and unique flow. He is known for his collaborations with other notable hip-hop artists, including [[Lord Finesse]] and [[Madlib]]. '''''Percee P''''' has released several albums throughout his career, including [[Perseverance (Percee P album)|Perseverance]] in 2007. He continues to be a respected figure in the underground hip-hop scene.

==Career==
In 2007, Percee P released his debut studio album, ''[[Perseverance (Percee P album)|Perseverance]]'', on [[Stones Throw Records]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/10560-perseverance/|title=Percee P: Perseverance|work=[[Pitchfork (website)|Pitchfork]]|first=Nate|last=Patrin|date=August 28, 2007|access-date=July 16, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180708020147/https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/10560-perseverance/|archive-date=July 8, 2018|url-status=live}}</ref> Entirely produced by [[Madlib]],<ref>{{cite web|url=https://exclaim.ca/music/article/percee_p-perseverance|title=Percee P - Perseverance|work=[[Exclaim!]]|first=Neil|last=Acharya|date=November 26, 2007|access-date=July 16, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180707201429/https://exclaim.ca/music/article/percee_p-perseverance|archive-date=July 7, 2018|url-status=live}}</ref> it featured guest appearances from [[Aesop Rock]], [[Chali 2na]], [[Diamond D]], [[Guilty Simpson]], and [[Prince Po]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.xlr8r.com/reviews/percee-p/perseverance|title=Percee P Perseverance|work=[[XLR8R]]|date=November 22, 2007|first=Rico|last=Washington|access-date=July 16, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140814065642/http://www.xlr8r.com/reviews/percee-p/perseverance|archive-date=August 14, 2014|url-status=live}}</ref>

He has also collaborated with a number of musicians, including [[Lord Finesse]], [[Kool Keith]], [[Jurassic 5]], and [[Jedi Mind Tricks]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.theage.com.au/news/music/i-persevere-percee-p/2008/01/03/1198949964394.html?page=fullpage|title=I persevere: Percee P|work=[[The Age]]|first=Dan|last=Rule|date=January 1, 2008|access-date=July 16, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160401204930/http://www.theage.com.au/news/music/i-persevere-percee-p/2008/01/03/1198949964394.html?page=fullpage|archive-date=April 1, 2016|url-status=live}}</ref>
