'''Sidney Royel Selby III''' (born May 3, 1997), known professionally as '''Desiigner''', is an American rapper, singer, and convicted sex offender. He is best known for his December 2015 debut single "[[Panda (Desiigner song)|Panda]]", which topped the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]]. Shortly after the song gained viral status online, he signed to [[Kanye West]]'s [[GOOD Music]], an imprint of [[Def Jam Recordings]] until becoming an independent artist in 2019.

In July 2016, he released the follow-up single "[[Tiimmy Turner]]", which peaked at number 34 on the ''Billboard'' Hot 100. Despite this, Desiigner is frequently described as a [[one-hit wonder]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://junkee.com/one-hit-wonders-decade/231537|title=Gone But Not Forgotten: The Biggest One-Hit Wonders Of The Decade|website=Junkee|last=Jolly|first=Nathan|access-date=July 5, 2020|date=November 22, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.businessinsider.com/top-selling-music-singles-from-one-hit-wonders-all-time-2019-6|title=The 11 top-selling music singles from one-hit wonders of all time, from Gotye to the 'Macarena'|website=Business Insider|last=McAlone|first=Nathan|access-date=July 5, 2020|date=June 26, 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.insider.com/most-popular-one-hit-wonders-history-2019-5#2016-panda-by-desiigner-62|title=The most popular one-hit wonder every year since 1955|website=Insider|last=Shaw|first=Gabbi|access-date=July 5, 2020|date=September 26, 2019}}</ref>

== Early life ==
Desiigner was born on May 3, 1997,<ref>{{cite magazine | url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/7341870/desiigner-panda-billboard-hot-100-number-1 | title=Desiigner's 'Panda' Claws to No. 1 on Hot 100, Bringing Americans Back to the Top | magazine=Billboard | date=April 25, 2016 | access-date=April 26, 2016 | last=Trust | first=Gary | quote=Desiigner is 18 years and 11 months old; he'll turn 19 on May 3.}}</ref> in [[Brooklyn]], New York City.<ref>{{cite web| url=http://pigeonsandplanes.com/2016/02/desiigner-panda/ |title=The New Wave: Desiigner – "Panda"|date=February 9, 2016|work=Pigeons & Planes}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2016/02/the-break-presents-desiigner/ |title=The Break Presents: Desiigner |author=emmanuel |date=February 19, 2016 |work=XXL Mag}}</ref> He is of [[Afro-Barbadian]] and [[African American]] descent,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://wwd.com/eye/people/panda-desiigner-rapper-interview-10431787/|title=Meet Desiigner: The Elusive and Kanye West-Mentored Rapper|first=Aria|last=Hughes|date=May 17, 2016|access-date=May 13, 2017}}</ref> and is the grandson of blues musician [[Guitar Crusher|Sidney "Guitar Crusher" Selby]]. He grew up in the [[Louis Armstrong]] housing projects in the [[Bedford–Stuyvesant, Brooklyn|Bedford–Stuyvesant]] neighborhood.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/magazine-feature/7400285/desiigner-on-panda-difficult-past-kanye-west-advice|title=Desiigner on His Difficult Past and How His No. 1 Hit 'Panda' Gave Him a Way Out|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=June 9, 2016}}</ref> He began practicing vocals at school choir and church. At the age of 14, he embarked on his musical career.<ref>{{cite web |url= http://www.hotnewhiphop.com/everything-you-need-to-know-about-desiigner-news.20299.html?gallery=1 |title=Everything You Need To Know About Desiigner |work=HotNewHipHop |date=February 22, 2016 |access-date=April 26, 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9YXCsqOFi20|title=Desiigner Freestyle and talks Panda & Kanye|work=KISS FM UK|date=May 3, 2016|access-date=May 24, 2016}}</ref>

== Career ==
<!--===2014–present: Beginnings and breakthrough===-->
Desiigner embarked on his music career under his original [[nickname]] Dezolo,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.com/artists/desiigner/|title=Desiigner – Music Videos, News, Photos, Tour Dates – MTV|website=[[MTV]]|access-date=July 1, 2016}}</ref> before he began releasing his work under the alias of Designer Royel, especially with Royel (his middle name). His sister convinced him to drop the "Royel" part and then keep the "Designer" part as his new [[stage name]], doubling the "I"{{'s}}. On December 3, 2015, under his new [[pseudonym]], Desiigner released his debut track, called "Zombie Walk". The song was [[Record production|produced]] by The Shell God.<ref>{{cite AV media |url= https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Uz8MzFisPnM |title= Desiigner Ft. King Savage- "Zombie Walk" (Prod. By: @TheShellGod) |date=December 3, 2015|via=YouTube}}</ref> On December 15, 2015, Desiigner released his commercial debut [[single (music)|single]], titled "[[Panda (Desiigner song)|Panda]]" on SoundCloud.<ref>{{cite AV media|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lsJLLEwUYZM|title=Desiigner- Panda (OFFICIAL SONG) Prod. By: Menace|date=December 20, 2015|via=YouTube}}</ref> The song released on [[iTunes]] five days later, before being re-released as of February 2016.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/panda-single/id1088507971 | title=Panda – Single | work=iTunes Store (US) | date=February 26, 2016 | access-date=April 18, 2016}}</ref> On February 11, 2016, Desiigner signed a [[recording contract]] with [[Kanye West]]'s [[GOOD Music]] imprint, under the aegis of [[Def Jam Recordings]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hotnewhiphop.com/brooklyn-rapper-desiigner-signs-to-good-music-news.20126.html|title=Brooklyn Rapper Desiigner Signs To G.O.O.D. Music|author=Danny Schwartz|date=February 11, 2016|work=HotNewHipHop}}</ref> After signing a record deal, Desiigner subsequently appeared on two tracks — "[[Pt. 2]]" (which features an [[Interpolation (popular music)|interpolation]] of "Panda") and "Freestyle 4" — that were taken from Kanye West's seventh album ''[[The Life of Pablo]]''. == Legal issues ==
On September 8, 2016, Desiigner and three other people were arrested in [[New York City]], after a 911 caller claimed that he pulled a gun on them. Desiigner was later found in an [[SUV]] with four other people and the vehicle was searched. The police allegedly found [[oxycodone]] and guns, for which he and the other individuals received drug and weapon charges. They were all detained in custody.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/nyc-crime/desiigner-arrested-gun-drugs-menacing-midtown-driver-article-1.2784606|title=Desiigner charged in gun-pointing road rage incident in Midtown|website=[[New York Daily News]]|date=September 10, 2016 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.tmz.com/2016/09/09/desiigner-arrested-drugs-sell/|title=Desiigner -- Arrested for Drugs, Intent to Sell and Loaded Gun (UPDATED PHOTOS + VIDEO)}}</ref> On September 10, 2016, his felony [[gun charge]] was dropped by prosecutors.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.vibe.com/2016/09/desiigner-felony-gun-charge-dropped/ |title=Desiigner acquitted of gun charge |last=Augustin |first=Camille |date=September 10, 2016 |website=[[Vibe (magazine)|Vibe]] |access-date=September 10, 2016}}</ref> He was cleared of the charges on September 12, 2016, when it was found that the pills were [[anabolic steroids]] belonging to his driver.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.xxlmag.com/news/2016/09/desiigner-steroids-not-oxycontin/ |title=Drugs found in desiigner's car were steroids |last=Simmons |first=Ted |date=September 12, 2016 |website=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]] |access-date=September 12, 2016}}</ref>

In April 2023, Desiigner was charged with indecent exposure after allegedly being caught on a [[Delta Air Lines]] flight [[masturbation|masturbating]] in front of [[flight attendant|flight attendants]] while travelling from [[Tokyo]] to [[Minneapolis]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://metro.co.uk/2023/04/24/desiigner-charged-with-indecent-exposure-after-masturbating-on-plane-18667203/|title=Rapper Desiigner 'charged with indecent exposure after revealing himself and masturbating on a plane'|website=Metro|last=Bashforth|first=Emily|date=25 April 2023|access-date=25 April 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/desiigner-alleged-indecent-exposure-plane|title=Desiigner Charged With Indecent Exposure After Allegedly Masturbating on Plane|website=Complex|last=Price|first=Joe|date=25 April 2023|access-date=25 April 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/music/news/desiigner-indicent-exposure-plane-delta-b2326001.html|title=Desiigner charged with indecent exposure after plane incident|website=Independent|last=Murray|first=Tom|date=25 April 2023|access-date=25 April 2023}}</ref>

== Musical style ==
Desiigner's musical style is influenced by [[Southern hip hop]], namely [[Trap music (hip hop)|trap music]] which originated in [[Atlanta, Georgia]].<ref name="bbinterview" /> His [[rapping]] technique and vocals have been heavily compared to that of Atlanta-based rapper [[Future (rapper)|Future]]. When asked about the frequent comparisons, he told ''[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]'': "God gave him a blessing, but he gave me a blessing too. I ain't gonna doubt the man's music. He makes beautiful music too. Music is made every day. Big ups to him, big ups to Future. I actually like Future's music. I like his music, you feel me. I'm not a hater or a critic on him, you know, I do me. God bless him, God bless me."<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/music/2016/02/desiigner-talks-about-the-success-of-panda-and-the-comparisons-to-future|title=Interview: Desiigner Talks About the Success of "Panda" and Comparisons to Future
|work=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]|date=February 19, 2016|access-date=March 29, 2016}}</ref>
